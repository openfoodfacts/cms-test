Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (ara t-Termini tal-użu mill-ġdid)

Kulħadd (inklużi iżda mhux limitati għall-kontributuri u l-kreaturi ta' Open Food Facts) jista' għalhekk jiddistribwixxi mill-ġdid u/jew jerġa' juża d-dejta biex jibni websajts, servizzi, softwer, applikazzjonijiet mobbli, jew biex jikteb artikli u studji. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Pereżempju, il-Pjattaforma tagħna għall-Produtturi hija totalment bla ħlas, u aħna aktar kuntenti b'dan għax naħsbu li tista' tgħinhom itejbu l-prodotti tagħhom.
