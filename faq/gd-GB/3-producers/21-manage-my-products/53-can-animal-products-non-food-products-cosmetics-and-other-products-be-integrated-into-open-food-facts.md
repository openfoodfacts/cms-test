Tha sinn air pròiseactan sònraichte a chruthachadh airson cungaidhean-leigheis, biadh bheathaichean, a bharrachd air toraidhean eile: [Open Beauty Facts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Open Pet Food Facts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) agus [Open Products Facts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) fa leth.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
