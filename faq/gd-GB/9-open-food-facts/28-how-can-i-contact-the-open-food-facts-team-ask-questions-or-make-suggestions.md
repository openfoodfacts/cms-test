Faodaidh tu ceistean fhaighneachd no molaidhean a dhèanamh air an [fhòram](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/) no cuir fios thugainn tro phost-d: [contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Ma tha thu nad riochdaire, cuir post-d thugainn aig producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Ma tha thu nad neach-naidheachd, cuir post-d thugainn aig presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Thoir an aire: ’S e stòr-dàta co-obrachail de thoraidhean bìdh a th’ ann an Open Food Facts, chan eil sinn a’ reic bathar sam bith agus chan eil ceanglaichean againn ris na riochdairean aca. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
