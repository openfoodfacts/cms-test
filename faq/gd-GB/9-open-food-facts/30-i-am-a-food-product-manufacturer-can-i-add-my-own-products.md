Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (faic na Cumhachan Tabhartais)

Tha sinn air àrd-ùrlar an-asgaidh a leasachadh gus leigeil leat dealbhan agus dàta mu na toraidhean agad a thoirt a-steach gu furasta, ge bith dè am meud a th’ ort. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Cuir fios thugainn gus beachdachadh air mar a dh’ fhaodadh sinn a dhol air adhart: producers@openfoodfacts.org
