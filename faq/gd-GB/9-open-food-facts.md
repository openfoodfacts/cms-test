## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Anns cha mhòr a h-uile cùis, tha na cumhachan seirbheis aca a’ toirmeasg gu soilleir cleachdadh neo-phearsanta sam bith agus toirt a-mach an dàta gu lèir no pàirtean den dàta.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Mar sin nuair a thòisich sinn air pròiseact Open Food Facts agus an aplacaid gluasadach, air ais ann an 2012, chuir sinn romhainn a chaochladh a dhèanamh: chan e a-mhàin gu bheil sinn a’ leigeil le cleachdadh agus ath-chleachdadh ar stòr-dàta, gu saor agus gun chosgais, do dhuine sam bith agus airson a h-uile cleachdadh (a’ gabhail a-steach malairteach), ach tha sinn ga bhrosnachadh cuideachd!

Le bhith a’ dèanamh an dàta ri fhaighinn gu poblach (ris an canar _dàta fosgailte_) leigidh sin le daoine fa leth, comann, companaidhean, luchd-rannsachaidh is eile bho air feadh an t-saoghail tagraidhean a chruthachadh agus a leasachadh airson an dàta nach biodh sinn air smaoineachadh orra a-riamh.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (faic na [Teirmichean ath-chleachdaidh](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Mar sin, faodaidh a h-uile duine (a’ gabhail a-steach ach gun a bhith cuibhrichte gu luchd-tabhartais agus luchd-cruthachaidh Open Food Facts) an dàta ath-sgaoileadh agus/no ath-chleachdadh gus làraichean-lìn, seirbheisean, bathar-bog, aplacaidean gluasadach a thogail, no gus artaigilean agus sgrùdaidhean a sgrìobhadh. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Mar eisimpleir, tha an Àrd-ùrlar againn airson Riochdairean gu tur an-asgaidh, agus tha sinn na bu thoilichte leis an sin oir tha sinn den bheachd gum faodadh e an cuideachadh le bhith a’ leasachadh an cuid thoraidhean.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

Ma tha thu nad riochdaire, cuir post-d thugainn aig [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org).

Ma tha thu nad neach-naidheachd, cuir post-d thugainn aig [presse@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:presse@openfoodfacts.org).

Thoir an aire: [’S e stòr-dàta co-obrachail de thoraidhean bìdh a th’ ann an Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/discover), chan eil sinn a’ reic bathar sam bith agus chan eil ceanglaichean againn ris na riochdairean aca. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Tha am fiosrachadh agus an dàta air an cur a-steach le luchd-taic Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. m.e. ma tha fiosrachadh beathachaidh toraidh gu math eadar-dhealaichte bho thoraidhean den aon roinn-seòrsa, is dòcha gur mearachd a th’ ann. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Ma tha thu nad riochdaire, cuir fios thugainn (mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (faic na [Teirmichean Tabhartais](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Tha sinn air àrd-ùrlar an-asgaidh a leasachadh gus leigeil leat dealbhan agus dàta mu na toraidhean agad a thoirt a-steach gu furasta, ge bith dè am meud a th’ ort. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Cuir fios thugainn gus beachdachadh air mar a dh’ fhaodadh sinn a dhol air adhart: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
