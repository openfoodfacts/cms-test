## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Sa halos lahat ng pagkakataon, tahasang ipinagbabawal ng kanilang mga tuntunin ng serbisyo ang anumang hindi personal na paggamit at anumang pagkuha ng lahat ng data o bahagi nito.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Kaya naman noong sinimulan namin ang proyektong Open Food Facts at mobile app, noong 2012, napagpasyahan naming gawin ang eksaktong kabaligtaran: hindi lamang namin pinapayagan ang paggamit at muling paggamit ng aming database, nang libre at walang bayad, para sa lahat at para sa lahat ng gamit (kabilang ang komersyal), kundi hinihikayat din namin ito!

Ang paggawa ng datos na magagamit ng publiko (tinatawag na _open data_) ay nagbibigay-daan sa mga indibidwal, asosasyon, kumpanya, mananaliksik, atbp. mula sa buong mundo na mag-isip at bumuo ng mga aplikasyon para sa datos na tiyak na hindi natin kailanman maiisip.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (tingnan ang [Mga Tuntunin ng muling paggamit](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Kaya naman, lahat (kabilang ngunit hindi limitado sa mga kontribyutor at tagalikha ng Open Food Facts) ay maaaring muling ipamahagi at/o gamitin muli ang datos upang bumuo ng mga website, serbisyo, software, mobile application, o upang magsulat ng mga artikulo at pag-aaral. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Halimbawa, ang aming Plataporma para sa mga Prodyuser ay libre, at lalo kaming natutuwa roon dahil sa tingin namin ay makakatulong ito sa kanila na mapabuti ang kanilang mga produkto.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## How can I contact the Open Food Facts team, ask questions or make suggestions?

Maaari kayong magtanong o magbigay ng mga mungkahi tungkol sa forum o makipag-ugnayan sa amin sa pamamagitan ng e-mail: contact@openfoodfacts.org

Kung ikaw ay isang prodyuser, mangyaring mag-email sa amin sa [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org).

Kung ikaw ay isang mamamahayag, mangyaring mag-email sa amin sa [presse@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:presse@openfoodfacts.org).

Pakitandaan: Ang [Open Food Facts ay isang kolaboratibong database ng mga produktong pagkain](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/discover), hindi kami nagbebenta ng anumang produkto at wala kaming mga link sa kanilang mga prodyuser. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Ang impormasyon at datos ay isinumite ng mga kontribyutor ng Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. hal. kung ang nutrition facts ng isang produkto ay ibang-iba sa mga produktong nasa parehong kategorya, maaaring isa itong pagkakamali. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Kung ikaw ay isang prodyuser, mangyaring [makipag-ugnayan sa amin](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Oo! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (tingnan ang [Mga Tuntunin ng Kontribusyon](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Gumawa kami ng libreng plataporma para madali kang makapag-import ng mga larawan at datos tungkol sa iyong mga produkto, anuman ang iyong laki. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Makipag-ugnayan sa amin upang pag-usapan kung paano kami makakapagpatuloy: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
