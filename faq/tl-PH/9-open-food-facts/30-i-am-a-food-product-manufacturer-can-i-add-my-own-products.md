Oo! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (tingnan ang Mga Tuntunin ng Kontribusyon)

Gumawa kami ng libreng plataporma para madali kang makapag-import ng mga larawan at datos tungkol sa iyong mga produkto, anuman ang iyong laki. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Makipag-ugnayan sa amin upang talakayin kung paano kami makakapag-umpisa: producers@openfoodfacts.org
