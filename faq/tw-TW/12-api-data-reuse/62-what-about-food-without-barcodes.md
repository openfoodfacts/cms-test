Nsɛm a ɛfa nnuan a wɔakyekyere ho nkutoo na ɛwɔ Open Food Facts mu. Sɛ wopɛ sɛ wɔkyekyem pɛpɛɛpɛ a, nnɔbae (sɛ nhwɛso no, tomato anaa banana) ne nnuan afoforo bo a, wubetumi de ɔman no mu aduannuru ho nsɛm a wɔahyehyɛ a aban mpanyimfo de ma no biako adi dwuma mmom.

Hyɛ no nsow: Nsɛm a wɔahyehyɛ a ɛwɔ ase ha no kura ɔman no mu nnuan ho nsɛm a ɛho hia sen biara no bi. Sɛ wosusu sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ wɔde database foforɔ bi ka list no ho a, yɛsrɛ wo, di yɛn ho nkɔmmɔ wɔ: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact
