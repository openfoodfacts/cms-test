Sɛ wode nneɛma bɛka yɛn platform no ho a, ɛsɛ sɛ wudi kan si sɛnea wopɛ sɛ wotoa so ho gyinae. Nneɛma 4 na ɛwɔ hɔ a wubetumi apaw:

- Nsa: sɛ wowɔ nneɛma kakraa bi a, ɛda so ara yɛ yie sɛ wode nsa bɛbɔ nneɛma krataa biara wɔ Pro Platform no so.

- Ɛnam spreadsheet import so: wobɛtumi atwe spreadsheet template a Open Food Facts de ama no, anaasɛ wobɛfa extraction bi afiri wo IT system no mu tẽẽ akɔ Pro platform no mu.

- Via product catalogues’ nkitahodi ahorow so. Sɛ woyɛ client wɔ EQUADIS, AGENA3000 anaa BAYARD a, wobɛtumi de data akɔ Open Food Facts mu wɔ 1 click mu. Sɛ wo ne PIM (Product Information Management) foforo reyɛ adwuma a, yɛsrɛ wo, di yɛn ho nkɔmmɔ : producers@openfoodfacts.org

- Ɛnam abusuabɔ a wɔahyɛ da ayɛ a ɛda wo adwumakuw ne Open Food Facts ntam so. Sɛ wokan nneɛma bɛboro 1000 wɔ wo portfolio mu a, yebetumi asusuw hokwan a ɛwɔ hɔ sɛ yɛbɛkyekye nkitahodi pɔtee bi a ɛbɛma data no akɔ so ayɛ mmerɛw no ho.Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn dwumadie akwankyerɛ no na/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn video nkyerɛkyerɛ no mu :-)
