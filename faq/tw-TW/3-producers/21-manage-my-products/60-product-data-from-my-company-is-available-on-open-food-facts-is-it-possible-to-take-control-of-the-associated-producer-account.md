Aane saa ara! Wubetumi adi producer akontaabu a ɛbata ho no so denam Open Food Facts akontaabu a wobɛhyehyɛ a w’adwuma address wom no so. Sɛ wokyerɛw wo din a, ka ahyehyɛde a ɛwɔ kratasin no so no din na ama woatumi anya producer space a ɛne no hyia no.

Anamɔn tiawa bi a ɛbɛma wɔagye atom no ho behia na ama woahwɛ ahu sɛ woyɛ nea ɔyɛe no ampa.

Wubetumi awie data no, de mfonini ahorow aka ho, anya nyansahyɛ ahorow a ɛyɛ adwuma ankasa de ama Nutri-Score no atu mpɔn, ne nea ɛkeka ho pii. Nokwarem no, eyi nyinaa yɛ nea wontua hwee koraa. Wubetumi nso de nnipa foforo a wɔyɛ nneɛma ho data nhyehyɛe te sɛ EQUADIS , AGENA3000 ne Bayard consulting abɔ mu.

→ Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn dwumadie akwankyerɛ no na/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn video nkyerɛkyerɛ :-)
