Yɛayɛ nnwuma pɔtee bi ama nnuru a wɔde yɛ ahosiesie, ama mmoa aduan, ne nneɛma afoforo nso:[Open Beauty Facts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/),[Open Pet Food Facts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)ne[Open Products Facts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/)sɛnea ɛte biara.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
