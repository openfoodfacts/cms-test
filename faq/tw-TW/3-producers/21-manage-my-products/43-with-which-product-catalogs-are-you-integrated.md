Mprempren wɔn a wɔyɛ nneɛma no betumi de wɔn nneɛma ho nsɛm ne mfonini ahorow amena wɔ bere ankasa mu afi Equadis, AGENA3000 ne BAYARD consulting akɔ Open Food Facts.

Nokwarem no, wubenya nso mfaso afi nyansahyɛ ahorow a wɔde ma wɔ nhyehyɛe foforo ne asɛnka agua no mu nneɛma nyinaa mu.

Yɛn blog post no kyerɛkyerɛ anammɔn a ɛsɛ sɛ wudi akyi sɛ woyɛ EQUADIS adetɔfo.

Sɛ woyɛ AGENA3000 adetɔfo (Product A3 PIM INDUSTRY) a, ɛsɛ sɛ wopaw nea ogye no "Open Food Facts" kɛkɛ bere a wode wo nneɛma ho nkrataa remena no. Nsɛm pii wɔ saa blog post yi so.

Sɛ woyɛ adetɔfoɔ a ɔwɔ nneɛma foforɔ data katalog (Salsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, ne nea ɛkeka ho) . yɛsrɛ sɛ ma yɛnhu wɔ producers@openfoodfacts.org

→ Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn dwumadie akwankyerɛ no na/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn video nkyerɛkyerɛ :-)
