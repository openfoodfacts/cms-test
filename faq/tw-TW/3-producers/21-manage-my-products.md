## Dɛn na ɛma awerɛhyem sɛ data a ɛwɔ Open Food Facts mu no yɛ papa?

**Sɛ yɛbɛkɔ so ama data no ayɛ papa a yɛde yɛn ho to adum 4 so:**

1. Mpɔtam hɔfo de data ne mfonini ahorow ka ho bere nyinaa na wɔyɛ nhwehwɛmufo a wɔhwɛ nneɛma so ankasa.

2. Yɛde nteaseɛ mmara asi hɔ de ahunu mfomsoɔ a ɛwɔ nneɛma a wɔde yɛ adwuma no nkrataa so.Sɛ nhwɛso no: sɛ nneɛma a wɔde yɛ aduan no mu duru nyinaa boro ade no mu duru nyinaa so a, anomaly bi wɔ hɔ. Yɛwɔ afoforo bɛyɛ 50 quality checks.

3. Esiane Machine Learning nti, yɛto asiane a ɛwɔ input mfomso mu no ano hye. Yɛn mfiridwuma no ma yetumi yi nsɛm a wɔakyerɛw afi mfonini ahorow a wɔn a wɔde mmoa mae no twae mu

4. Esiane Santé Publique France mmoa nti, yɛyɛɛ portal bi a ɛma wɔn a wɔyɛ nneɛma no tumi de wɔn data a wɔayɛ no pii gu so na wɔnam saayɛ so teɛteɛ/wie mpɔtam hɔfo ntoboa.

## Ɔkwan bɛn so na wɔde nneɛma bɛka ho?

Sɛ wode nneɛma bɛka yɛn platform no ho a, ɛsɛ sɛ wudi kan si sɛnea wopɛ sɛ wotoa so ho gyinae. Nneɛma 4 na ɛwɔ hɔ a wubetumi apaw:

- **Nsaano**: sɛ wowɔ nneɛma kakraa bi a, ɛda so ara yɛ yie sɛ wode nsa bɛbɔ nneɛma krataa biara wɔ Pro Platform no so.

- **Ɛnam spreadsheet import so**: wobɛtumi atwe spreadsheet [template](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) a Open Food Facts de ama no, anaasɛ wobɛtumi de biribi a wɔayi afiri wo IT nhyehyɛeɛ no mu aba Pro platform no mu tẽẽ.

- **Via product catalogues' nkitahodi ahorow**. Sɛ woyɛ client wɔ EQUADIS, AGENA3000 anaa BAYARD a, wobɛtumi de data akɔ Open Food Facts mu wɔ 1 click mu. Sɛ wo ne PIM (Product Information Management) foforo reyɛ adwuma a, yɛsrɛ wo, di yɛn ho nkɔmmɔ : producers@openfoodfacts.org

- **Via a tailored connexion** a ɛda wo company ne Open Food Facts ntam. Sɛ wokan nneɛma bɛboro 1000 wɔ wo portfolio mu a, yebetumi asusuw hokwan a ɛwɔ hɔ sɛ yɛbɛkyekye nkitahodi pɔtee bi a ɛbɛma data a wɔde kɔ baabi foforo no ayɛ mmerɛw ho.

Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn [ɔdefo akwankyerɛ](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) na/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn [video nkyerɛkyerɛ](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Is it possible to delete products that are no longer sold?

Sɛ wopɛ sɛ woyi ade bi a enni gua so bio a, ɛsɛ sɛ wohyɛ adaka a ɛne "wɔntɔn saa ade yi bio" no mu kɛkɛ bere a woresiesie wo nneɛma krataa no.

Sɛ wopɛ sɛ woyi nneɛma pii fi hɔ a, yɛhyɛ nyansa sɛ di ɔkwan a wɔaka ho asɛm wɔ [ɔdefo akwankyerɛ](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Platform-User-Guide.pdf), wɔ kr. 18. Ɔde ne nsa kyerɛɛ ne so.

Wubetumi nso ne yɛn adi nkitaho denam producers@openfoodfacts.org so sɛ wowɔ nsɛmmisa biara a.

Sɛ wɔde ade no sie a, ɛrenpue bio wɔ Open Food Facts mu.Nanso ɛbɛkɔ so atra yɛn database no mu sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ a yebetumi akora nneɛma a wɔtɔn bere tenten no ho abakɔsɛm.

## So adwumakuw no ho nsɛm di kan sen nsɛm a ɛwɔ asɛnka agua so dedaw?

Sɛ obi a ɔyɛ nneɛma no de ne nneɛma no biako to Intanɛt so a ne yɛfo akontaabu ka ho na nneɛma ho krataa bi wɔ hɔ dedaw a, eyi bɛma awie.

Nsɛm a nea ɔyɛ no de bɛto hɔ no bedi kan bere nyinaa na ɔno nkutoo na obetumi asesa ne nneɛma bere a wɔahyɛ nsɛm no ma no.

Sɛ wɔkyekyɛ data a ɛnam platform no so de mena no mu a, nsɛm a mpɔtam hɔfo de ka ho fi nneɛma a wɔde ahyɛ mu no betumi awie.

## Ɔkwan bɛn so na ɛsɛ sɛ mede me data mena?

[template](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) yɛ nea Open Food Facts na ɛde ma.

Nanso, spreadsheet format biara (xlsx, csv) bɛtumi de aba wɔ platform no mu.  
Wɔayɛ yɛn algorithm no sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ a ɛne su ahorow a efi wo fael no mu no bɛyɛ pɛ, ne nea ɛwɔ Open Food Facts mu no. Hwɛ yiye sɛ wobɛhwɛ stage a ɛne no hyia no mprenu bere nyinaa efisɛ ebia ebehia sɛ wode nsa siesie emu bi.

Sɛ wo nsɛm no mu apaapae wɔ spreadsheet ahorow pii ntam a, wubetumi de aba nnidiso nnidiso - hwɛ hu sɛ wobɛyɛ nhyiam no sɛ format no sesa a.

Wubetumi nso de Excel fael bi aba de aka EQUADIS anaa AGENA3000 a wode aba no ho, sɛ data no nnya nkɔɔ wo nsɛm nhyehyɛe no mu a. Wɔ saa nsɛm yi mu no, mma wo werɛ mmfi sɛ wobɛsrɛ sɛ wɔmfa nkɔ ɔmanfo database no mu mfi interface no so.

→ Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn [ɔdefo akwankyerɛ](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) ne/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn [video nkyerɛkyerɛ](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Product catalogs bɛn na wode afrafra?

Mprempren wɔn a wɔyɛ nneɛma no betumi de wɔn nneɛma ho nsɛm ne mfonini amena wɔ bere ankasa mu afi **Equadis,** **AGENA3000 ne BAYARD consulting** so akɔ Open Food Facts so.

Nokwarem no, wubenya nso mfaso afi nyansahyɛ ahorow a wɔde ma wɔ nhyehyɛe foforo ne asɛnka agua no mu nneɛma nyinaa mu.

[Yɛn blog post](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/real-time-product-data-from-producers-on-open-food-facts-thanks-to-the-new-equadis-integration) kyerɛkyerɛ anammɔn a ɛsɛ sɛ wudi akyi sɛ woyɛ EQUADIS adetɔfo a.

Sɛ woyɛ AGENA3000 adetɔfo (Product A3 PIM INDUSTRY) a, ɛsɛ sɛ wopaw nea ogye no "Open Food Facts" kɛkɛ bere a wode wo nneɛma ho nkrataa remena no. [Nsɛm pii wɔ saa blog asɛm yi so](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/share-your-product-data-in-1-click-with-the-new-agena3000-connector).

Sɛ woyɛ adetɔfoɔ a ɔwɔ nneɛma foforɔ data katalog (Salsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, ne nea ɛkeka ho) . yɛsrɛ sɛ ma yɛnhu wɔ [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

→ Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn [ɔdefo akwankyerɛ](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) ne/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn [video nkyerɛkyerɛ](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Sɛnea wobɛfa Pro platform no so de nneɛma a enni barcodes aka ho?

Wɔ nneɛma a enni barcode, wɔ site no so anaa producer platform no so no, "Product without barcode" button wɔ benkum so a ɛma wo kwan ma wode ka ho. Afei wɔyɛ identifier bi ankasa.

## Can animal products, non-food products, cosmetics and other products be integrated into Open Food Facts?

Yɛayɛ nnwuma pɔtee bi ama cosmetics, ama mmoa aduan, ne saa ara nso na nneɛma afoforo:Open Beauty Facts,Open Pet Food FactsandOpen Products Factsrespectively.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.

## Product data a efi m’adwumakuw no hɔ wɔ Open Food Facts so. So ebetumi aba sɛ wobedi nea ɔyɛ nneɛma a ɛbata ho no akontaabu so?

Aane saa ara! Wubetumi adi producer akontaabu a ɛbata ho no so denam Open Food Facts akontaabu a wobɛhyehyɛ a w’adwuma address wom no so. Sɛ wokyerɛw wo din a, ka ahyehyɛde a ɛwɔ kratasin no so no din na ama woatumi anya producer space a ɛne no hyia no.

Anamɔn tiawa bi a ɛbɛma wɔagye atom no ho behia na ama woahwɛ ahu sɛ woyɛ nea ɔyɛe no ampa.

Wubetumi awie data no, de mfonini ahorow aka ho, anya nyansahyɛ ahorow a ɛyɛ adwuma ankasa de ama Nutri-Score no atu mpɔn, ne nea ɛkeka ho pii. Nokwarem no, eyi nyinaa yɛ nea wontua hwee koraa. Wubetumi nso de nnipa foforo a wɔyɛ nneɛma ho data nhyehyɛe te sɛ EQUADIS , AGENA3000 ne Bayard consulting abɔ mu.

→ Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn [ɔdefo akwankyerɛ](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) ne/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn [video nkyerɛkyerɛ](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Bere tenten ahe na egye ansa na wɔayɛ producer data foforo wɔ app foforo a ɛsan de Open Food Facts database di dwuma no so?

Egyina apps no so. Wɔn a wɔde yɛn API ahorow a ɛyɛ dodow no ara di dwuma no fam no, ɛyɛ ntɛm ara.  
Wɔn a wɔde yɛn da biara da nneɛma a yɛde kɔ amannɔne di dwuma no, ɛyɛ D+1 sɛ wɔyɛ no foforo a.

## So data a wode fi adetɔn ho kyerɛwtohɔ mu ba no wie anaasɛ ɛkyerɛw nneɛma ho kyerɛwtohɔ a ɛwɔ hɔ dedaw no so?

Yɛde barcode no di dwuma de hu nneɛma, sɛ wode kyerɛwtohɔ bi a ɛwɔ barcode koro no ara ne kyerɛwtohɔ a ɛwɔ hɔ dedaw no ba a, wɔbɛka nsɛm no abom.

Wɔ data te sɛ ingredient list, nutritional values (botae a ɛteɛ biako pɛ na ebetumi aba), data a wɔde fa producer platform so de bɛmena no bɛkyerɛw data a ɛwɔ hɔ dedaw no so.

Wɔ data te sɛ labels/categories/brands (gyinabea ahorow pii a ɛteɛ a ebetumi aba) ho no, wɔka nsɛm no bom. Sɛ data no nyɛ nokware wɔ ɔmanfoɔ atenaeɛ no so a, wobɛtumi asesa afiri krataa a ɛwɔ ɔmanfoɔ atenaeɛ no so de ayi botaeɛ a ɛnteɛ no afiri hɔ.

→ Sɛ wopɛ nsɛm pii a, yɛto nsa frɛ wo sɛ twe yɛn [ɔdefo akwankyerɛ](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) ne/anaasɛ hwɛ yɛn [video nkyerɛkyerɛ](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## So nneɛma a wɔde yɛ aduan no nnidiso nnidiso ho hia?

Nneɛma a wɔde yɛ aduan no nnidiso nnidiso ho hia: egyina hɔ ma nhyehyɛe no denam dodow so.
Ɔkwan a eye sen biara a wobɛfa so ahyɛ nneɛma a wɔde yɛ aduan no ma ne sɛ wubedi nea wɔakyerɛw wɔ nneɛma a wɔde ahyɛ mu no so pɛpɛɛpɛ. Ɛno nti na ɛho hia kɛse nso sɛ wobɛtintim nneɛma a wɔde yɛ aduan no ho mfonini.

## So metumi de mfonini ahorow afa AGENA3000 nso amena?
