## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Ɛkame ayɛ sɛ wɔ tebea horow nyinaa mu no, wɔn adwumayɛ ho nhyehyɛe no bara pefee sɛ wɔmfa nni dwuma a ɛnyɛ wɔn ankasa de ne sɛ wobeyi data anaa data no afã horow nyinaa afi mu.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Enti bere a yɛhyɛɛ Open Food Facts adwuma ne mobile app ase, wɔ afe 2012 mu tɔnn no, yɛsii gyinae sɛ yɛbɛyɛ nea ɛne no bɔ abira pɛpɛɛpɛ: ɛnyɛ sɛ yɛma kwan ma wɔde yɛn database no di dwuma na wɔsan de di dwuma bio, kwa na wontua hwee, ma obiara ne dwumadie nyinaa (a aguadi ka ho), na mmom yɛhyɛ ho nkuran nso!

Data no a wɔbɛma ɔmanfo anya (nea wonim no sɛ _open data_) ma ankorankoro, fekuw, nnwumakuw, nhwehwɛmufo ne nea ɛkeka ho a wofi wiase nyinaa tumi susuw ho na wɔyɛ application ahorow ma data no a akyinnye biara nni ho sɛ anka yɛrensusuw ho da.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (hwɛ [Nsɛm a wɔde di dwuma bio](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Enti obiara (a Open Food Facts mmoa ne wɔn a wɔyɛɛ no ka ho nanso ɛnyɛ wɔn nko ara) betumi asan akyekyɛ na/anaasɛ wɔasan de data no adi dwuma de ayɛ wɛbsaet, nnwuma, softwea, mobile application, anaasɛ akyerɛw nsɛm ne adesua. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Sɛ nhwɛso no, yɛn Platform for Producers no yɛ nea wontua hwee koraa, na yɛn ani gye ho kɛse efisɛ yesusuw sɛ ebia ɛbɛboa wɔn ma wɔatu mpɔn wɔ wɔn nneɛma mu.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

Sɛ woyɛ obi a ɔyɛ nneɛma a, yɛsrɛ wo, fa email mena yɛn wɔ [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org).

Sɛ woyɛ nsɛm ho amanneɛbɔfo a, yɛsrɛ wo, fa email mena yɛn wɔ [presse@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:presse@openfoodfacts.org).

Yɛsrɛ sɛ hyɛ no nsow: [Open Food Facts yɛ nnuan ho nsɛm a wɔbom yɛ](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/discover), yɛntɔn nneɛma biara na yenni nkitahodi biara ne wɔn a wɔyɛ no. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Nsɛm ne data no, Open Food Facts a wɔde mmoa mae no na wɔde mena. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. s.e., sɛ aduannuru ho nokwasɛm a ɛwɔ ade bi mu no yɛ soronko koraa wɔ nneɛma a ɛwɔ ɔfã koro mu a, ebetumi ayɛ mfomso. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Sɛ woyɛ producer a, yɛsrɛ wo [di yɛn ho nkɔmmɔ](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (hwɛ [Ntoboa ho Nsɛm](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Yɛayɛ platform a wontua hwee a ɛbɛma woatumi de mfonini ne data a ɛfa wo nneɛma ho aba ntɛm, ɛmfa ho sɛnea wo kɛse te. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Di yɛn ho nkɔmmɔ na yɛasusuw sɛnea yebetumi akɔ so ho: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
