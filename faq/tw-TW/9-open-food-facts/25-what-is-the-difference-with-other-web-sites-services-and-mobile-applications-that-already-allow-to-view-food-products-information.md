The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Ɛkame ayɛ sɛ wɔ tebea horow nyinaa mu no, wɔn adwumayɛ ho nhyehyɛe no bara pefee sɛ wɔmfa nni dwuma a ɛnyɛ wɔn ankasa de ne sɛ wobeyi data anaa data no afã horow nyinaa afi mu.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Enti bere a yɛhyɛɛ Open Food Facts adwuma ne mobile app ase, wɔ afe 2012 mu tɔnn no, yɛsii gyinae sɛ yɛbɛyɛ nea ɛne no bɔ abira pɛpɛɛpɛ: ɛnyɛ sɛ yɛma kwan ma wɔde yɛn database no di dwuma na wɔsan de di dwuma bio, kwa na wontua hwee, ma obiara ne dwumadie nyinaa (a aguadi ka ho), na mmom yɛhyɛ ho nkuran nso!

Data no a wɔbɛma ɔmanfo anya (nea wonim no sɛ open data) ma ankorankoro, fekuw, nnwumakuw, nhwehwɛmufo ne nea ɛkeka ho a wofi wiase nyinaa tumi susuw ho na wɔyɛ application ahorow ma data no a akyinnye biara nni ho sɛ anka yɛrensusuw ho da.
