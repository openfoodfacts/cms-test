Ɛbɛgye
bere tenten dodo sɛ yɛbɛbobɔ ahyehyɛdeɛ a wɔde wɔn ho to Open Food Facts
platform no nyinaa din.

- Mobile application bɛboro 100
  di atiefo pii ahiade ho dwuma: asikreyare (VeryDiab), allergies, aduan
  kari pɛ denam artificial intelligence (FoodVisor), dɛmdi, ne nea ɛkeka ho. Yɛn
  database no nso maa kwan ma wɔbɔɔ Yuka.
- Nsɛmma nhoma pii: CombienDeSucres.fr,
  CestEmballePresDeChezVous.fr...
- Nsɛm ho amanneɛbɔfo : Les Décodeurs du Monde, BFM
  TV
- Nhwehwɛmufo a wɔwɔ nyansahu mu
  nsɛm bɛyɛ 100 mu
- Aduankorabea ne biakoyɛ sotɔɔ ahorow:
  Data For Good adwuma bi a ɛkɔ so wɔ adwuma a wɔde sɔ hwɛ a
  Franse Mmeamudua Kɔkɔɔ no fii ase no mu, nkabom wɔ sika a wɔhwɛ so wɔ aduan
  adwumayɛkuw ketewa bi mu.
- Nnwumakuw: Terraillon de yɛn data
  di dwuma de ma nkwa ma ne nsenia a ɛka bom no.
- Dwumadie sukuu ahodoɔ (Simplon, Open
  Adesuadan) de Open Food Facts ahyɛ mu sɛ adwuma a ɛkanyan wɔ wɔn
  adesua nhyehyɛeɛ mu.
