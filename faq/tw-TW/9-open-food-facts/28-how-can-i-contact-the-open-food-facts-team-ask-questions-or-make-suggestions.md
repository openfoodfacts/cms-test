Wubetumi abisa nsɛm anaa wode nyansahyɛ ahorow ama wɔ[forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)anaasɛ wobɛfa e-mail so ne yɛn adi nkitaho:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Sɛ woyɛ obi a ɔyɛ nneɛma a, yɛsrɛ wo, fa email mena yɛn wɔ producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Sɛ woyɛ nsɛm ho amanneɛbɔfo a, yɛsrɛ wo, fa email mena yɛn wɔ presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Yɛsrɛ sɛ hyɛ no nsow: Open Food Facts yɛ nnuan ho nsɛm a wɔbom yɛ, yɛntɔn nneɛma biara na yenni nkitahodi biara ne wɔn a wɔyɛ no. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
