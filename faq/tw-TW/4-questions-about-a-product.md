## Mewɔ asɛmmisa / asɛm bi a wɔka fa ade bi ho

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/) yɛ adwumayɛkuo a adetɔfoɔ ayɛ na wɔma wɔn aduane sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛtumi akyerɛ nkyerɛwdeɛ ase na wɔde atoto nneɛma ho.

Sɛ wopɛ ade bi anaa asɛm bi a wɔka ho nsɛm pii a, kyerɛw nea ɔyɛe no. Yɛnnni nkitahodi ho nsɛm a ɛfa wɔn a wɔyɛ nneɛma ho nanso wɔtaa wɔ nneɛma a wɔde kyekyere nneɛma no so.

Sɛ wopɛ sɛ woboa yɛn ma yɛde nhumu pii ba adetɔfo te sɛ wo anaa yɛn anim a, wubetumi de yɛn iPhone anaa Android app no adi dwuma de nneɛma aka ho: [https://openfoodfacts.app](https://openfoodfacts.app/)

Na sɛ wopɛ sɛ wobɔ adwuma no ho nkɔmmɔ a, wubetumi akɔka Facebook kuw a wɔde mmoa ma no ho: [https://www.facebook.com/groups/openfoodfacts/](https://www.facebook.com/groups/openfoodfacts/) anaasɛ wo ne afoforo a wɔde mmoa mae no abɔ nkɔmmɔ wɔ Slack:

[http://slack.openfoodfacts.org](https://slack.openfoodfacts.org/) Nsɛm a wɔka kyerɛ wɔ ɔkwan a ɛyɛ nwonwa so.
