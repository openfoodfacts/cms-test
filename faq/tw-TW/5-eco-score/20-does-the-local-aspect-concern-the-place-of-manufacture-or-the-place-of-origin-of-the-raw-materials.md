title: "So mpɔtam hɔ afã no fa beae a wɔyɛe anaa baabi a nneɛma a wɔde yɛ nneɛma no fi ba no ho?"
order: 20. Ɔde ne nsa kyerɛɛ ne so
lang: tw-gb

Mpɔtam hɔ fã no fa faako a nneɛma a wɔde yɛ nneɛma no fi bae no ho. Eyi nti na ɛho hia sɛ wɔkyerɛw wɔn ɔha biara mu nkyem ne wɔn mfiase nso wɔ nneɛma a wɔde yɛ nneɛma no din mu anaasɛ wɔ afuw a wɔahyira so ama nneɛma a wɔde yɛ no mfiase no.
