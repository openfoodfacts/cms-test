## Dɛn nti na Nutri-Score nni hɔ mma mmoa?

Ɛsono aboa biara (ɔwɔ nni aduannuru a ohia te sɛ ɔkraman anaa ɔkraman) .

Wɔ aboa koro te sɛ akraman mu mpo no, aduannuru a wohia no gu ahorow sɛnea asetra a ɔtra ase tena ase, a ebetumi aba sɛ wɔayiyi no anaa nyarewa ahorow te.

Enti ɛyɛ den sɛ wobebu nkontabuo a wɔde bɛma mmoa aduane a nhwehwɛmufoɔ du du pii mfa wɔn ho nhyɛ mu na wɔnyɛ algorithm ahodoɔ du du pii, a wɔnyɛɛ saa de bɛsi nnɛ.

## Where can I find the Open Pet Food Facts API ?

Open Pet Food Facts API is 98% the same as Open Food Facts API so if you use Open Food Facts API documentation you should have a pretty clear view of the Open Pet Food Facts API.

Note that features like the Knowledge Panels, Eco-Score, Nutri-Score are not supported (either due to the topic, or to the old codebase).

[https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/ref-v2/](https://aduane ho nsɛm a wɔabue.github.io/aduan ho nsɛm a wɔabue-somfo/api/ref-v2/)
