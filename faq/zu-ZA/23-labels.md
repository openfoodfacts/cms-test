## Njengelebula, kubalulekile yini ukudala i-akhawunti?

Njengelebula, njengoba ungeyena umkhiqizi oqondile wemikhiqizo, awudingi ukudala i-akhawunti yomkhiqizi.

---

## Ngingumthengisi welebula, ngingahlanganyela kanjani kulo msebenzi?

Njengelebula into efanele kakhulu okufanele uyenze ukuxhumana nathi **(producers@openfoodfacts.org)** bese usithumelela uhlu lwemikhiqizo lapho ilebula yakho iboniswa khona ukuze sikwazi ukuyifaka emashidini omkhiqizo ahlobene.
