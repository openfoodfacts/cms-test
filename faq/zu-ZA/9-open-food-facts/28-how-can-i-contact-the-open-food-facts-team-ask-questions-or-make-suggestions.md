Ungabuza imibuzo noma wenze iziphakamiso ku-[forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)noma usithinte nge-imeyili:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Uma ungumkhiqizi, sicela usithumelele i-imeyili ku-producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Uma ungumlobi wezindaba, sicela usithumelele i-imeyili ku-presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Sicela uqaphele: I-Open Food Facts iyisizindalwazi esisebenzisanayo semikhiqizo yokudla, asithengisi noma yimuphi umkhiqizo futhi asinazo izixhumanisi kubakhiqizi bayo. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
