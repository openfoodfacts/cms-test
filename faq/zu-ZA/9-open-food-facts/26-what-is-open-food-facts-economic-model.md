Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (bheka Imigomo Yokusebenzisa Kabusha)

Wonke umuntu (kufaka phakathi kodwa kungagcini lapho kubanikeli nabadali be-Open Food Facts) angaphinde asabalalise futhi/noma asebenzise kabusha idatha ukwakha amawebhusayithi, izinsizakalo, isofthiwe, izinhlelo zokusebenza zeselula, noma ukubhala izihloko nezifundo. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Isibonelo, i-Platform yethu Yabakhiqizi imahhala ngokuphelele, futhi sijabule kakhulu ngalokho ngoba sicabanga ukuthi kungabasiza ukuthuthukisa imikhiqizo yabo.
