## Ngingathanda ukwengeza ilogo entsha yamalebula

Nansi inqubo:

- thola igama elibhalwe nge-canonical\*\* lelebula ku-[labels' taxonomy](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/blob/main/taxonomies/labels.txt) (yinto yokuqala ohlwini lwamagama afanayo amalebula, isib. en:100% yemifino)
-

**thola ilogo isezingeni elihle**: gwema ukusebenzisa isithombe somnikeli esingafaneleki kuleli cala; amalebula amaningi anama-logo asemthethweni ekhwalithi ephezulu, ngezinye izikhathi ngefomethi ye-vector (okungcono nakakhulu kithi); uma nje sisebenzisa ilogo ukwazisa ngobuqotho ngokuba khona kwelebula
ekupakishweni komkhiqizo, asikho isidingo sokucela imvume.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

**qamba ifayela kanje**: name-of-the-label.[width]x90.png lapho ububanzi buyibubanzi belogo uma iphakeme ngamaphikseli angu-90. Amagama efayela kudingeka angafakwanga isikhanyiso, abe ngosonhlamvukazi abancane futhi asebenzise u-"-" esikhundleni sezikhala.

- bese ufaka ilogo kufolda ehambisana negama layo elisemthethweni. Uma igama elisemthethweni lingu-en:something, khona-ke kudingeka libe ku-/en/. I-root directory yama-logo ithi [https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/tree/main/html/images/lang](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/tree/main/html/images/lang)
