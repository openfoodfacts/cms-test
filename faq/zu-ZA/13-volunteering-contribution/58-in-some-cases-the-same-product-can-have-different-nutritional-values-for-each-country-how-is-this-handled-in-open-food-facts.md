99% wesikhathi, abakhiqizi bazokwakha amabhakhodi ahlukene ezinhlotsheni ezahlukene zemikhiqizo yabo. Isibonelo esidumile umehluko phakathi kweNutella yesiFulentshi nesiJalimane ngokwezinga lokujiya, ngenxa yomehluko wesinkwa emazweni ahlukahlukene. Amafomula amabili ahlukene, amabhakhodi amabili ahlukene.

Nokho, ukungqubuzana kwebhakhodi kungenzeka kumakhodi amafushane (i-EAN-8) avame ukusetshenziswa kabusha yizitolo ezithile kulo lonke elaseYurophu nase-US. Okwamanje asixazululi lokho kungqubuzana kwebhakhodi, kodwa kufanele kwenzeke ukwenza kanjalo ngokuthola indawo evamile yomsebenzisi (akuvamile kakhulu ukuba nokungqubuzana kwebhakhodi ezweni).

Esikhathini eside, sikhuthaza abakhiqizi ukuthi bathuthele ku-EAN-13 ukuze bagweme lokho kungqubuzana kwamabhakhodi.
