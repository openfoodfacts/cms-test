Inhloso yalezi zinkomba ukuhlinzeka ngolwazi oluqondakala kalula kumthengi.

Iqembu le-NOVA libhekene nokuguqulwa komkhiqizo, i-Eco-Score nomthelela wayo emvelweni kanye ne-Nutri-Score ngezakhi zokudla okunempilo, lazi ukuthi uma umkhiqizo unama-score amaningi aseduze no-A, kulapho uba sezingeni eliphezulu khona.

Izikolo ezintathu azibhekani nezinto ezifanayo. Ngakho-ke akunakwenzeka ukuziqhathanisa zodwa.
