Ukuze wengeze imikhiqizo epulatifomu yethu, okokuqala udinga ukunquma ukuthi ufuna ukuqhubeka kanjani. Kunezinketho ezine:

- Ngokwenza ngesandla: uma unenani elincane lemikhiqizo, kusengenzeka ukudala ishidi ngalinye lomkhiqizo ngesandla ku-Pro Platform.

- Ngokungenisa ispredishithi: ungalanda ithempulethi yespredishithi enikezwe yi-Open Food Facts, noma ungenise ngqo okukhishwe ohlelweni lwakho lwe-IT uye epulatifomu ye-Pro.

- Ngezixhumi zamakhathalogi emikhiqizo. Uma ungumthengi ku-EQUADIS, AGENA3000 noma i-BAYARD, ungalayisha idatha ku-Open Food Facts ngokuchofoza okukodwa. Uma usebenza nenye i-PIM (Ukuphathwa Kolwazi Lomkhiqizo), sicela usithinte ku-: producers@openfoodfacts.org

- Ngokuxhumana okuklanyelwe inkampani yakho kanye ne-Open Food Facts. Uma ubala imikhiqizo engaphezu kwe-1000 kuphothifoliyo yakho, singaxoxa ngethuba lokwakha isixhumi esithile ukuze kube lula ukudluliselwa kwedatha. Ukuze uthole imininingwane eyengeziwe, sikumema ukuthi ulande umhlahlandlela wethu womsebenzisi kanye/noma ubheke izifundo zethu zevidiyo :-)
