Abakhiqizi manje sebengathumela idatha yomkhiqizo wabo nezithombe ngesikhathi sangempela kusuka ku-Equadis, i-AGENA3000 kanye ne-BAYARD consulting kuya ku-Open Food Facts.

Uzozuza futhi eziphakamisweni zokuguqulwa kwesakhiwo kanye nazo zonke izici zepulatifomu.

Okuthunyelwe kwethu kwebhulogi kuchaza izinyathelo okufanele uzilandele uma ungumthengi we-EQUADIS.

Uma ungumthengi we-AGENA3000 (i-Product A3 PIM INDUSTRY), kufanele ukhethe umamukeli "Amaqiniso Okudla Avulekile" lapho uthumela amashidi omkhiqizo wakho. Ulwazi olwengeziwe ngalokhu okuthunyelwe kwebhulogi.

Uma ungumthengi wekhathalogi yedatha yomkhiqizo womunye (i-Salsify - ex-Alkemics, i-ConsoTrust, i-1worldSync, i-Akeneo, i-PIMWorks, njll.) sicela usazise ku-producers@openfoodfacts.org
