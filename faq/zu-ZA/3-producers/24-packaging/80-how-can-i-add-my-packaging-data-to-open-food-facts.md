Njengomkhiqizi, indlela ephumelela kakhulu yokwengeza idatha yokupakisha emikhiqizweni yakho ukugcwalisa lesi sipredishithi se-excel ulwazi oludingekayo, kusukela kukholomu ethi "AU".

Kuzo zonke izingxenye zokupakisha, kuzodingeka ufake:

- Inani lamayunithi (isb: amabhodwe ama-yogurt ama-4)

- Isimo (isib: imbiza, isembozo, ibhokisi, njll.)

- Izinto (isib: i-PET, iphepha, ingilazi, njll.)

- Umyalelo wokusebenzisa kabusha (isib: ukuphinda usebenzise, ukulahla, njll.)

- Isisindo seyunithi eyodwa engenalutho (nge-gr)

- Inani eliphelele lomkhiqizo eliqukethwe ngeyunithi ngayinye

Lesi sipredishithi singangeniswa epulatifomu yethu enikezelwe kochwepheshe.
