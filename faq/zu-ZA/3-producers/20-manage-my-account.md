## Ingabe ama-akhawunti amaningi e-Open Food Facts angaxhunyaniswa nenkampani eyodwa?

Yebo, sicela uthumele i-imeyili ku-[producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:support@openfoodfacts.org), ukuze sikwazi ukuxhumanisa wonke ama-akhawunti nenkampani efanayo.

---

## Ungayiguqula kanjani i-akhawunti yami ye-Open Food Facts ibe yi-akhawunti ye-Pro?

Ungafinyelela i-[Pro platform](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/) ngeziqinisekiso ze-akhawunti yakho ekhona.

Uma unguchwepheshe, kuzodingeka usebenzise ikheli lakho le-imeyili lochwepheshe ukuze sikwazi ukuqinisekisa i-akhawunti yakho.

## Ngingawatholaphi amadokhumenti angangisiza ngisebenzise ipulatifomu ye-Pro?

Kunezinsiza ezimbalwa zokukusiza ukuthi usebenzise kahle ipulatifomu ye-Pro.

Wamukelekile ku:

- hlola [umhlahlandlela wethu womsebenzisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) - sikumema ukuthi ulande i-PDF ukuze izixhumanisi zichofozeke, okuzokwenza kube lula ukuzulazula kwakho kudokhumenti.

- bheka [izifundo zethu zevidiyo](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55)
