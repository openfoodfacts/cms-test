## Ingabe kukhona izincomo noma kuphi kumadokhumenti mayelana nokuthi usayizi ongakanani wezithombe ezilayishiwe?

Lokho kungancika emazweni, uma inethiwekhi ihamba kancane noma ibiza kakhulu. noma yini engaphezu kwamaphikseli angu-5000 ngesisindo noma ukuphakama cishe ayisizi ngalutho. futhi uma ungabona ngandlela thile ukuthi inethiwekhi ihamba kancane, khona-ke ngisho nesithombe samaphikseli angu-2000 singaba sihle (ngokuqinisekile kungcono kunokungabi nesithombe!)

## Kuthiwani ngokudla okungenawo ama-barcode?

I-Open Food Facts iqukethe ulwazi mayelana nokudla okupakishiwe kuphela. Ukuze uthole amanani ajwayelekile omkhiqizo (isibonelo, utamatisi noma ubhanana) kanye neminye imikhiqizo yokudla, ungasebenzisa enye yezindawo ezisemthethweni zolwazi lokudla okunempilo zikazwelonke esikhundleni salokho.

**Qaphela:** Uhlu olungezansi luqukethe ezinye zezindawo zokugcina ukudla ezibaluleke kakhulu kuzwelonke. Uma ucabanga ukuthi kufanele kufakwe enye i-database ohlwini, sicela usithinte ku: [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact)

## Ngingakwazi yini ukusesha igama lomkhiqizo eliqondile nge-API?

Ngeshwa akukakwazi ukusesha kalula igama lomkhiqizo kuphela nangokuqondile nge-API.

Ukusebenzisa isihlungi esigabeni kungakusiza ukuthi usesho lwakho luqonde kakhudlwana.

## Ngingayithola/ngiyiqoqa kanjani idatha yamaphrojekthi ami?

Ekhasini eliyinhloko le-Open Food Facts, ekhoneni eliphezulu kwesobunxele kwesikrini, kukhona imenyu yokuskrola. Ngaphansi kwayo, uzothola inketho ethi “ukusesha okuthuthukisiwe”, ongachofoza kuyo. Kukuwe-ke ukuthi unqume ukuthi yiziphi izindlela ezifanele kakhulu kuphrojekthi yakho. Uma usukhethiwe, uzokwazi ukulanda imiphumela etholakele ngokuskrola phansi phansi kwekhasi bese uchofoza ku-“Landa imiphumela”.

Ungabonisana futhi:

- Imibhalo yethu ye-API:[ https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/)
- Imigomo Yokusebenzisa Yamaqiniso Okudla Avulekile:[ ](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)
- Kudatha yethu:[ https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data)

## Ingabe kukhona izimo zokusebenzisa i-API?

Yonke imibhalo ephathelene nokusetshenziswa kwe-API ingatholakala ku-[ikhasi lemibhalo ye-API](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/), kodwa nansi isifinyezo esisheshayo:

- Isizindalwazi se-Open Food Facts siyatholakala njengedatha evulekile ngaphansi kwe-Open Database License (ODbL), bheka [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use) ukuthola imininingwane yezomthetho. Lezi zimo ezimbili ziwukwabiwa kanye nokwabelana ngokufanayo. Uma uhlanganisa idatha evela ku-Open Food Facts nezinye izizindalwazi, khona-ke i-ODbL idinga ukuthi izizindalwazi eziphumayo kumele zikhishwe njengedatha evulekile. Kusho futhi ukuthi ungahlanganisa idatha kuphela nemithombo engavumela ukusatshalaliswa kabusha okunjalo.

- Kumelwe usebenzise i-\*\*\* User-Agent yangokwezifiso uma wenza izingcingo ze-API ukuze ukhombe uhlelo lwakho lokusebenza.

- Imikhawulo yesilinganiso iyaphoqelelwa ku-endpoint ngayinye ye-API.

## Ngingayithola kanjani idatha yomlando?

Okwamanje, asihlinzeki ngokulahla idatha yomlando (i-JSONL, i-MongoDB, i-CSV).

Noma kunjalo, ngemikhiqizo ngayinye, kungenzeka ukufinyelela izinguqulo zangaphambilini zedatha yomkhiqizo usebenzisa i-API noma ekhasini lomkhiqizo usebenzisa izibuyekezo.

Njalo uma umkhiqizo ubuyekezwa, kudalwa isibuyekezo esisha (inombolo ekhulayo kusukela ku-1).

Isibonelo, ukuthola inguqulo yokuqala (=inguqulo yokuqala yomkhiqizo) yalo mkhiqizo, sebenzisa

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/product/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/product/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1).

Ngokufanayo, ipharamitha ye-rev ingasetshenziswa ne-API:

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/api/v2/product/7623186089763?rev=1](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/api/v2/product/7623186089763?rev=1)
