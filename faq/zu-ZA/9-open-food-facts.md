## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Cishe kuzo zonke izimo, imigomo yabo yesevisi ikwenqabela ngokusobala noma yikuphi ukusetshenziswa okungekona okomuntu siqu kanye nanoma yikuphi ukukhishwa kwayo yonke idatha noma izingxenye zedatha.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Ngakho-ke lapho siqala iphrojekthi ye-Open Food Facts kanye nohlelo lokusebenza lweselula, emuva ngo-2012, sanquma ukwenza okuphambene nalokho: asigcini nje ngokuvumela ukusetshenziswa nokusetshenziswa kabusha kwesizindalwazi sethu, ngokukhululeka nangaphandle kwenkokhelo, kuwo wonke umuntu nakuzo zonke izindlela zokusebenzisa (kufaka phakathi ezohwebo), kodwa futhi siyakukhuthaza!

Ukwenza idatha itholakale emphakathini (okwaziwa ngokuthi _idatha evulekile_) kuvumela abantu ngabanye, izinhlangano, izinkampani, abacwaningi njll. abavela emhlabeni wonke ukuthi bacabange futhi bathuthukise izinhlelo zokusebenza zedatha ebesingeke sicabange ngazo.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (bheka [Imigomo yokusetshenziswa kabusha](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Wonke umuntu (kufaka phakathi kodwa kungagcini lapho kubanikeli nabadali be-Open Food Facts) angaphinde asabalalise futhi/noma asebenzise kabusha idatha ukwakha amawebhusayithi, izinsizakalo, isofthiwe, izinhlelo zokusebenza zeselula, noma ukubhala izihloko nezifundo. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Isibonelo, i-Platform yethu Yabakhiqizi imahhala ngokuphelele, futhi sijabule kakhulu ngalokho ngoba sicabanga ukuthi kungabasiza ukuthuthukisa imikhiqizo yabo.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Ulwazi kanye nedatha kuthunyelwa ngabanikeli be-Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. isib. uma amaqiniso okudla okunempilo komkhiqizo ehluke kakhulu emikhiqizweni yesigaba esifanayo, kungaba yiphutha. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Uma ungumkhiqizi, sicela [uxhumane nathi](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (bheka [Imigomo yomnikelo](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Sithuthukise ipulatifomu yamahhala yokukuvumela ukuthi ungenise kalula izithombe nedatha mayelana nemikhiqizo yakho, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi ungakanani usayizi wakho. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Xhumana nathi ukuze sixoxe ngokuthi singaqhubeka kanjani: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
