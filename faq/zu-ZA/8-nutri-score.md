## Yini okufanele ngiyenze uma i-Nutri-Score yemikhiqizo yami ingalungile?

Uma kwenzeka inkinga nge-Nutri-Score yakho, hlola ukuthi ulwazi lwakho lulungile kuthebhu ethi “shintsha” yeshidi lomkhiqizo wakho. Uma kukhona ulwazi olungekho noma olungalungile, ungangabazi ukuxhumana nathi ku-\*\* producers@openfoodfacts.org\*\*

---

## Hlobo luni lolwazi okufanele ngilunikeze ukuze i-Nutri-Score ibalwe ngokunembe ngangokunokwenzeka?

Ukubalwa kwe-Nutri-Score kuzokwenziwa ngethebula lokudla okunempilo lomkhiqizo wakho.

Ungakhohlwa ukufaka iphesenti lezithelo, imifino namantongomane etafuleni lakho, ukuze i-Nutri-Score ebaliwe ingabi yiphutha.

---

## Ivelaphi i-Nutri-Score?

I-Nutri-Score iphethwe yi-Santé Publique France. Ifingqa uhlu lwezindinganiso zokudla okunempilo ukuze wonke umuntu akwazi ukukuqonda. Umgomo uwukunikeza wonke umuntu ukufinyelela kolwazi olucacile noluqondakalayo lokudla okunempilo.
