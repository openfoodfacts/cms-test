Ulwazi lwentengo lubaluleke kakhulu ekuqondeni izinhlelo zokudla. Kuyisici esibalulekile ekuqondeni izindleko zokudla nokukhuthaza ukudla okunempilo. Idatha yentengo yokuvula iyindlela yokwenza kube lula kubacwaningi, izintatheli, kanye nezakhamuzi ukuqonda kangcono ukuthi amanani okudla ahluka kanjani ngokwendawo nangesikhathi.
