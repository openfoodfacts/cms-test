- thola igama elisemthethweni laleli gama ku-taxonomy yamalebula (yinto yokuqala ohlwini lwamagama afanayo amalebula, isib. en:100% yemifino)
- thola ilogo esezingeni elihle: gwema ukusebenzisa isithombe somnikeli esingafaneleki kuleli cala; amalebula amaningi anama-logo asemthethweni ekhwalithi ephezulu, ngezinye izikhathi ngefomethi ye-vector (okungcono nakakhulu kithi); uma nje sisebenzisa ilogo ukwazisa ngobuqotho ngokuba khona kwelebula
  ekupakishweni komkhiqizo, asikho isidingo sokucela imvume.
