## Ngingumthengisi futhi ngifuna ukufaka amanani. Ngingakwenza kanjani lokho?

Unganikela ngamanani ngokusebenzisa i-[API yethu](https://prices.openfoodfacts.org/api/docs). Uma ufuna ukufaka amanani ngezinga elithile, sicela uxhumane nathi ku-[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org).

---

## Ingabe ucabanga ngokususa amanani kumawebhusayithi abathengisi?

Ngenxa yezizathu zomthetho nezobuchwepheshe, asikubheki ukususa amanani kumawebhusayithi abathengisi njengendlela evumelekile yokufaka isandla kumanani avulekile. Sifuna ukuqinisekisa ukuthi amanani esiwaqoqayo anembile futhi asesikhathini, futhi ukuthola amanani ahlanziwe kubanikeli akusivumeli ukwenza lokho.

Ukuklwebha amanani kuyindlela ecatshangelwayo enguqulweni yesikhathi esizayo ye-Open Prices, kodwa kuzokwenziwa yi-Open Prices uqobo ukuze sibe nobufakazi bentengo ngokusekelwe ekhasini le-HTML.

---

## Kungani i-Open Food Facts yenza lokhu (Amanani Avulekile)?

Ulwazi lwentengo lubaluleke kakhulu ekuqondeni izinhlelo zokudla. Kuyisici esibalulekile ekuqondeni izindleko zokudla nokukhuthaza ukudla okunempilo. Idatha yentengo yokuvula iyindlela yokwenza kube lula kubacwaningi, izintatheli, kanye nezakhamuzi ukuqonda kangcono ukuthi amanani okudla ahluka kanjani ngokwendawo nangesikhathi.
