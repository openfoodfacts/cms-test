## Ingabe ikhona indlela yokususa izithombe ezilayishiwe zemikhiqizo?

Abaqondisi kuphela abangasusa izithombe, ukuze bagweme ukucekelwa phansi okungenzeka.

Mane ucele ku-Slack noma ku-**contact@openfoodfacts.org** ukuthi ususe izithombe zakho eziphindaphindiwe noma izithombe ezingafanele (kufanele uzame ukunikeza inombolo yebhakhodi noma i-URL).

Siphinde sibe ne-API entsha yombiko wesithombe uma ungumhleli wezinhlelo.

---

## Ngingumklami. Ngingasiza kanjani?

Sihlela yonke imisebenzi ehlobene nokuklama ku-[https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-design](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-design) kanye nasesiteshini sengxoxo esizinikele. Sivame ukwenza imihlangano yeqembu kanye nokuxoxisana ngezinselelo ezithile.

---

## Kwezinye izimo umkhiqizo ofanayo ungaba namanani ahlukene okudla okunempilo ezweni ngalinye, lokhu kuphathwa kanjani ku-Open Food Facts?

99% wesikhathi, abakhiqizi bazokwakha amabhakhodi ahlukene ezinhlotsheni ezahlukene zemikhiqizo yabo. Isibonelo esidumile umehluko phakathi kweNutella yesiFulentshi nesiJalimane ngokwezinga lokujiya, ngenxa yomehluko wesinkwa emazweni ahlukahlukene. Amafomula amabili ahlukene, amabhakhodi amabili ahlukene.

Nokho, ukungqubuzana kwebhakhodi kungenzeka kumakhodi amafushane (i-EAN-8) avame ukusetshenziswa kabusha yizitolo ezithile kulo lonke elaseYurophu nase-US. Okwamanje asixazululi lokho kungqubuzana kwebhakhodi, kodwa kufanele kwenzeke ukwenza kanjalo ngokuthola indawo evamile yomsebenzisi (akuvamile kakhulu ukuba nokungqubuzana kwebhakhodi ezweni).

Esikhathini eside, sikhuthaza abakhiqizi ukuthi bathuthele ku-EAN-13 ukuze bagweme lokho kungqubuzana kwamabhakhodi.
