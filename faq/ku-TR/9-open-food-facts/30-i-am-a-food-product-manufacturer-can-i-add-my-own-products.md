Erê! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (Li Mercên Beşdariyê binêre)

Me platformek belaş pêşxistiye da ku hûn bikaribin bi hêsanî wêne û daneyên li ser hilberên xwe, bêyî ku mezinahiya we çi be, îtxal bikin. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Ji bo nîqaşkirina ka em çawa dikarin pêşve biçin, bi me re têkilî daynin: producers@openfoodfacts.org
