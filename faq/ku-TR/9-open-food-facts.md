## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Di hema bêje hemû rewşan de, şert û mercên xizmeta wan bi eşkereyî her karanîna ne-kesane û her derxistina hemû daneyan an jî beşên daneyan qedexe dikin.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Ji ber vê yekê, dema ku me di sala 2012an de projeya Open Food Facts û sepana mobîl dest pê kir, me biryar da ku tam berevajî vê yekê bikin: ne tenê em destûrê didin bikaranîn û ji nû ve bikaranîna databasa me, bi awayekî azad û bêpere, ji bo her kesî û ji bo hemî karan (tevî bazirganî), lê di heman demê de em wê teşwîq dikin!

Berdestkirina daneyan bo raya giştî (tiştê ku wekî _daneyên vekirî_) tê zanîn, rê dide kes, komele, şîrket, lêkolîner û hwd. ji çar aliyên cîhanê ku ji bo daneyan serlêdanan bifikirin û pêşve bibin ku me qet nefikirîbû.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (li [Mercên ji nû ve bikaranînê] binêre(https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Her kes (tevî beşdar û afirînerên Open Food Facts lê ne bi tenê) dikare daneyan ji nû ve belav bike û/an jî ji nû ve bikar bîne da ku malper, xizmet, nermalav, sepanên mobîl ava bike, an jî gotar û lêkolînan binivîse. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Mînakî, Platforma me ya ji bo Hilberîneran bi tevahî belaş e, û em ji vê yekê hîn bêtir kêfxweş in ji ber ku em difikirin ku ew dikare ji wan re bibe alîkar ku hilberên xwe baştir bikin.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

Ji kerema xwe not bikin: [Open Food Facts databeseke hevkar a berhemên xwarinê ye](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/discover), em ti berheman nafiroşin û girêdanên me bi hilberînerên wan re tune ne. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Agahdarî û daneyên ji hêla beşdarên Open Food Facts ve têne pêşkêş kirin. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. mînak, eger daneyên xurekiyê yên berhemekê ji berhemên heman kategoriyê pir cuda bin, dibe ku xeletîyek be. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Heke hûn hilberîner in, ji kerema xwe [bi me re têkilî daynin](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Erê! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (li [Mercên Beşdariyê](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution) binêre)

Me platformek belaş pêşxistiye da ku hûn bikaribin bi hêsanî wêne û daneyên li ser hilberên xwe, bêyî ku mezinahiya we çi be, îtxal bikin. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Ji bo nîqaşkirina ka em çawa dikarin pêşve biçin, bi me re têkilî daynin: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
