Me ji bo kozmetîkan, ji bo xwarina heywanan, û her weha ji bo hilberên din projeyên taybetî çêkirine: [Open Beauty Facts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Open Pet Food Facts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) û [Open Products Facts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/)bi rêzê ve.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
