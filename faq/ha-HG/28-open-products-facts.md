## Yadda ake samun damar bayanai game da Buɗewar Kayayyaki akan iPhone?

Ana samun Bayanan Kayayyakin Buɗewa a cikin babban manhajar Buɗe Bayanan Abinci. Wannan haɗin kai yana bawa masu amfani da iPhone damar shiga dukkan bayanan ta hanyar aikace-aikacen guda ɗaya mai sauƙi.

---

## Ina so in goyi bayan Buɗe Bayanan Kayayyaki. Men zan iya yi ?

Da fatan za a karanta shafinmu na wiki, mun lissafa hanyoyin shiga: [https://wiki.openfoodfacts.org/Open_Products_Facts](https://wiki.openfoodfacts.org/Open_Products_Facts)

Don tallafawa Buɗaɗɗen Bayanan Kayayyaki, zaku iya la'akari da zaɓuɓɓuka masu zuwa, kowannensu an tsara shi don nau'ikan gudummawa daban-daban:

-

**Bayanan Masu Tallafawa Masu Taimakon Agaji/Jiha don Buɗaɗɗen Kayayyaki**:
Gudummawar taimakon agaji suna da mahimmanci wajen cimma burin cikakken dandamalin bayanai na zagaye, kimanta samfura, da tattara mafita na zagaye, a cikin fiye da nau'ikan 20000+, babban burin Bayanan Kayayyaki na Buɗaɗɗen Kayayyaki. Ba a ba da kuɗaɗen muhimman sassa na wannan hangen nesa ba tukuna, kuma tallafin kuɗin ku na iya yin babban tasiri. Gudummawar ku na iya taimakawa wajen samar da muhimman ci gaba da kayayyakin more rayuwa. Domin bayar da gudummawa ta kuɗi, tuntuɓi contact@openfoodfacts.org

-

**Masu Ba da Gudummawa ga Bayanai Ta Amfani da Manhajar Bayanan Kayayyakin Buɗe**:
Gudummawar bayanai suna da mahimmanci don haɓaka Bayanan Kayayyakin Buɗe. Ta hanyar amfani da manhajar Android don ƙara sabbin bayanai game da samfura, sabunta shigarwar da ke akwai, ko tabbatar da bayanai, kuna taimakawa wajen faɗaɗa da kuma inganta bayanan. Wannan haɗin gwiwa yana ba da gudummawa wajen gina ingantaccen tushe mai inganci ga kowa.

-

**Masu Ba da Gudummawa ga Fasaha don Bayyanar Samfuran Buɗaɗɗe**:
Idan kuna da ƙwarewar fasaha a fannoni kamar shirye-shirye, nazarin bayanai, ko ƙirar ƙwarewar mai amfani, ƙwarewar ku na iya amfanar da Bayanan Samfuran Buɗaɗɗe sosai. Shiga cikin haɓaka manhajar, inganta ayyukanta, ko haɓaka hanyoyin sadarwa na masu amfani gudummawa ne masu mahimmanci. Domin shiga, shiga tashar #openproductsfacts Slack, inda al'ummar Open Products Facts ke haɗin gwiwa. Za ku iya samun hanyar haɗi zuwa wannan wurin aiki na Slack akan gidan yanar gizon Buɗe Bayanan Abinci.

Kowace irin gudummawa tana da mahimmanci ga ci gaba da nasarar Buɗe Bayanan Kayayyaki, wanda ke taimakawa wajen ƙirƙirar dandamali mai ɗorewa da kuma bayanai ga kowa.

## A ina zan iya samun API ɗin Buɗe Bayanan Kayayyaki?

API ɗin Buɗaɗɗen Bayanan Kayayyaki yana da kashi 98% daidai da API ɗin Buɗaɗɗen Bayanan Kayayyaki don haka idan kuna amfani da takaddun API na Buɗaɗɗen Bayanan Kayayyaki, ya kamata ku sami cikakken ra'ayi game da API ɗin Buɗaɗɗen Bayanan Kayayyaki. Mun kuma rubuta kwas ɗin koyarwa don ba da damar aiwatar da scan na duniya a cikin app ɗinku.
