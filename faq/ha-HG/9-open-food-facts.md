## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. A kusan dukkan lokuta, sharuɗɗan aikinsu sun haramta duk wani amfani da ba na mutum ba da kuma cire duk wani bayani ko sassan bayanan.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Don haka lokacin da muka fara aikin Open Food Facts da manhajar wayar hannu, a shekarar 2012, mun yanke shawarar yin akasin haka: ba wai kawai muna ba da damar amfani da kuma sake amfani da bayananmu ba, kyauta kuma ba tare da kuɗi ba, ga kowa da kowa da kuma ga dukkan amfani (har da na kasuwanci), amma muna kuma ƙarfafa hakan!

Samar da bayanai a bainar jama'a (abin da aka sani da _buɗaɗɗen bayanai_) yana bawa mutane, ƙungiyoyi, kamfanoni, masu bincike da sauransu daga ko'ina cikin duniya damar yin tunani da haɓaka aikace-aikacen bayanan da ba za mu taɓa tunani a kansu ba.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (duba [Sharuɗɗan sake amfani](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Kowa (gami da amma ba'a iyakance ga masu ba da gudummawa da masu ƙirƙirar Buɗaɗɗen Abinci ba) don haka zai iya sake rarrabawa da/ko sake amfani da bayanan don gina gidajen yanar gizo, ayyuka, software, aikace-aikacen wayar hannu, ko don rubuta labarai da nazari. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Misali, Dandalinmu na Masu Shiryawa kyauta ne gaba ɗaya, kuma mun fi gamsuwa da hakan domin muna ganin zai iya taimaka musu wajen inganta kayayyakinsu.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Masu ba da gudummawa ga Buɗaɗɗen Abinci ne suka gabatar da bayanan da bayanai. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. misali idan bayanin abinci mai gina jiki na wani samfuri ya bambanta sosai da samfuran da ke cikin rukuni ɗaya, yana iya zama kuskure. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Idan kai furodusa ne, da fatan za a [tuntube mu](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (duba [Sharuɗɗan gudummawa](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Mun ƙirƙiri wani dandamali kyauta don ba ku damar shigo da hotuna da bayanai game da samfuran ku cikin sauƙi, ba tare da la'akari da girman ku ba. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Tuntube mu domin tattauna yadda za mu ci gaba: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
