## Za a iya haɗa asusun Buɗe Abinci da yawa zuwa kamfani ɗaya?

Eh, don Allah a aika imel zuwa [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:support@openfoodfacts.org), don mu iya haɗa dukkan asusun zuwa kamfani ɗaya.

---

## Yadda zan mayar da asusun Buɗe Abinci na zuwa asusun Pro?

Za ka iya shiga [dandalin Pro](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/) tare da takardun shaidar asusunka na yanzu.

Idan kai ƙwararre ne, za ka buƙaci amfani da adireshin imel ɗinka na ƙwararru domin mu iya tabbatar da asusunka.

→ Domin ƙarin bayani, muna gayyatarku da ku sauke [jagorar mai amfani](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) da/ko ku duba [koyarwar bidiyo](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

---

## A ina zan iya samun takardu don taimaka mini amfani da dandamalin Pro?

Akwai wasu albarkatu da za su taimaka muku cin gajiyar dandamalin Pro.

Barka da zuwa:

- duba [jagorar mai amfani](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) - muna gayyatarku da ku sauke PDF ɗin don hanyoyin haɗin su kasance masu dannawa, wanda zai sauƙaƙa muku kewayawa ta cikin takardar.

- duba [koyarwar bidiyo](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55)

- raba ra'ayoyi ko shawarwari ta hanyar [wannan fom](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1QSZ_v50Ueoe8JPCqivvcyEdv8AnsuUbrbKQQofSAMFc/edit)
