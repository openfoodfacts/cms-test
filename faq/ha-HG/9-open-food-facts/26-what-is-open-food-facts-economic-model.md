We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Misali, Dandalinmu na Masu Shiryawa kyauta ne gaba ɗaya, kuma mun fi gamsuwa da hakan domin muna ganin zai iya taimaka musu wajen inganta kayayyakinsu.
