Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (duba Sharuɗɗan gudummawa)

Mun ƙirƙiri wani dandamali kyauta don ba ku damar shigo da hotuna da bayanai game da samfuran ku cikin sauƙi, ba tare da la'akari da girman ku ba. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Tuntube mu don tattauna yadda za mu iya ci gaba: producers@openfoodfacts.org
