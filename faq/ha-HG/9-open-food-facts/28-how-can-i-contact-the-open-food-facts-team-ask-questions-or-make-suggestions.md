Za ku iya yin tambayoyi ko bayar da shawarwari a [forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/) ko ku tuntube mu ta imel:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Idan kai furodusa ne, da fatan za a aiko mana da imel a producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Idan kai ɗan jarida ne, da fatan za a aiko mana da imel a presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Lura: Open Food Facts wani rumbun adana bayanai ne na kayayyakin abinci, ba ma sayar da wani samfuri kuma ba mu da alaƙa da masu samar da su. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
