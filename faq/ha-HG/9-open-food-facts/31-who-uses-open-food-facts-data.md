It would
take too long to list all the organizations that rely on the Open Food Facts
platform.

- Manhajojin wayar hannu sama da 100
  suna magance buƙatun masu sauraro da yawa: ciwon suga (VeryDiab), rashin lafiyan jiki, daidaiton abinci
  ta hanyar fasahar wucin gadi (FoodVisor), nakasa, da ƙari. Bayanan mu na
  sun kuma ba da damar ƙirƙirar Yuka.
- Yanar gizo da yawa: CombienDeSucres.fr,
  CestEmballePresDeChezVous.fr...
- 'Yan jarida na bayanai: Les Décodeurs du Monde, BFM
  TV
- Masu bincike a cikin labarai kusan 100 na kimiyya
- Food banks and solidarity stores: a
  Data For Good project in the continuity of a pilot project initiated by the
  French Red Cross, integration in the cash management of a small food
  cooperative.
- Kamfanoni: Terraillon yana amfani da bayananmu
  don samar da rayuwa ga ma'aunin da aka haɗa.
- Makarantun shirye-shirye (Simplon, Open
  Azuzuwan) sun haɗa Buɗaɗɗen Abinci Facts a matsayin wani aiki mai ƙarfafa gwiwa a cikin manhajar karatunsu ta
  .
