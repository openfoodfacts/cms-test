Unogona kubvunza mibvunzo kana kupa mazano pa[forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)kana kutibata ne email:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Kana uri mugadziri wezvigadzirwa, ndapota titumirei email pa producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Kana uri mutori wenhau, ndapota titumirei email pa presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Ndokumbirawo mucherechedze: Open Food Facts idhatabhesi yezvigadzirwa zvekudya zvakabatana, hatitengese chero chigadzirwa uye hatina malink evanogadzira chikafu. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
