Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (ona Mitemo yekupa)

Takagadzira puratifomu yemahara yekukubvumidza kupinza mapikicha neruzivo nezvezvigadzirwa zvako zviri nyore, zvisinei nehukuru hwako. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Tibatei kuti tikurukure kuti tingaenderera mberi sei: producers@openfoodfacts.org
