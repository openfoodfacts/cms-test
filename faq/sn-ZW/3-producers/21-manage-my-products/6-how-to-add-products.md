Kuti uwedzere zvigadzirwa papuratifomu yedu, unofanira kutanga wasarudza kuti unoda kuenderera mberi sei. Kune sarudzo ina:

- Nemaoko: kana uine zvigadzirwa zvishoma, zvichiri kukwanisika kugadzira bepa rega rega rechigadzirwa nemaoko paPro Platform.

- Kuburikidza nekutumira spreadsheet: unogona kudhawunirodha template yespreadsheet yakapihwa neOpen Food Facts, kana kupinza zvakananga chinhu chakatorwa kubva kuIT system yako kuenda kuPro platform.

- Kuburikidza nezvinobatanidza zvemakatalogu ezvigadzirwa. Kana uri mutengi kuEQUADIS, AGENA3000 kana BAYARD, unogona kuisa data muOpen Food Facts nekudzvanya kamwe chete. Kana muchishanda neimwe PIM (Product Information Management), ndapota taurai nesu pa: producers@openfoodfacts.org

- Kuburikidza nekubatana kwakagadzirirwa kambani yako neOpen Food Facts. Kana ukaverenga zvigadzirwa zvinopfuura chiuru mu portfolio yako, tinogona kukurukura nezvemukana wekuvaka chinongedzo chaicho kuti zvive nyore kutamisa data. Kuti uwane rumwe ruzivo, tinokukoka kuti udhawunirodhe gwara redu rekushandisa uye/kana kutarisa mavhidhiyo edu ekudzidzisa :-)
