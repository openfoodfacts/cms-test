Kurongeka kwezvinhu**kwakakosha**: kunomiririra kurongeka kwehuwandu.
Nzira yakanakisisa yekuzadza zvinhu ndeyekutevera chaizvo zvakanyorwa papakeji. Ndosaka zvichikoshawo kuti mifananidzo yezvinhu zvirimo idhindwe.
