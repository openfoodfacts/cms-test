## Chii chinovimbisa kunaka kwedata riri muOpen Food Facts?

**Kuti tirambe tichivandudza mhando yedata tinovimba nezvikamu zvina:**

1. Nharaunda iri kugara ichiwedzera ruzivo nemifananidzo uye inzvimbo chaiyo inoongorora mafambiro enyaya.

2. Takaisa mitemo yekufunga kuti tione zvikanganiso zviri pamapepa ezvigadzirwa.Semuenzaniso: kana huwandu hwehuremu hwezvinhu zviri mukati hwakakura kupfuura huremu hwese hwechigadzirwa, pane chimwe chinhu chisina kujairika. Tine mamwe macheki emhando yepamusoro angangoita makumi mashanu.

3. Nekuda kweMachine Learning, tinoderedza njodzi yekukanganisa kwekuisa zvinhu. Tekinoroji yedu inotibvumira kutora ruzivo rwemashoko kubva mumifananidzo yakatorwa nevanopa rubatsiro

4. Nerutsigiro rweSanté Publique France, takagadzira nzvimbo inobvumira vagadziri kuisa data ravo rakagadzirwa nevanhu vakawanda uye nokudaro kugadzirisa/kupedzisa mipiro yenharaunda.

## Ungawedzera sei zvigadzirwa?

Kuti uwedzere zvigadzirwa papuratifomu yedu, unofanira kutanga wasarudza kuti unoda kuenderera mberi sei. Kune sarudzo ina:

- **Nemaoko**: kana uine zvigadzirwa zvishoma, zvichiri kukwanisika kugadzira bepa rega rega rechigadzirwa nemaoko paPro Platform.

- **Kuburikidza nekuisa spreadsheet**: unogona kudhawunirodha spreadsheet [template](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) yakapihwa neOpen Food Facts, kana kupinza zvakananga chinhu chakatorwa kubva kuIT system yako kuenda kuPro platform.

- **Kuburikidza nezvinobatanidza zvemakatalogu ezvigadzirwa**. Kana uri mutengi kuEQUADIS, AGENA3000 kana BAYARD, unogona kuisa data muOpen Food Facts nekudzvanya kamwe chete. Kana muchishanda neimwe PIM (Product Information Management), ndapota taurai nesu pa: producers@openfoodfacts.org

- **Kuburikidza nekubatana kwakagadzirirwa kambani yako** neOpen Food Facts. Kana ukaverenga zvigadzirwa zvinopfuura chiuru muportfolio yako, tinogona kukurukura nezvemukana wekuvaka chinongedzo chaicho kuti zvive nyore kutamisa data.

Kuti uwane rumwe ruzivo, tinokukoka kuti udhawunirodhe [guide yedu yekushandisa](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) uye/kana kuti utarise [video tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Is it possible to delete products that are no longer sold?

Kana uchida kubvisa chigadzirwa chisisipo pamusika, unongofanira kutarisa bhokisi rinoti "chigadzirwa ichi hachichatengeswi" paunogadzirisa bepa rako rechigadzirwa.

Kana uchida kubvisa zvigadzirwa zvakawanda, tinokurudzira kuti utevedzere maitiro ataurwa mu [guide user](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Platform-User-Guide.pdf), papeji 18.

Munogonawo kutibata kuburikidza ne producers@openfoodfacts.org kana muine mibvunzo.

Kana chigadzirwa chacho chachengetwa, hachizoonekwizve muOpen Food Facts.Zvisinei, zvicharamba zviri mudura redu kuitira kuti tikwanise kuchengetedza nhoroondo yezvigadzirwa zvinotengeswa nekufamba kwenguva.

## Ko ruzivo rwekambani runotora nzvimbo yekutanga pane ruzivo rwatovepo papuratifomu here?

Kana mugadziri akaisa chimwe chezvigadzirwa zvake online neakaundi yake yemugadziri uye pepa rezvigadzirwa ratovapo, izvi zvinopedzisa.

Ruzivo rwuchatumirwa nemugadziri ruchagara rwuri rwekutanga uye iye ega ndiye achakwanisa kugadzirisa zvigadzirwa zvake kana ruzivo rwazadzwa.

Kana data rinotumirwa kuburikidza nepuratifomu rakapatsanurwa, ruzivo rwakawedzerwa nenharaunda kubva mupakeji runogona kuripedzisa.

## Ndinofanira kutumira data rangu muchimiro chipi?

[template](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) inopihwa neOpen Food Facts.

Zvisinei, chero fomati yespredishiti (xlsx, csv) inogona kutumirwa mupuratifomu.  
Maitiro edu ekugadzirisa zvinhu akagadzirirwa kuenderana otomatiki nemaitiro ari mufaira rako, neari muOpen Food Facts. Chenjerera kuti ugare uchitarisa kaviri nhanho yekufananidza sezvo ungangoda kugadzirisa zvimwe zvacho nemaoko.

Kana ruzivo rwako rwakakamurwa pakati pemaspredishiti akati wandei, unogona kuapinza akatevedzana - uchiva nechokwadi chekuti waenzanisa kana fomati yacho ikachinja.

Unogona zvakare kupinza faira reExcel pamusoro peEQUADIS kana AGENA3000 import, kana data racho risati rave musystem yako yeruzivo. Muzviitiko izvi, musakanganwa kukumbira kutumirwa kune database yeruzhinji kubva pane interface.

→ Kuti uwane rumwe ruzivo, tinokukoka kuti udhawunirodhe [guide yedu yekushandisa](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) uye/kana kuti utarise [video tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Makatarogu ezvigadzirwa api awakabatanidza?

Vagadziri vanogona kutumira data rezvigadzirwa zvavo nemifananidzo panguva chaiyo kubva ku **Equadis,** **AGENA3000 uye BAYARD consulting** kuenda kuOpen Food Facts.

Ehezve uchabatsirwawo nemazano ekugadzirisa puratifomu pamwe chete nezvose zviri papuratifomu iyi.

[Chinyorwa chedu cheblog](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/real-time-product-data-from-producers-on-open-food-facts-thanks-to-the-new-equadis-integration) chinotsanangura matanho ekutevera kana uri mutengi weEQUADIS.

Kana uri mutengi weAGENA3000 (Product A3 PIM INDUSTRY), unongofanira kusarudza "Open Food Facts" paunotumira mapepa ezvigadzirwa zvako. [Mamwe mashoko pamusoro pechinyorwa ichi cheblog](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/share-your-product-data-in-1-click-with-the-new-agena3000-connector).

Kana uri mutengi weimwe katarogu yedata rezvigadzirwa (Salsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, nezvimwewo) Ndapota tizivisei pa [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

→ Kuti uwane rumwe ruzivo, tinokukoka kuti udhawunirodhe [guide yedu yekushandisa](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) uye/kana kuti utarise [video tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Ruzivo rwezvigadzirwa kubva kukambani yangu runowanikwa paOpen Food Facts. Zvinokwanisika here kutora kutonga kweakaundi yemugadziri yakabatana?

Ehe saizvozvo! Unogona kudzora account yemugadziri webhizinesi rako nekugadzira account yeOpen Food Facts. Paunonyoresa, taura zita resangano riripo pafomu kuti ukwanise kuwana nzvimbo yakakodzera yemugadziri.

Danho pfupi rekusimbisa richave rinodiwa kuti uve nechokwadi chekuti ndiwe mugadziri wechokwadi.

Uchakwanisa kupedzisa data, kuwedzera mifananidzo, kuwana mazano otomatiki ekuvandudza Nutri-Score, nezvimwe zvakawanda. Zvese izvi zvechokwadi hazvina muripo zvachose. Unogona zvakare kubatanidza sisitimu yekutarisira data rezvigadzirwa yebato rechitatu senge EQUADIS, AGENA3000 uye Bayard consulting.

→ Kuti uwane rumwe ruzivo, tinokukoka kuti udhawunirodhe [guide yedu yekushandisa](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) uye/kana kuti utarise [video tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Kupinza data kubva kuchinyorwa chechigadzirwa kunopedzisa here kana kuti kunobvisa chinyorwa chechigadzirwa chiripo?

Isu tinoshandisa barcode kuziva zvigadzirwa, kana ukatumira rekodhi ine barcode yakafanana nerekodhi iripo, ruzivo rwacho ruchabatanidzwa.

Padata rakadai serondedzero yezvinhu zvinoshandiswa, kukosha kwezvekudya (mutengo mumwe chete wakarurama unogoneka), data rinotumirwa kuburikidza nepuratifomu yemugadziri richabvisa data riripo.

Padata rakadai semazita/mapoka/mabrandi (kana paine kukosha kwakaringana), ruzivo rwacho runosanganiswa. Kana data risina kururama papuratifomu yeruzhinji, unogona kugadzirisa peji rechigadzirwa papuratifomu yeruzhinji kuti ubvise kukosha kusina kururama.

→ Kuti uwane rumwe ruzivo, tinokukoka kuti udhawunirodhe [guide yedu yekushandisa](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) uye/kana kuti utarise [video tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)
