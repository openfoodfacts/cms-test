我們分別針對化妝品、動物飼料以及其他產品創建了專門的項目：[打開美容資訊](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/)、[打開寵物食品資訊](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)和[打開產品資訊](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/)。
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
