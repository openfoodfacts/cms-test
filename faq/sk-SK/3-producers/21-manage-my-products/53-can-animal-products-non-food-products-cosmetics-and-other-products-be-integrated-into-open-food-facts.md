Vytvorili sme špecifické projekty pre kozmetiku, krmivo pre zvieratá, ako aj pre ďalšie produkty: [Open Beauty Facts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Open Pet Food Facts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) a [Open Products Facts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/).
