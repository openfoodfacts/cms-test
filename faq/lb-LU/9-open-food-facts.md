## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. A bal alle Fäll verbidden hir Benotzungsbedingunge explizit all net-perséinlech Notzung an all Extraktioun vun allen Daten oder Deeler vun den Daten.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Wéi mir also 2012 den Open Food Facts Projet an d'mobil App gestart hunn, hu mir decidéiert genau dat Géigendeel ze maachen: mir erlaben net nëmmen d'Benotzung an d'Wiederverwendung vun eiser Datebank, fräi an ouni Käschten, fir jiddereen a fir all Zwecker (och kommerziell), mee mir encouragéieren et och!

D'ëffentlech Zougänglechkeet vun den Donnéeën (sougenannte _Open Data_) erméiglecht et Eenzelpersounen, Associatiounen, Firmen, Fuerscher etc. aus der ganzer Welt, Uwendungen fir d'Donnéeën auszedenken an z'entwéckelen, un déi mir sécherlech ni geduecht hätten.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (kuckt d'[Konditioune vun der Wiederverwendung](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Jiddereen (och d'Mataarbechter an d'Creatoren vun Open Food Facts) kann d'Donnéeën also weiderverdeelen an/oder nei benotzen, fir Websäiten, Servicer, Software, mobil Applikatiounen ze bauen oder Artikelen a Studien ze schreiwen. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Zum Beispill ass eis Plattform fir Produzenten komplett gratis, a mir si méi frou doriwwer, well mir mengen, datt et hinnen hëllefe kéint, hir Produkter ze verbesseren.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

D'Informatiounen an d'Donnéeë gi vun den Open Food Facts-Mataarbechter geliwwert. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. z.B. wann d'Ernierungsinformatioune vun engem Produkt ganz anescht sinn wéi Produkter aus der selwechter Kategorie, kéint et e Feeler sinn. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Wann Dir e Produzent sidd, kontaktéiert eis w.e.g. (mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (kuckt d'[Konditioune vum Bäitrag](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Mir hunn eng gratis Plattform entwéckelt, fir datt Dir einfach Fotoen an Donnéeën iwwer Är Produkter importéiere kënnt, egal wéi grouss Dir sidd. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Kontaktéiert eis fir ze diskutéieren, wéi mir virgoe kënnen: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
