वयं प्रसाधनसामग्रीणां कृते, पशुभोजनस्य कृते, अन्येषां उत्पादानाम् अपि विशिष्टानि परियोजनानि निर्मितवन्तः:[Open Beauty Facts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/),[Open Pet Food Facts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)तथा[Open Products Facts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/)क्रमशः।
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
