Nou kreye pwojè espesifik pou pwodui kosmetik, pou manje bèt, ansanm ak lòt pwodui: [Louvri Enfòmasyon sou Bote](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Louvri Enfòmasyon sou Manje Bèt Kay](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) ak [Louvri Enfòmasyon sou Pwodui yo](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) respektivman.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
