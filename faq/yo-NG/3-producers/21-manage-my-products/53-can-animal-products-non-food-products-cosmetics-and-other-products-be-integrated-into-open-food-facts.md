A ti ṣẹ̀dá àwọn iṣẹ́ pàtó kan fún ohun ìṣaralóge, fún oúnjẹ ẹranko, àti fún àwọn ọjà mìíràn:[Ìtàn Ẹwà Ṣíṣí](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/),[Ìtàn Oúnjẹ Ẹranko Ṣíṣí](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)àti[Ìtàn Àwọn Ọjà Ṣíṣí](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) lẹ́sẹẹsẹ.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
