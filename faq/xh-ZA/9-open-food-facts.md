## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Phantse kuzo zonke iimeko, imiqathango yabo yenkonzo iyakwalela ngokucacileyo ukusetyenziswa okungekuko okomntu kunye nokukhutshwa kwayo yonke idatha okanye iinxalenye zayo.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Ngoko ke xa saqalisa iprojekthi ye-Open Food Facts kunye ne-app yeselula, ngo-2012, sagqiba ekubeni senze okwahlukileyo koko: asivumeli nje kuphela ukusetyenziswa nokusetyenziswa kwakhona kwesiseko sedatha yethu, ngokukhululekileyo nangaphandle kwentlawulo, kuye wonke umntu nakuzo zonke izinto ezisetyenziswayo (kuquka nezorhwebo), kodwa sikwakhuthaza oko!

Ukwenza idatha ifumaneke esidlangalaleni (oko kwaziwa ngokuba yi-_open data_) kuvumela abantu ngabanye, imibutho, iinkampani, abaphandi njl.njl. abavela kwihlabathi liphela ukuba bacinge baze baphuhlise usetyenziso lwedatha ebesingenakuze siyicinge.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (jonga [Imigaqo yokusetyenziswa kwakhona](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Wonke umntu (kuquka kodwa kungaphelelanga apho abaxhasi kunye nabadali be-Open Food Facts) banokusasaza kwakhona kunye/okanye basebenzise kwakhona idatha ukwakha iiwebhusayithi, iinkonzo, isoftware, usetyenziso lweselfowuni, okanye ukubhala amanqaku kunye nezifundo. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Umzekelo, iPlatform yethu yabavelisi isimahla ngokupheleleyo, kwaye sonwabile ngakumbi yiloo nto kuba sicinga ukuba inokubanceda baphucule iimveliso zabo.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Ulwazi kunye nedatha zingeniswa ngabanikeli be-Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. Umzekelo, ukuba iinkcukacha zesondlo semveliso zahlukile kakhulu kwiimveliso zodidi olufanayo, oko kusenokuba yimpazamo. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Ukuba ungumvelisi, nceda [unxibelelane nathi](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (jonga [Imigaqo yomnikelo](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Siphuhlise iqonga lasimahla lokukuvumela ukuba ungenise ngokulula iifoto kunye nedatha malunga neemveliso zakho, nokuba ungakanani na ubungakanani bakho. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Nxibelelana nathi ukuze sixoxe ngendlela esinokuqhubeka ngayo: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
