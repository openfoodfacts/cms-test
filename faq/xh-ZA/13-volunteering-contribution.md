## Ngaba ikho indlela yokususa imifanekiso elayishiweyo kwiimveliso?

Ngabahloli kuphela abanokususa iifoto, ukuze kuthintelwe ukonakala okunokwenzeka.

Cela nje kuSlack okanye ku **contact@openfoodfacts.org** ukuba ususe iifoto zakho eziphindwe kabini okanye naziphi na iifoto ezingafanelekanga (kufuneka uzame ukunika inombolo yebhakhowudi okanye i-URL ukuze ifumaneke).

Sikwanayo ne-API entsha yengxelo yomfanekiso ukuba ungumhleli weenkqubo.

## Ndingumyili. Ndinganceda njani?

Siququzelela yonke imisebenzi enxulumene noyilo kwi [https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-design](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-design) nakwitshaneli yencoko ezinikeleyo. Sihlala senza iintlanganiso zamaqela kwaye sithetha ngemingeni ethile.

## Kwezinye iimeko imveliso efanayo inokuba nexabiso lesondlo elahlukileyo kwilizwe ngalinye, oku kusingathwa njani kwi-Open Food Facts?

Kwi-99% yexesha, abavelisi baya kudala iibhakhowudi ezahlukeneyo kwiinguqulelo ezahlukeneyo zeemveliso zabo. Umzekelo odumileyo ngumahluko phakathi kweNutella yaseFransi neyaseJamani ngokubhekiselele kubukhulu bayo, ngenxa yomahluko kwisonka kumazwe ngamazwe. Iifomula ezimbini ezahlukeneyo, iibhakhowudi ezimbini ezahlukeneyo.

Nangona kunjalo, ukungqubana kwebhakhowudi kunokwenzeka kwiikhowudi ezimfutshane (i-EAN-8) ezidla ngokusetyenziswa kwakhona ziivenkile ezithile kulo lonke elaseYurophu nase-US. Okwangoku asijongani nokungqubana kweebhakhowudi, kodwa kufanele ukuba kwenzeke oko ngokufumana indawo eqhelekileyo yomsebenzisi (kunqabile nangakumbi ukuba kubekho ukungqubana kweebhakhowudi kwilizwe elithile).

Kwixesha elide, sikhuthaza abavelisi ukuba batshintshele kwi-EAN-13 ukuze baphephe loo ngxabano yeebhakhowudi.
