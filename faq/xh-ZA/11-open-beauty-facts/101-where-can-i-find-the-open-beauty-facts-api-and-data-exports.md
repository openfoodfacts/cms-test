I-Open Food Facts API ifana nge-98% ne-Open Beauty Facts API, ngoko ke ukuba usebenzisa uxwebhu lwe-Open Food Facts API, kuya kufuneka ube nombono ocacileyo we-Open Beauty Facts API.

Qaphela ukuba iimpawu ezifana neeKnowledge Panels, i-Eco-Score, i-Nutri-Score azixhaswa (nokuba kungenxa yesihloko, okanye ngenxa ye-codebase endala).
