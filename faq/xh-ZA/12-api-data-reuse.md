## Ngaba kukho iingcebiso naphi na kumaxwebhu malunga nokuba yeyiphi na isayizi efanelekileyo yeefoto ezilayishiweyo?

Oko kunokuxhomekeka kumazwe, ukuba inethiwekhi icotha okanye ibiza kakhulu. nantoni na engaphezulu kweepikseli ezingama-5000 ngobunzima okanye ukuphakama mhlawumbi ayiluncedo kangako. kwaye ukuba ungayibona ngandlela ithile ukuba inethiwekhi icotha, ngoko nomfanekiso weephikseli ezingama-2000 ubuya kuba mhle (ngokuqinisekileyo ungcono kunokungabi nawo umfanekiso!)

## Kuthekani ngokutya okungenazo iibhakhowudi?

I-Open Food Facts iqulethe ulwazi malunga nokutya okupakishiweyo kuphela. Ukuze ufumane ixabiso eliphakathi lemveliso (umzekelo, iitumato okanye iibhanana) kunye nezinye iimveliso zokutya, ungasebenzisa enye yeedathabheyisi zezondlo zesizwe ezisemthethweni endaweni yoko.

**Qaphela:** Olu luhlu lungezantsi luqulathe ezinye zezona nkcukacha zibalulekileyo zokutya zesizwe. Ukuba ucinga ukuba kufuneka kufakwe enye i-database kuluhlu, nceda unxibelelane nathi ku: [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact)

## Ngaba ndingakwazi ukukhangela igama lemveliso elichanekileyo nge-API?

Ngelishwa akukafumaneki lula ukukhangela igama lemveliso kuphela kwaye ngokuchanekileyo nge-API.

Ukusebenzisa isihluzi kudidi kunokukunceda wenze uphendlo lwakho lube ngokuchanekileyo.

## Ndingayifumana/ndiyiqokelela njani idatha yeeprojekthi zam?

Kwiphepha eliphambili le-Open Food Facts, kwikona ephezulu ngasekhohlo kwesikrini, kukho imenyu yokuskrola. Ezantsi kwayo, uza kufumana ukhetho "lokukhangela okuphambili", onokucofa kulo. Kuxhomekeke kuwe ke ukuba zeziphi iikhrayitheriya ezifanelekileyo kwiprojekthi yakho. Nje ukuba ukhethe, uya kukwazi ukukhuphela iziphumo ozifumeneyo ngokuskrola ezantsi kwephepha uze ucofe ku-“Download results”.

Ungabonisana kwakhona:

- Amaxwebhu ethu e-API:[ https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/)
- Imigaqo Yokusebenzisa Iinyaniso Zokutya Ezivulekileyo:[ ](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)
- Kwidatha yethu:[ https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data)

## Ngaba kukho imiqathango yokusebenzisa i-API?

Onke amaxwebhu malunga nokusetyenziswa kwe-API angafumaneka kwi [API documentation page](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/), kodwa nantsi isishwankathelo esifutshane:

- Isiseko sedatha se-Open Food Facts siyafumaneka njengedatha evulekileyo phantsi kwe-Open Database License (ODbL), bona [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use) ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha zomthetho. Ezi meko zimbini zizinto ezifana nokwabiwa kunye nokwabelana ngokufanayo. Ukuba udibanisa idatha evela kwi-Open Food Facts nezinye ii-database, i-ODbL ifuna ukuba i-database ephumayo ikhutshwe njengedatha evulekileyo. Kwakhona kuthetha ukuba ungadibanisa idatha kuphela nemithombo eya kuvumela ukusasazwa kwakhona okunjalo.

- Kufuneka usebenzise i-\*\*User-Agent rhoqo xa usenza iifowuni ze-API ukuze uchonge i-app yakho.

- Imida yereyithi iyanyanzeliswa kwisiphelo ngasinye se-API.

## Ndingayifumana njani idatha yembali?

Okwangoku, asiboneleli nge-data dump yembali (JSONL, MongoDB, CSV).

Nangona kunjalo, kwiimveliso nganye nganye, kunokwenzeka ukufikelela kwiinguqulelo zangaphambili zedatha yemveliso usebenzisa i-API okanye kwiphepha lemveliso usebenzisa uhlaziyo.

Ngalo lonke ixesha imveliso ihlaziywa, kwenziwa uhlaziyo olutsha (oluqala kwi-1) olunyukayo.

Umzekelo, ukuze ufumane uhlaziyo lokuqala (=uhlobo lokuqala lwemveliso) lwale mveliso, sebenzisa

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/product/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/product/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1).

Ngokufanayo, ipharamitha ye-rev ingasetyenziswa ne-API:

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/api/v2/product/7623186089763?rev=1](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/api/v2/product/7623186089763?rev=1)
