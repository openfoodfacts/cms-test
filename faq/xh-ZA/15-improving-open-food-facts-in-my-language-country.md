## Ndingathanda ukongeza ilogo entsha yeeleyibhile

Nantsi inkqubo:

- fumana igama elibhalwe _canonical_\* leleyibhile kwi-[labels' taxonomy](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/blob/main/taxonomies/labels.txt) (yinto yokuqala kuluhlu lwamagama afanayo eeleyibhile, umz. en:100% yemifuno)
-

**fumana ilogo isemgangathweni olungileyo**: kuphephe ukusebenzisa ifoto yomntu onikelayo engafanelanga eli tyala; uninzi lweeleyibhile luneelogo ezisemgangathweni ophezulu zewebhusayithi, ngamanye amaxesha zikwifomathi yevektha (ingcono nangakumbi kuthi); lo gama nje sisebenzisa ilogo ukwazisa ngokwenyani malunga nokuba khona kweleyibhile ye-
kwiphakheji yemveliso, akukho mfuneko yokucela imvume.
