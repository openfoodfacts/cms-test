Nantsi inkqubo:

- fumana igama elisemthethweni leleyibhile kwi-taxonomy yeeleyibhile (yinto yokuqala kuluhlu lwamagama afanayo eeleyibhile, umz. en:100% yemifuno)
- fumana ilogo ikumgangatho olungileyo: kuphephe ukusebenzisa ifoto yomntu onikelayo engafanelanga eli tyala; uninzi lweeleyibhile luneelogo ezisemgangathweni ophezulu zewebhusayithi, ngamanye amaxesha zikwifomathi yevektha (ingcono nangakumbi kuthi); lo gama nje sisebenzisa ilogo ukwazisa ngokwenyani malunga nokuba khona kweleyibhile ye-
  kwiphakheji yemveliso, akukho mfuneko yokucela imvume.
- nika ifayile igama ngolu hlobo: name-of-the-label.[width]x90.png apho ububanzi bububanzi belogo xa iphezulu ngama-90 pixels. Amagama efayile kufuneka angagxininiswa, abhalwe ngoonobumba abancinci aze asebenzise u-"-" endaweni yezithuba.
- uze wongeze ilogo kwifolda ehambelana negama layo elisemthethweni. Ukuba igama elisemthethweni liyi-en:something, ngoko ke kufuneka libe kwi-/en/. Isiseko seelogo yi https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/tree/main/html/images/lang
