## Yintoni eqinisekisa umgangatho wedatha ekwi-Open Food Facts?

**Ukuze siqhubeke siphucula umgangatho wedatha, sixhomekeke kwiintsika ezine:**

1. Uluntu luhlala lusongeza idatha kunye neefoto kwaye luliqela elithembekileyo labahloli.

2. Sibeke imithetho yokuqiqa ukuze sibone iimpazamo kumaphepha emveliso.Umzekelo: ukuba ubunzima bezithako bungaphezulu kobunzima bemveliso iyonke, kukho into engaqhelekanga. Sinezinye iitshekhi zomgangatho ezimalunga nama-50.

3. Ngenxa yeMachine Learning, sinciphisa umngcipheko weempazamo zokufaka. Itekhnoloji yethu isivumela ukuba sikhuphe idatha yombhalo kwiifoto ezithathwe ngabanikeli

4. Ngenxa yenkxaso yeSanté Publique France, senze i-portal evumela abavelisi ukuba balayishe idatha yabo eyenziwe ngobuninzi baze ngaloo ndlela balungise/bagqibezele igalelo loluntu.

## Ungongeza njani iimveliso?

Ukuze wongeze iimveliso kwiqonga lethu, kufuneka uqale ugqibe ukuba ufuna ukuqhubeka njani. Kukho iindlela ezi-4:

- **Ngesandla**: ukuba unenani elincinci leemveliso, kusenokwenzeka ukuba wenze iphepha ngalinye lemveliso ngesandla kwiPro Platform.

- **Ngokusebenzisa ispredishithi yokungenisa**: ungakhuphela ispredishithi [template](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) ebonelelwe yi-Open Food Facts, okanye ungenise ngokuthe ngqo isicatshulwa kwinkqubo yakho ye-IT kwiqonga le-Pro.

- **Ngezihlanganisi zeekhathalogu zemveliso**. Ukuba ungumthengi kwi-EQUADIS, AGENA3000 okanye kwi-BAYARD, ungalayisha idatha kwi-Open Food Facts ngokucofa kanye. Ukuba usebenza nenye i-PIM (uLawulo loLwazi lweMveliso), nceda unxibelelane nathi ku: producers@openfoodfacts.org

- **Ngokusebenzisa unxibelelwano olulungiselelwe wena** phakathi kwenkampani yakho kunye ne-Open Food Facts. Ukuba ubala iimveliso ezingaphezu kwe-1000 kwipotifoliyo yakho, singaxoxa ngethuba lokwakha isiqhagamsheli esithile ukuze kube lula ukudluliselwa kwedatha.

Ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ezithe vetshe, siyakumema ukuba ukhuphele [isikhokelo somsebenzisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) kunye/okanye ujonge [iividiyo zethu zokufundisa](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Is it possible to delete products that are no longer sold?

Ukuba ufuna ukususa imveliso engasafumanekiyo kwimarike, kufuneka ujonge ibhokisi ethi "le mveliso ayisathengiswa" xa uhlela iphepha lemveliso yakho.

Ukuba ufuna ukususa iimveliso ezininzi, sicebisa ukuba ulandele inkqubo ekhankanyiweyo kwi [isikhokelo somsebenzisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Platform-User-Guide.pdf), kwiphepha le-18.

Ungaqhagamshelana nathi nge-producers@openfoodfacts.org ukuba unemibuzo.

Xa imveliso igciniwe, ayisayi kuphinda ivele kwi-Open Food Facts.Nangona kunjalo, iya kuhlala kwisiseko sethu sedatha ukuze sikwazi ukugcina imbali yeemveliso ezithengiswayo ngokuhamba kwexesha.

## Ngaba ulwazi lwenkampani lubaluleke ngaphezu kolwazi olusele lukhona eqongeni?

Xa umvelisi ebeka enye yeemveliso zakhe kwi-intanethi kwiakhawunti yakhe yomvelisi kwaye iphepha lemveliso sele likhona, oku kuya kuyigqibezela.

Ulwazi oluza kuthunyelwa ngumvelisi luya kuhlala lubalulekile kwaye nguye yedwa oya kukwazi ukutshintsha iimveliso zakhe xa ulwazi luzalisiwe.

Ukuba idatha ethunyelwe ngeqonga iqhekekile, ulwazi olongezwe luluntu oluvela kwiphakheji lungayigqiba.

## Ndifanele ndithumele idatha yam ngaluphi uhlobo?

I-[template](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) ibonelelwa yi-Open Food Facts.

Nangona kunjalo, nayiphi na ifomathi yespredishithi (xlsx, csv) iya kungeniswa kwiqonga.  
Indlela yethu yokusebenza yenzelwe ukufanisa ngokuzenzekelayo iimpawu ezivela kwifayile yakho, kunye nezo zikhoyo kwi-Open Food Facts. Lumka ukuba usoloko ujonga kabini inqanaba lokufanisa njengoko kunokufuneka ulungise ezinye zazo ngesandla.

Ukuba ulwazi lwakho lwahlulwe phakathi kweespredishithi ezininzi, ungazingenisa ngokulandelelana - uqinisekisa ukuba uyayifanisa loo nto ukuba ifomathi iyatshintsha.

Ungangenisa kwakhona ifayile ye-Excel ukongeza kwi-EQUADIS okanye kwi-AGENA3000 yokungenisa, ukuba idatha ayikabikho kwinkqubo yakho yolwazi. Kwezi meko, ungalibali ukucela ukuthunyelwa kwi-database kawonke-wonke ukusuka kwi-interface.

→ Ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ezithe vetshe, siyakumema ukuba ukhuphele [isikhokelo somsebenzisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) kunye/okanye ujonge [iividiyo zethu zokufundisa](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Udityaniswe neziphi iikhathalogu zemveliso?

Abavelisi ngoku banokuthumela idatha yemveliso yabo kunye neefoto ngexesha langempela ukusuka kwi-**Equadis,** **AGENA3000 kunye ne-BAYARD consulting** ukuya kwi-Open Food Facts.

Ngokuqinisekileyo uya kuzuza nakwiingcebiso zokuhlaziya kunye nazo zonke iimpawu zeqonga.

[Iposi yethu yebhlog](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/real-time-product-data-from-producers-on-open-food-facts-thanks-to-the-new-equadis-integration) ichaza amanyathelo ekufuneka uwalandele ukuba ungumthengi we-EQUADIS.

Ukuba ungumthengi we-AGENA3000 (iMveliso ye-A3 PIM INDUSTRY), kufuneka ukhethe umamkeli "Iinkcukacha Zokutya Ezivulekileyo" xa uthumela amaphepha emveliso yakho. [Ulwazi oluthe kratya ngale posi yebhlog](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/share-your-product-data-in-1-click-with-the-new-agena3000-connector).

Ukuba ungumthengi wekhathalogu yedatha yemveliso eyahlukileyo (iSalsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, njl.njl.) nceda usazise ku-[producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

→ Ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ezithe vetshe, siyakumema ukuba ukhuphele [isikhokelo somsebenzisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) kunye/okanye ujonge [iividiyo zethu zokufundisa](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Idatha yemveliso evela kwinkampani yam iyafumaneka kwi-Open Food Facts. Ngaba kunokwenzeka ukulawula iakhawunti yomvelisi enxulumene nayo?

Ewe kunjalo! Ungalawula iakhawunti yomvelisi enxulumene nayo ngokudala iakhawunti ye-Open Food Facts enedilesi yeshishini lakho. Xa ubhalisa, khankanya igama lombutho okhoyo kwifomu ukuze ukwazi ukufikelela kwindawo efanelekileyo yomvelisi.

Inyathelo elifutshane lokuqinisekisa liya kufuneka ukuqinisekisa ukuba ngokwenene ungumvelisi.

Uya kukwazi ukugqiba idatha, ukongeza imifanekiso, ufumane iingcebiso ezizenzekelayo zokuphucula iNutri-Score, nokunye okuninzi. Konke oku kukhululekile ngokupheleleyo. Ungaqhagamshela nenkqubo yolawulo lwedatha yemveliso yomntu wesithathu efana ne-EQUADIS, i-AGENA3000 kunye ne-Bayard consulting.

→ Ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ezithe vetshe, siyakumema ukuba ukhuphele [isikhokelo somsebenzisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) kunye/okanye ujonge [iividiyo zethu zokufundisa](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Ngaba ukungenisa idatha kwirekhodi yemveliso kuyayigqiba okanye kuyayitshintsha irekhodi yemveliso ekhoyo?

Sisebenzisa ibhakhowudi ukuchonga iimveliso, ukuba ungenisa irekhodi enebhakhowudi efanayo nerekhodi ekhoyo, ulwazi luya kudityaniswa.

Kwidatha efana noluhlu lwezithako, amaxabiso ezondlo (ixabiso elinye elichanekileyo linokwenzeka), idatha ethunyelwe ngeqonga lomvelisi iya kubhala ngaphezulu kwedatha ekhoyo.

Kwidatha efana neelebheli/iindidi/iibhrendi (amaxabiso amaninzi achanekileyo anokwenzeka), ulwazi luyadityaniswa. Ukuba idatha ayichanekanga kwiqonga loluntu, ungayitshintsha ishidi lemveliso kwiqonga loluntu ukuze ususe amaxabiso angachanekanga.

→ Ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ezithe vetshe, siyakumema ukuba ukhuphele [isikhokelo somsebenzisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) kunye/okanye ujonge [iividiyo zethu zokufundisa](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)
