Injongo yezi mpawu kukubonelela ngolwazi oluqondakala lula kumthengi.

Iqela le-NOVA lijongene notshintsho lwemveliso, i-Eco-Score kunye nempembelelo yayo kwindalo kunye ne-Nutri-Score ngezinto zesondlo, lisazi ukuba okukhona imveliso inamanqaku asondele ku-A, kokukhona iba nomgangatho ophezulu.

Amanqaku amathathu awajongani nezinto ezifanayo. Ngoko ke akunakwenzeka ukuba zithelekiswe.
