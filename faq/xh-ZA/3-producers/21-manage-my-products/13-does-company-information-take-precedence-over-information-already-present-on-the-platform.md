Xa umvelisi ebeka enye yeemveliso zakhe kwi-intanethi kwiakhawunti yakhe yomvelisi kwaye iphepha lemveliso sele likhona, oku kuya kuyigqibezela.

Ulwazi oluza kuthunyelwa ngumvelisi luya kuhlala lubalulekile kwaye nguye yedwa oya kukwazi ukutshintsha iimveliso zakhe xa ulwazi luzalisiwe.

Ukuba idatha ethunyelwe ngeqonga iqhekekile, ulwazi olongezwe luluntu oluvela kwiphakheji lungayigqiba.
