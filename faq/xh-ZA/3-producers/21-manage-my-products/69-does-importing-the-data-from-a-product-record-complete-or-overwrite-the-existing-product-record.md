Sisebenzisa ibhakhowudi ukuchonga iimveliso, ukuba ungenisa irekhodi enebhakhowudi efanayo nerekhodi ekhoyo, ulwazi luya kudityaniswa.

Kwidatha efana noluhlu lwezithako, amaxabiso ezondlo (ixabiso elinye elichanekileyo linokwenzeka), idatha ethunyelwe ngeqonga lomvelisi iya kubhala ngaphezulu kwedatha ekhoyo.

Kwidatha efana neelebheli/iindidi/iibhrendi (amaxabiso amaninzi achanekileyo anokwenzeka), ulwazi luyadityaniswa. Ukuba idatha ayichanekanga kwiqonga loluntu, ungayitshintsha ishidi lemveliso kwiqonga loluntu ukuze ususe amaxabiso angachanekanga.
