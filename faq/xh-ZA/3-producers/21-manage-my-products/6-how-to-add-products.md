Ukuze wongeze iimveliso kwiqonga lethu, kufuneka uqale ugqibe ukuba ufuna ukuqhubeka njani. Kukho iindlela ezi-4:

- Ngesandla: ukuba unenani elincinci leemveliso, kusenokwenzeka ukuba wenze iphepha ngalinye lemveliso ngesandla kwiPro Platform.

- Ngokungenisa ispredishithi: ungakhuphela itemplate yespredishithi ebonelelwe yi-Open Food Facts, okanye ungenise ngokuthe ngqo isicatshulwa kwinkqubo yakho ye-IT kwiqonga le-Pro.

- Ngezihlanganisi zeekhathalogu zemveliso. Ukuba ungumthengi kwi-EQUADIS, AGENA3000 okanye kwi-BAYARD, ungalayisha idatha kwi-Open Food Facts ngokucofa kanye. Ukuba usebenza nenye i-PIM (uLawulo loLwazi lweMveliso), nceda unxibelelane nathi ku: producers@openfoodfacts.org

- Ngokusebenzisa unxibelelwano olulungiselelwe inkampani yakho kunye ne-Open Food Facts. Ukuba ubala iimveliso ezingaphezu kwe-1000 kwipotifoliyo yakho, singaxoxa ngethuba lokwakha isiqhagamsheli esithile ukuze kube lula ukudluliselwa kwedatha. Ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha ezithe vetshe, siyakumema ukuba ukhuphele isikhokelo sethu somsebenzisi kunye/okanye ujonge iividiyo zethu zokufundisa :-)
