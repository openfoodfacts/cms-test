Ukuba ufuna ukususa imveliso engasafumanekiyo kwimarike, kufuneka ujonge ibhokisi ethi "le mveliso ayisathengiswa" xa uhlela iphepha lemveliso yakho.

Ukuba ufuna ukususa iimveliso ezininzi, sicebisa ukuba ulandele inkqubo ekhankanyiweyo kwisikhokelo somsebenzisi, kwiphepha le-18.

Ungaqhagamshelana nathi nge-producers@openfoodfacts.org ukuba unemibuzo.

Xa imveliso igciniwe, ayisayi kuphinda ivele kwi-Open Food Facts.Nangona kunjalo, iya kuhlala kwisiseko sethu sedatha ukuze sikwazi ukugcina imbali yeemveliso ezithengiswayo ngokuhamba kwexesha.
