Ithempleyithi ibonelelwa yi-Open Food Facts.

Nangona kunjalo, nayiphi na ifomathi yespredishithi (xlsx, csv) iya kungeniswa kwiqonga.Indlela yethu yokusebenza yenzelwe ukufanisa ngokuzenzekelayo iimpawu ezivela kwifayile yakho, kunye nezo zikhoyo kwi-Open Food Facts. Lumka ukuba usoloko ujonga kabini inqanaba lokufanisa njengoko kunokufuneka ulungise ezinye zazo ngesandla.

Ukuba ulwazi lwakho lwahlulwe phakathi kweespredishithi ezininzi, ungazingenisa ngokulandelelana - uqinisekisa ukuba uyayifanisa loo nto ukuba ifomathi iyatshintsha.

Ungangenisa kwakhona ifayile ye-Excel ukongeza kwi-EQUADIS okanye kwi-AGENA3000 yokungenisa, ukuba idatha ayikabikho kwinkqubo yakho yolwazi. Kwezi meko, ungalibali ukucela ukuthunyelwa kwi-database kawonke-wonke ukusuka kwi-interface.
