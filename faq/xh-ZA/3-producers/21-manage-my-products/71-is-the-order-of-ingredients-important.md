Ulandelelwano lwezithako**lubalulekile**: lumele ulandelelwano ngobuninzi.
Eyona ndlela ilungileyo yokuzalisa izithako kukulandela kanye oko kubhalwe kwiphakheji. Kungoko ke kubaluleke kakhulu ukuba kuprintwe iifoto zezithako.
