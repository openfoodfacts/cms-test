Abavelisi ngoku banokuthumela idatha yemveliso yabo kunye neefoto ngexesha langempela ukusuka kwi-Equadis, i-AGENA3000 kunye ne-BAYARD consulting ukuya kwi-Open Food Facts.

Ngokuqinisekileyo uya kuzuza nakwiingcebiso zokuhlaziya kunye nazo zonke iimpawu zeqonga.

Iposi yethu yebhlog ichaza amanyathelo omawuwalandele ukuba ungumthengi we-EQUADIS.

Ukuba ungumthengi we-AGENA3000 (iMveliso ye-A3 PIM INDUSTRY), kufuneka ukhethe umamkeli "Iinkcukacha Zokutya Ezivulekileyo" xa uthumela amaphepha emveliso yakho. Ulwazi oluthe kratya ngale posi yebhlog.

Ukuba ungumthengi wekhathalogu yedatha yemveliso eyahlukileyo (iSalsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, njl.njl.) nceda usazise ku-producers@openfoodfacts.org
