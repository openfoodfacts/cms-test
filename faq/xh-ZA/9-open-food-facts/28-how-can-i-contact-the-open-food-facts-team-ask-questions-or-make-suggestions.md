Ungabuza imibuzo okanye wenze iziphakamiso kwi[forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)okanye unxibelelane nathi nge-imeyile:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Ukuba ungumvelisi, nceda usithumelele i-imeyile ku-producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Ukuba ungumlobi weendaba, nceda usithumelele i-imeyile ku-presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Nceda uqaphele: I-Open Food Facts yidathabheyisi esebenzisana neemveliso zokutya, asithengisi nayiphi na imveliso kwaye asinazo iikhonkco kubavelisi bazo. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
