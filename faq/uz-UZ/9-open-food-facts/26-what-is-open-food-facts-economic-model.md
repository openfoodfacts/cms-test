Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (Qayta foydalanish shartlariga qarang)

Shu tariqa hamma (shu jumladan, lekin ular bilan cheklanmagan holda, Open Food Facts hissa qo'shuvchilari va yaratuvchilari) veb-saytlar, xizmatlar, dasturiy ta'minot, mobil ilovalar yaratish yoki maqolalar va tadqiqotlar yozish uchun ma'lumotlarni qayta tarqatishi va/yoki qayta ishlatishi mumkin. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Masalan, bizning Ishlab chiqaruvchilar uchun platformamiz mutlaqo bepul va biz bundan yanada mamnunmiz, chunki bu ularga mahsulotlarini yaxshilashga yordam berishi mumkin deb o'ylaymiz.
