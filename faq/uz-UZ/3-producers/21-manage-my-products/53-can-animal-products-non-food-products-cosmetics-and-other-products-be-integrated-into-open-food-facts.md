Biz kosmetika, hayvonlar uchun ozuqa va boshqa mahsulotlar uchun maxsus loyihalar yaratdik: [Ochiq go'zallik faktlari](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Ochiq uy hayvonlari uchun ozuqa faktlari](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) va [Ochiq mahsulotlar faktlari](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/).
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
