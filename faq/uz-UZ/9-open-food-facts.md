## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Deyarli barcha hollarda, ularning xizmat ko'rsatish shartlari shaxsiy bo'lmagan har qanday foydalanishni va barcha ma'lumotlarni yoki ularning qismlarini olishni qat'iyan taqiqlaydi.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Shunday qilib, biz 2012-yilda Open Food Facts loyihasi va mobil ilovasini ishga tushirganimizda, biz buning aksini qilishga qaror qildik: biz nafaqat ma'lumotlar bazamizdan hamma uchun va barcha maqsadlarda (shu jumladan tijorat maqsadlarida) bepul va bepul foydalanish va qayta foydalanishga ruxsat beramiz, balki buni rag'batlantiramiz!

Ma'lumotlarni ommaga ochiq qilish (_ochiq ma'lumotlar_ deb nomlanuvchi narsa) butun dunyo bo'ylab shaxslar, uyushmalar, kompaniyalar, tadqiqotchilar va boshqalarga biz hech qachon o'ylamagan ma'lumotlar uchun ilovalarni o'ylab topish va ishlab chiqish imkonini beradi.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. ([Qayta foydalanish shartlari](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reusega qarang))

Shu tariqa hamma (shu jumladan, lekin ular bilan cheklanmagan holda, Open Food Facts hissa qo'shuvchilari va yaratuvchilari) veb-saytlar, xizmatlar, dasturiy ta'minot, mobil ilovalar yaratish yoki maqolalar va tadqiqotlar yozish uchun ma'lumotlarni qayta tarqatishi va/yoki qayta ishlatishi mumkin. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Masalan, bizning Ishlab chiqaruvchilar uchun platformamiz mutlaqo bepul va biz bundan yanada mamnunmiz, chunki bu ularga mahsulotlarini yaxshilashga yordam berishi mumkin deb o'ylaymiz.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Ma'lumotlar va ma'lumotlar Open Food Facts hissa qo'shuvchilari tomonidan taqdim etiladi. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. Masalan, agar mahsulotning ozuqaviy qiymati bir xil toifadagi mahsulotlardan juda farq qilsa, bu xato bo'lishi mumkin. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Agar siz prodyuser bo'lsangiz, iltimos, [biz bilan bog'laning](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. ([Hissa qo'shish shartlari](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contributionga qarang))

Biz sizga o'lchamingizdan qat'i nazar, mahsulotlaringiz haqidagi fotosuratlar va ma'lumotlarni osongina import qilish imkonini beruvchi bepul platformani ishlab chiqdik. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Qanday davom etishimiz mumkinligini muhokama qilish uchun biz bilan bog'laning: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
