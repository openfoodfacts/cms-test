## I mani loyi a nga na vutihlamuleri byo hlayela footprint na/kumbe score?

Nutri-Score, ntlawa wa NOVA na Eco-Score swi hlayeriwa kuya hi data leyi nga kona eka Open Food Facts. Tialgorithm ta hina ti landzelela hi ku hetiseka tindlela ta ximfumo ta swikoweto leswi.

---

## Hi kwihi ku hambana exikarhi ka Eco-Score, Nutri-Score na ntlawa wa NOVA?

Xikongomelo xa swikombiso leswi i ku nyika vuxokoxoko lebyi twisisekaka hi ku olova eka muxavi.

Ntlawa wa [NOVA](https://fr.openfoodfacts.org/nova) wu tirhana na ku cinca ka xiendliwa, [Eco-Score](https://fr.openfoodfacts.org/ecoscore) na ku khumbeka ka xona eka mbango na [Nutri-Score](https://fr.openfoodfacts.org/nutriscore) na swiaki swa swakudya, hi ku tiva leswaku loko xiendliwa xi ri na swikoweto swo tala ekusuhi na A, xi va xa xiyimo xa le henhla.

Swikoweto swa 3 a swi tirhani na swiaki leswi fanaka. Hikwalaho a swi koteki ku ma pimanisa ni un’wana.

---

## Xana swa koteka ku tekelela Eco-Score kumbe Nutri-Score ya switirhisiwa swa mina?

Hiku tsarisa eka pulatifomo ya vahumelerisi naku upload data ya switirhisiwa swa wena, unga hlayela Nutri-Score na Eco-Score leswi fambelanaka na switirhisiwa swa wena.



Hambiswiritano, u fanele ku yi susa loko ku tekeleriwa ku endliwile leswaku yi nga rhumeriwi eka pulatifomo ya mani na mani na swikumiwa leswi se swi xavisiwaka eka nxaxamelo wa wena.

→ Ku kuma vuxokoxoko byo tala, hi ku rhamba ku kopa [xiletelo xa mutirhisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) ya hina na/kumbe ku languta [tidyondzo ta vhidiyo](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)
