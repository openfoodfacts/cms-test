Hi tumbuluxile tiphurojeke to karhi ta switolwa, ta swakudya swa swifuwo, xikan’we na switirhisiwa swin’wana:[Vuvula Tinhla ta ku Saseka](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/),[Vula Tinhla ta Swakudya swa Swifuwo swa le Ndlwini](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)na[Ntiyiso wa Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/)hi ku landzelelana.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
