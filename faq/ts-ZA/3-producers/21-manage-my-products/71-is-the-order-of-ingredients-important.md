Ku landzelelana ka swiaki**i swa nkoka**: swi yimela ku landzelelana hi nhlayo.
Ndlela leyinene yo tata swilo leswi endliweke i ku landzelela kahle leswi tsariweke eka xipaci. Hi yona mhaka swi tlhelaka swi va swa nkoka swinene ku va swifaniso swa swilo leswi endliweke swi kandziyisiwile.
