## Ndlela yo fikelela Open Products Facts eka iPhone ?

Open Products Facts yi kumeka endzeni ka app leyikulu ya Open Food Facts. Ku hlanganisiwa loku ku pfumelela vatirhisi va iPhone ku fikelela tidathabeyisi leti hatimbirhi hi ku tirhisa xitirhisiwa xin’we, lexi olovaka.

---

## Ndzi tsakela ku seketela Open Products Facts. Xana ndzi nga endla yini ?

Hi kombela u hlaya tluka ra hina ra wiki, hi xaxamete tindlela to nghenelela: [https://wiki.openfoodfacts.org/Open_Products_Facts](https://wiki.openfoodfacts.org/Open_Products_Facts)

Ku seketela Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke, u nga languta swihlawulekisi leswi landzelaka, xin’wana na xin’wana xi endleriwe tinxaka to hambana ta minyikelo:

-

**Vaseketeri va Vupfuni/Mfumo eka Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke**:
Ku hoxa xandla ka vupfuni i swa nkoka swinene eku hetisiseni ka xivono xa pulatifomo ya datha ya xirhendzevutani leyi heleleke, ku nyika swikoweto swa swikumiwa, na nhlengeleto wa swintshuxo swa xirhendzevutani, ku tsemakanya swiyenge swo tlula 20000+, xikongomelo xa nkoka xa Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke. Swiphemu swo tala swa nkoka swa xivono lexi a swi si seketeriwa hi timali, naswona nseketelo wa wena wa timali wu nga endla nkucetelo lowukulu. Minyikelo ya n’wina yi nga pfuneta ku seketela hi timali nhluvukiso wa nkoka na switirhisiwa. Ku hoxa xandla hi timali, hi kombela u tihlanganisa eka contact@openfoodfacts.org

-

**Vahoxa xandla va Data hi ku Tirhisa App ya Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke**:
Ku hoxa xandla ka datha i ka nkoka swinene eka ku ringanisa Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke. Hi ku tirhisa app ya Android ku engetela vuxokoxoko bya swikumiwa leswintshwa, ku pfuxeta leswi nghenisiweke leswi nga kona, kumbe ku tiyisisa datha, u pfuneta ku ndlandlamuxa na ku antswisa database. Matshalatshala lawa ya nhlanganelo ya hoxa xandla eku akeni ka xitirhisiwa lexi tiyeke no tshembheka eka un’wana na un’wana.

-

**Vahoxa xandla va Xithekiniki eka Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke**:
Loko u ri na vuswikoti bya xithekiniki eka tindhawu to fana na minongonoko, nxopaxopo wa datha, kumbe dizayini ya ntokoto wa vatirhisi, vutshila bya wena byi nga vuyerisa swinene Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke. Ku nghenelela eka nhluvukiso wa app, ku antswisa matirhelo ya yona, kumbe ku ndlandlamuxa switirhisiwa swa vatirhisi i ku hoxa xandla ka nkoka. Ku nghenelela, joyina xitichi xa #openproductsfacts Slack, laha vaaki va Open Products Facts va tirhisanaka kona. U nga kuma xihlanganisi xa ndhawu leyi yo tirhela eka yona ya Slack eka webusayiti ya Open Food Facts.

Muxaka wun’wana na wun’wana wa ku hoxa xandla i wa nkoka eka ku kula na ku humelela ka Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke, ku pfuneta ku tumbuluxa pulatifomo leyi nga ta tshama nkarhi wo leha na leyi nga na vuxokoxoko eka un’wana na un’wana.

## Xana ndzi nga yi kuma kwihi API ya Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke ?

Open Products Facts API yi fana hi 98% na Open Food Facts API hikokwalaho loko u tirhisa matsalwa ya Open Food Facts API u fanele ku va na xivono lexi nga erivaleni swinene xa Open Products Facts API. Hi tlhele hi tsala dyondzo leyi tinyiketeleke ku pfumelela ku tirhisa universal scan eka app ya wena.

[https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/ref-v2/](https://vuhlayiselo bya swakudya.github.io/vuhlayiselo bya swakudya-sevha/api/ref-v2/)
