## Ku vuriwa yini hi swakudya leswi nga riki na ti-barcode ?

Open Food Facts yi na vuxokoxoko ntsena mayelana na swakudya leswi pakiweke. Eka mimpimo ya xikarhi ya swihumelerisiwa (xikombiso, matamatisi kumbe mabanana) na switirhisiwa swin’wana swa swakudya, unga tirhisa yin’wana ya ti database ta ximfumo ta swakudya swa rixaka ematshan’wini ya sweswo.

**Xiya:** Nxaxamelo lowu nga laha hansi wu na tin’wana ta tidathabeyisi ta nkoka swinene ta swakudya swa rixaka. Loko u ehleketa leswaku database yin’wana yi fanele ku katsiwa eka nxaxamelo, hi kombela u hi tihlanganisa eka: [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact)

## Xana ndzi nga lavisisa vito ra xiendliwa leri kongomeke hi API?

Khombo ra kona a swi si koteka ku lavisisa hi ku olova eka vito ra xiendliwa ntsena naswona hi ku kongoma hi ku tirhisa API.

Ku tirhisa xisefo eka xiyenge swi nga ku pfuna ku endla leswaku ku lavisisa ka wena ku kongoma swinene hambiswiritano.

## Xana ndzi nga yi fikelela/ku hlengeleta njhani datha ya tiphurojeke ta mina?

Eka tluka lerikulu ra Open Food Facts, ekhonweni ya le henhla ya ximatsi ya xikirini, ku na menyu leyi rhendzelekaka. Ehansi ka yona, u ta kuma “advanced search,” leyi u nga yi tsindziyelaka. Kutani swi le ka wena ku kumisisa leswaku hi swihi swipimelo leswi faneleke ngopfu eka (ti)phurojeke ta wena. Loko se u hlawuriwile, u ta kota ku kopa mimbuyelo leyi kumiweke hi ku rhelela ehansi ehansi ka tluka ivi u tikhoma eka “Download results”.

U nga ha tlhela u vulavurisana na:

- Matsalwa ya hina ya API:[ https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/)
- Mimpimanyeto ya Matirhiselo ya Tinhla ta Swakudya leti Pfulekeke:[ ](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/mimpimanyeto-ya-ku-tirhisa)
- Eka data ya hina:[ https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data)

## Xana ku na swiyimo swo tirhisa API?

Matsalwa hinkwawo mayelana na matirhiselo ya API ya nga kumeka eka [Tluka ra matsalwa ya API](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/), kambe hi lowu nkatsakanyo wo hatlisa:

- Dathabeyisi ya Tinhla ta Swakudya leti Pfulekeke yi kumeka tanihi datha leyi pfulekeke ehansi ka Layisense ya Dathabeyisi leyi Pfulekeke (ODbL), vona [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use) ku kuma vuxokoxoko bya nawu. Swiyimo leswimbirhi i attribution na share-alike. Loko u hlanganisa data kusuka eka Open Food Facts na ti database tin’wana, kutani ODbL yi lava leswaku database leyi humelelaka yifanele ku humesiwa tani hi data leyi pfulekeke na yona. Swi tlhela swi vula leswaku u nga hlanganisa datha ntsena na swihlovo leswi nga pfumelelaka ku hangalasiwa nakambe ko tano.

- U fanele ku **minkarhi hinkwayo** tirhisa Mutirhisi-Muyimeri wa ntolovelo loko u endla swikombelo swa API ku kuma app ya wena.

- Swipimelo swa mpimo swi sindzisiwa eka ndhawu yin’wana na yin’wana yo hetelela ya API.

## Xana ndzi nga yi kuma njhani rungula ra matimu?

Sweswi, a hi nyiki ku lahliwa ka datha ya matimu (JSONL, MongoDB, CSV).

Hambiswiritano, eka swikumiwa ha xin’we, swa koteka ku fikelela tivhidiyo ta khale ta datha ya xiendliwa hi ku tirhisa API kumbe eka tluka ra xiendliwa hi ku tirhisa ku pfuxetiwa.

Nkarhi wun’wana na wun’wana loko xiendliwa xi pfuxetiwa, ku endliwa mpfuxeto wuntshwa (dijiti leyi engetelekaka ku sukela eka 1).

Xikombiso, ku kuma ku pfuxetiwa ko sungula (=vuhundzuluxi byo sungula bya xiendliwa) bya xiendliwa lexi, tirhisa
