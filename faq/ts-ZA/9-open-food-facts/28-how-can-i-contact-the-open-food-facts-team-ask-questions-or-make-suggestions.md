U nga vutisa swivutiso kumbe ku endla switsundzuxo eka[foramu](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)kumbe u hi tihlanganisa hi imeyili:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Loko uri muhumelerisi, hi kombela u hi rhumela email eka producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Loko u ri muteki wa mahungu, hi kombela u hi rhumela email eka presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Hi kombela mi xiya: Open Food Facts i database ya ntirhisano ya switirhisiwa swa swakudya, a hi xavisi xitirhisiwa xihi na xihi naswona a hi na swihlanganisi na vahumelerisi va swona. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
