## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. I mbeagnach gach cás, toirmiscíonn a dtéarmaí seirbhíse go sainráite aon úsáid neamhphearsanta agus aon eastóscadh na sonraí go léir nó codanna de na sonraí.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Mar sin, nuair a thosaíomar tionscadal agus aip shoghluaiste Open Food Facts, siar in 2012, shocraíomar a mhalairt ar fad a dhéanamh: ní hamháin go gceadaímid úsáid agus athúsáid ár mbunachar sonraí, go saor agus gan táille, do gach duine agus do gach úsáid (lena n-áirítear úsáid tráchtála), ach molaimid é freisin!

Trí na sonraí a chur ar fáil go poiblí (ar a dtugtar _sonraí oscailte_), is féidir le daoine aonair, cumainn, cuideachtaí, taighdeoirí srl. ó gach cearn den domhan feidhmeanna a cheapadh agus a fhorbairt do na sonraí nach mbeadh smaoineamh againn orthu riamh.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (féach na [Téarmaí Athúsáide](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Dá bhrí sin, is féidir le gach duine (lena n-áirítear rannpháirtithe agus cruthaitheoirí Open Food Facts ach gan a bheith teoranta dóibh) na sonraí a athdháileadh agus/nó a athúsáid chun suíomhanna gréasáin, seirbhísí, bogearraí, feidhmchláir shoghluaiste a thógáil, nó chun ailt agus staidéir a scríobh. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Mar shampla, tá ár nArdán do Tháirgeoirí go hiomlán saor in aisce, agus táimid níos sásta leis sin mar cheapann muid go bhféadfadh sé cabhrú leo a gcuid táirgí a fheabhsú.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

Tabhair faoi deara le do thoil: [Is bunachar sonraí comhoibríoch de tháirgí bia é Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/discover), ní dhíolaimid aon táirge agus níl naisc againn lena dtáirgeoirí. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Cuireann rannpháirtithe Open Food Facts an fhaisnéis agus na sonraí isteach. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. m.sh. má tá fíricí cothaitheacha táirge an-difriúil ó tháirgí den chatagóir chéanna, d'fhéadfadh gur earráid í. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Más léiritheoir thú, déan teagmháil linn le do thoil (mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Tá! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (féach na [Téarmaí ranníocaíochta](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Tá ardán saor in aisce forbartha againn chun ligean duit grianghraif agus sonraí faoi do tháirgí a allmhairiú go héasca, beag beann ar do mhéid. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Téigh i dteagmháil linn le plé a dhéanamh ar conas is féidir linn dul ar aghaidh: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
