Tá tionscadail shonracha cruthaithe againn do chosmaidí, do bheatha ainmhithe, chomh maith le táirgí eile: [Oscail Fíricí Áilleachta](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Oscail Fíricí Bia Peataí](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) agus [Oscail Fíricí Táirgí](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) faoi seach.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
