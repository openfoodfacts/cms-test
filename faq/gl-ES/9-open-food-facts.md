## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. En case todos os casos, as súas condicións de servizo prohiben explicitamente calquera uso non persoal e calquera extracción de todos os datos ou partes dos datos.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Entón, cando comezamos o proxecto Open Food Facts e a aplicación móbil, alá polo 2012, decidimos facer exactamente o contrario: non só permitimos o uso e a reutilización da nosa base de datos, de forma libre e gratuíta, para todos e para todos os usos (incluídos os comerciais), senón que tamén o fomentamos!

Facer que os datos sexan dispoñibles publicamente (o que se coñece como _datos abertos_) permite que particulares, asociacións, empresas, investigadores etc. de todo o mundo imaxinen e desenvolvan aplicacións para os datos nas que, desde logo, nunca teriamos pensado.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (consulta as [Condicións de reutilización](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Calquera persoa (incluídos, entre outros, os colaboradores e creadores de Open Food Facts) pode así redistribuír e/ou reutilizar os datos para crear sitios web, servizos, software e aplicacións móbiles, ou para escribir artigos e estudos. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Por exemplo, a nosa Plataforma para Produtores é totalmente gratuíta e estamos aínda máis satisfeitos con iso porque cremos que podería axudalos a mellorar os seus produtos.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

A información e os datos son enviados polos colaboradores de Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. por exemplo, se a información nutricional dun produto é moi diferente da dos produtos da mesma categoría, pode tratarse dun erro. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Se vostede é produtor, por favor [póñase en contacto connosco](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Si! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (consulta as [Condicións de contribución](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Desenvolvemos unha plataforma gratuíta que che permite importar facilmente fotos e datos sobre os teus produtos, independentemente do teu tamaño. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Póñase en contacto connosco para falar sobre como podemos proceder: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
