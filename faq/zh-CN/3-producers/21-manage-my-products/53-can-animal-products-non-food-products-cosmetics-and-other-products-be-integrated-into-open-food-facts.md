我们分别针对化妆品、动物饲料以及其他产品创建了专门的项目：[打开美容资讯](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/)、[打开宠物食品资讯](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)和[打开产品资讯](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/)。
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
