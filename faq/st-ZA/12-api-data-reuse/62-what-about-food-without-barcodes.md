Open Food Facts e na le tlhahisoleseding feela mabapi le dijo tse phuthetsweng. Bakeng sa boleng bo tloaelehileng ba lihlahisoa (mohlala, tamati kapa libanana) le lihlahisoa tse ling tsa lijo, u ka sebelisa e 'ngoe ea li-database tsa semmuso tsa phepo ea naha ho fapana le hoo.

Ela Hloko: Lethathamo le ka tlase le na le tse ling tsa di-database tsa bohlokwa ka ho fetisisa tsa dijo tsa naha. Haeba o nahana hore database e 'ngoe e lokela ho kenyelletsoa lenaneng, ka kopo ikopanye le rona ho: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact

Lethathamo la Di-database tsa Naha tsa Lijo

- Australia - FSANZ - NUTTAB 2006: https://www.foodstandards.gov.au/media/documents/FSANZ%20Conf%20PostersNUTTAB.pdf
- Belgium - NUBUL - Lintlha tsa Sebopeho sa Lijo tsa Belgium: https://www.internubel.be
- Canada - FCEN: https://aliments-nutrition.canada.ca/cnf-fce/index-fra.jsp
- Czech Republic - Database ea Sebopeho sa Lijo Setsing sa Naha sa Bophelo bo Botle ba Sechaba: http://www.chpr.szu.cz/dbdata/foodcomp/nut2001.asp
- Denmark - Banka ea Boitsebiso ba Lijo tsa Denmark: https://frida.fooddata.dk/?lang=en
- Estonia - Database ea Sebopeho sa Lijo tsa Estonia: https://tka.nutridata.ee/en/
- Finland - Database ea Sebopeho sa Lijo tsa Finland - FINELI: https://fineli.fi/fineli/en/index
- Fora - CIQUAL: https://www.anses.fr/en/search/site/Table%20ciqual
- Jeremane - Sebaka sa Marang-rang sa Souci-Fachmann-Kraut: https://www.sfk.online/#/home kapa Setsi sa Sejeremane sa semmuso: Bundeslebensmittelschlüssel: https://blsdb.de/
- Italy - Banca Dati di Composizione degli Alimenti CREA: https://www.crea.gov.it/web/alimenti-e-nutrizione/banche-dati
- Netherlands - Database ea Sebopeho sa Lijo tsa Madache: https://www.rivm.nl/en/dutch-food-composition-database
- Norway - Tafole ea Sebopeho sa Lijo tsa Norway ea 2006: https://www.matvaretabellen.no/?language=en
- Poland - Litafole tsa Sebopeho sa Lijo: http://www.izz.waw.pl/en/?lang=en
- Spain - Database ea Sebopeho sa Lijo tsa Spain - BEDCA: https://www.bedca.net/bdpub/index.php
- Switzerland - Database ea Sebopeho sa Lijo tsa Switzerland: https://www.naehrwertdaten.ch/de/
- UK - Sebopeho sa sete ya data e kopantsweng ya dijo (CoFID): https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/composition-of-foods-integrated-dataset-cofid
- USA - USDA: https://ndb.nal.usda.gov/
