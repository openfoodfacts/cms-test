O ka botsa lipotso kapa wa etsa ditlhahiso ho [foramo](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)kapa wa ikopanya le rona ka imeile:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Haeba u mohlahisi, ka kopo re romelle imeile ho producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Haeba u moqolotsi oa litaba, ka kopo re romelle lengolo-tsoibila ho presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Hlokomela: Open Food Facts ke database e kopanetsoeng ea lihlahisoa tsa lijo, ha re rekise sehlahisoa leha e le sefe 'me ha re na lihokelo ho bahlahisi ba tsona. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
