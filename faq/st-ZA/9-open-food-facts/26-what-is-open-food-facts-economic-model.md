Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (sheba Melao ea ho sebelisa hape)

Motho e mong le e mong (ho kenyeletsoa le bafani le baqapi ba Open Food Facts empa ho sa felle feela ho bona) a ka aba le/kapa a sebedisa data hape ho aha diwebsaete, ditshebeletso, software, dikopo tsa mehala ya thekeng, kapa ho ngola dihlooho le dithuto. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Mohlala, Sethala sa rona sa Bahlahisi ke mahala ka ho felletseng, 'me re thabetse seo le ho feta hobane re nahana hore se ka ba thusa ho ntlafatsa lihlahisoa tsa bona.
