Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (sheba Melao ea monehelo)

Re thehile sethala sa mahala ho o dumella ho kenya difoto le tlhahisoleseding e mabapi le dihlahiswa tsa hao habonolo, ho sa tsotelehe boholo ba hao. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Ikopanye le rona ho buisana ka hore na re ka tsoela pele joang: producers@openfoodfacts.org
