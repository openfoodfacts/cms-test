Morero oa "Ho Lokisa Lijo ka Pakete" o na le ho bokella tlhahisoleseling e qaqileng mabapi le ho paka lihlahisoa tse sebelisoang haholo (sebopeho, thepa, litaelo tsa ho hlopha, boima, bokhoni).

Ho latela mokhoa oa saense o kopanetsoeng, e tla it≈°etleha ka lik'hamphani tse lumellanang ho arolelana lintlha tsa tsona tsa ho paka le ho bapala papali ea pepeneneng, le ho baahi ba lumellang pokello ea tlhahisoleseling mabapi le lihlahisoa. Tlhahisoleseding ena e tla sebediswa hape mahala ("data e bulehileng").
