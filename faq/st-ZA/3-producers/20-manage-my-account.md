## Na liakhaonto tse ngata tsa Open Food Facts li ka hokahanngoa le k'hamphani e le 'ngoe?

E, ka kopo romela imeile ho [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:support@openfoodfacts.org), e le hore re ka hokahanya liakhaonto tsohle le k'hamphani e le 'ngoe.

---

## Nka fetolela ak'haonte ea ka ea Open Food Facts joang hore e be ak'haonte ea Pro?

O ka fihlella [Sethala sa Pro](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/) ka mangolo a tumello a akhaonto ya hao a seng a ntse a le teng.

Haeba u setsebi, u tla hloka ho sebelisa aterese ea hau ea lengolo-tsoibila ea profeshenale e le hore re ka netefatsa ak'haonte ea hau.

→ Bakeng sa lintlha tse ling, re u mema hore u khoasolle [tataiso ea mosebelisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) le/kapa u shebe [lithuto tsa rona tsa video](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

---

## Nka fumana litokomane hokae tse ka nthusang ho sebelisa sethala sa Pro?

Ho na le mehloli e 'maloa e tla u thusa ho sebelisa sethala sa Pro hamolemo.

U amohelehile ho:

- hlahloba [tataiso ea rona ea mosebelisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) - re u mema hore u jarolle PDF e le hore lihokelo li ka tobeha, e leng se tla nolofatsa ho tsamaea ha hau tokomaneng.

- sheba [lithuto tsa rona tsa video](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55)

- arolelana maikutlo kapa litlhahiso ka [foromo ena](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1QSZ_v50Ueoe8JPCqivvcyEdv8AnsuUbrbKQQofSAMFc/edit)
