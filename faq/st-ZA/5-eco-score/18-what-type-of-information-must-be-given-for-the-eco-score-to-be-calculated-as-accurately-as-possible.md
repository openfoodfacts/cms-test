Hore Eco-Score e baloe hantle, peresente ea motsoako o mong le o mong le t≈°imoloho ea oona li tlameha ho boleloa.

Haeba tlhahisoleseding ena e so boleloe lenaneng la metsoako, o ka eketsa kholomo ho e bolela, ka sebopeho se latelang:

Phofo ea koro 16% (Fora)

Tsokolate 8% (Ivory Coast, Ecuador)

Re hloka hape likarolo tsa sephutheloana ka mokhoa oa: filimi e le 'ngoe ea polasetiki eo u ka e lahlang, lebokose le le leng la karete ea FSC eo u ka e sebelisang hape, libotlolo tse 6 tsa polasetiki tse bonaletsang tsa PET tsa 1.5 L ea ho sebelisa hape, likhetla tse 6 tsa polasetiki tse mebala e sa bonahaleng, makotikoti a 12 a polasetiki le 33 cl ea aluminium.
