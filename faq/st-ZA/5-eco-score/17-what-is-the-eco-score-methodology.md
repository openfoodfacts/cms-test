Eco-Score e ipapisitse le palo ea tlhahlobo ea potoloho ea bophelo e hlahisitsoeng ke ADEME (setsi sa phetoho ea tikoloho) le INRAE (setsi sa naha sa lipatlisiso tsa temo). Ho baloa le ho hokahanngoa likarolo tse ka bang leshome le metso e mehlano ho theha lintlha tsa PEF.

Ho lintlha tsena tsa PEF ho eketsoa libonase le li-maluse (mokhoa oa tlhahiso, t≈°imoloho ea

metsoako, mefuta e kotsing ea ho timela, sephutheloana) e le ho bala Eco-Score.

Bakeng sa tlhahisoleseding e batsi, sheba leqephe la kakaretso https://world.openfoodfacts.org/eco-score-the-environmental-impact-of-food-products ho Open Food Facts hammoho le webosaeteng ea mokhatlo oa Eco-Score. (Tlhahiso ea Eco-Score https://docs.score-environnemental.com/)
