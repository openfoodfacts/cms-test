## Mokhoa oa Eco-Score ke ofe?

Eco-Score e ipapisitse le palo ea tlhahlobo ea potoloho ea bophelo e hlahisitsoeng ke ADEME (setsi sa phetoho ea tikoloho) le INRAE (setsi sa naha sa lipatlisiso tsa temo). Ho baloa le ho hokahanngoa likarolo tse ka bang leshome le metso e mehlano ho theha lintlha tsa PEF.

Ho lintlha tsena tsa PEF ho eketsoa libonase le li-maluse (mokhoa oa tlhahiso, tšimoloho ea

metsoako, mefuta e kotsing ea ho timela, sephutheloana) e le ho bala Eco-Score.

Bakeng sa tlhahisoleseding e batsi, sheba leqephe la kakaretso [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/eco-score-the-environmental-impact-of-food-products](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/eco-score-the-environmental-impact-of-food-products) ho Open Food Facts hammoho le webosaeteng ya mokgatlo wa Eco-Score. (Tlhahiso ea Eco-Score [https://docs.score-environnemental.com/](https://docs.score-environnemental.com/))

---

## Ke mofuta ofe oa tlhahisoleseling o lokelang ho fanoa hore Eco-Score e baloe ka nepo kamoo ho ka khonehang?

Hore Eco-Score e baloe hantle, peresente ea motsoako o mong le o mong le tšimoloho ea oona li tlameha ho boleloa.

Haeba tlhahisoleseding ena e so boleloe lenaneng la metsoako, o ka eketsa kholomo ho e bolela, ka sebopeho se latelang:

Phofo ea koro 16% (Fora)

Tsokolate 8% (Ivory Coast, Ecuador)

Re hloka hape likarolo tsa sephutheloana ka mokhoa oa: filimi e le 'ngoe ea polasetiki eo u ka e lahlang, lebokose le le leng la karete ea FSC eo u ka e sebelisang hape, libotlolo tse 6 tsa polasetiki tse bonaletsang tsa PET tsa 1.5 L ea ho sebelisa hape, likhetla tse 6 tsa polasetiki tse mebala e sa bonahaleng, makotikoti a 12 a polasetiki le 33 cl ea aluminium.

## Litekanyetso tsa matšoao a 15 li kopantsoe joang?

**Ho utloisisa hantle sistimi ea PEF, o ka bala: tlhaloso ea lintlha tsa PEF sebakeng sa marang-rang sa mekhoa ea Eco-Score**

---

## Na karolo ea lehae e ama sebaka seo thepa e tala e entsoeng ho sona kapa sebaka seo thepa e tala e simolohileng ho sona?

**Tlhokomelo ea lehae e amana le tšimoloho ea thepa e tala. Ke ka lebaka lena ho leng bohlokoa ho kenya liphesente tsa tsona hammoho le tšimoloho ea tsona lethathamong la metsoako ea sehlahisoa kapa tšimong e inehetseng ho tšimoloho ea metsoako.**

---

## Mokhoa oa ho kenya tšimoloho ea metsoako bakeng sa eco-score?

Haeba o lakatsa ho arolelana tlhahisoleseding mabapi le tšimoloho ea thepa e tala maqepheng a hau, re u mema hore u e kenye lethathamong la metsoako.
