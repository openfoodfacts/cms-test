## Na ho na le dikgothaletso kae kapa kae ditokomaneng mabapi le hore na ke boholo bofe bo loketseng bakeng sa difoto tse kentsweng?

Seo se ka itšetleha ka linaha, haeba netweke e lieha kapa e theko e boima. ntho efe kapa efe e fetang dipiksele tse 5000 ka boima kapa bolelele mohlomong ha e na thuso haholo. 'me haeba ka tsela e itseng u ka lemoha hore marang-rang a lieha, joale esita le setšoantšo sa li-pixel tse 2000 se ka ba setle (ka sebele se betere ho feta ho se be le setšoantšo!)

## Ho thoe'ng ka lijo tse se nang li-barcode?

Open Food Facts e na le tlhahisoleseding feela mabapi le dijo tse phuthetsweng. Bakeng sa boleng bo tloaelehileng ba lihlahisoa (mohlala, tamati kapa libanana) le lihlahisoa tse ling tsa lijo, u ka sebelisa e 'ngoe ea li-database tsa semmuso tsa phepo ea naha ho fapana le hoo.

**Hlokomela:** Lethathamo le ka tlase le na le tse ling tsa di-database tsa bohlokwa ka ho fetisisa tsa dijo tsa naha. Haeba o nahana hore database e 'ngoe e lokela ho kenyelletsoa lenaneng, ka kopo ikopanye le rona ho: [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact)

## Na nka batla lebitso le nepahetseng la sehlahisoa ka API?

Ka bomalimabe ha ho so khonehe ho batla habonolo lebitsong la sehlahisoa feela le ka nepo ka API.

Ho sebelisa sefe sehlopheng ho ka u thusa ho etsa hore patlo ea hau e nepahale haholoanyane.

## Nka fumana/bokella data bakeng sa merero ea ka joang?

Leqepheng le ka sehloohong la Open Food Facts, hukung e ka hodimo ka letsohong le letshehadi la skrine, ho na le lenane la ho skrola. Botlaaseng ba eona, u tla fumana khetho ea "patlo e tsoetseng pele", eo u ka tobetsang ho eona. Ebe ho ho uena ho etsa qeto ea hore na ke litekanyetso life tse amanang le projeke ea hau. Hang ha o se o kgethile, o tla kgona ho jarolla diphetho tse fumanweng ka ho skrola tlase tlase ho leqephe mme o tobetse ho “Jarolla diphetho”.

U ka boela ua buisana le:

- Litokomane tsa rona tsa API:[ https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/)
- Melao ea Ts'ebeliso ea Lintlha tsa Lijo tse Butsoeng:[ ](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)
- Ho data ea rona:[ https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data)

## Na ho na le maemo a ho sebelisa API?

Litokomane tsohle tse mabapi le tšebeliso ea API li ka fumanoa ho [leqephe la litokomane tsa API](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/), empa kakaretso e khutšoanyane ke ena:

- Database ea Open Food Facts e fumaneha e le data e bulehileng tlas'a Open Database Laesense (ODbL), bona [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use) bakeng sa lintlha tsa molao. Maemo ana a mabedi ke ho abela le ho arolelana. Haeba o kopanya data e tswang ho Open Food Facts le di-database tse ding, ODbL e hloka hore database e hlahisitsweng e lokollwe e le data e bulehileng hape. Hape ho bolela hore o ka kopanya data feela le mehloli e ka lumellang kabo e joalo hape.

- O tlameha ho **kamehla** ho sebedisa Moemedi wa Mosebelisi ya ikgethileng ha o etsa mehala ya API ho hlwaya sesebelisoa sa hao.

- Meeli ea sekhahla e qobelloa bakeng sa ntlha e 'ngoe le e 'ngoe ea API.

## Nka fumana data ea nalane joang?

Hajoale, ha re fane ka mokhoa oa ho lahla data ea nalane (JSONL, MongoDB, CSV).

Leha ho le jwalo, bakeng sa dihlahiswa ka bomong, ho a kgoneha ho fihlella diphetolelo tse fetileng tsa data ya sehlahiswa o sebedisa API kapa leqepheng la sehlahiswa o sebedisa diphetoho.

Nako le nako ha sehlahisoa se ntjhafatswa, ho etswa ntjhafatso e ntjha (dinomoro tse ntseng di eketseha ho tloha ho 1).

Mohlala, ho fumana phetolelo ya pele (=mofuta wa pele wa sehlahiswa) sa sehlahiswa sena, sebedisa

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/product/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/product/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1).

Ka ho tšoanang, paramethara ea rev e ka sebelisoa le API:

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/api/v2/product/7623186089763?rev=1](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/api/v2/product/7623186089763?rev=1)
