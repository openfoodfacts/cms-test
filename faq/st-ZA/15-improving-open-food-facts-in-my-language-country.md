## Ke kopa ho eketsa letshwao le letjha bakeng sa mabitso

Ts'ebetso ke ena:

- fumana **lebitso la canonical** la leibole ho [taxonomy ea li-labels](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/blob/main/taxonomies/labels.txt) (ke ntho ea pele lethathamong la mantsoe a t≈°oanang a li-label, mohlala: 100% meroho)
-

**fumana letshwao ka boleng bo botle**: qoba ho sebedisa foto ya motho ya kentseng letsoho e sa lokelang nyeoe ena; mabitso a mangata a na le mabitso a semmuso a websaete a boleng bo hodimo, ka dinako tse ding ka sebopeho sa vekthara (ho molemo le ho feta ho rona); ha feela re sebedisa letshwao ho tsebisa ka ho hlaka ka boteng ba letshwao la
sephuthelong sa sehlahiswa, ha ho hlokahale hore o kope tumello.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**reha faele ka tsela ena**: lebitso-la-leibole.[bophara]x90.png moo bophara e leng bophara ba letshwao ha le phahame ka dipiksele tse 90. Mabitso a difaele a hloka ho ba a sa hlahiswang, ka ditlhaku tse nyane mme a sebedise "-" ho ena le dibaka.

- ebe o eketsa letshwao bukeng e tsamaellanang le lebitso la yona la semmuso. Haeba lebitso la molao ke en:ntho e itseng, joale li hloka ho ba ho /en/. Bukana ea motso ea li-logo ke [https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/tree/main/html/images/lang](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/openfoodfacts-server/tree/main/html/images/lang)
