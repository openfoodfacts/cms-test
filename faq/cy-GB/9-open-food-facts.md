## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Ym mron pob achos, mae eu telerau gwasanaeth yn gwahardd yn benodol unrhyw ddefnydd nad yw'n bersonol ac unrhyw echdynnu'r holl ddata neu rannau o'r data.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Felly pan ddechreuon ni’r prosiect Ffeithiau Bwyd Agored a’r ap symudol, yn ôl yn 2012, penderfynon ni wneud yn union yr gwrthwyneb: nid yn unig rydyn ni’n caniatáu defnyddio ac ailddefnyddio ein cronfa ddata, yn rhad ac am ddim a heb ffi, i bawb ac ar gyfer pob defnydd (gan gynnwys masnachol), ond rydyn ni hefyd yn ei annog!

Mae gwneud y data ar gael i'r cyhoedd (yr hyn a elwir yn _data agored_) yn caniatáu i unigolion, cymdeithasau, cwmnïau, ymchwilwyr ac ati o bob cwr o'r byd feddwl am gymwysiadau ar gyfer y data a'u datblygu na fyddem ni byth wedi meddwl amdanyn nhw yn sicr.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (gweler y [Telerau ailddefnyddio](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Felly gall pawb (gan gynnwys ond heb fod yn gyfyngedig i gyfranwyr a chrewyr Open Food Facts) ailddosbarthu a/neu ailddefnyddio'r data i adeiladu gwefannau, gwasanaethau, meddalwedd, apiau symudol, neu i ysgrifennu erthyglau ac astudiaethau. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Er enghraifft, mae ein Platfform i Gynhyrchwyr yn hollol rhad ac am ddim, ac rydym yn fwy falch fyth o hynny oherwydd ein bod yn credu y gallai eu helpu i wella eu cynnyrch.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

Gallwch ofyn cwestiynau neu wneud awgrymiadau ar y fforwm neu gysylltu â ni drwy e-bost: contact@openfoodfacts.org

Os ydych chi'n gynhyrchydd, anfonwch e-bost atom yn [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org).

Os ydych chi'n newyddiadurwr, anfonwch e-bost atom yn [presse@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:presse@openfoodfacts.org).

Noder: [Mae Open Food Facts yn gronfa ddata gydweithredol o gynhyrchion bwyd](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/discover), nid ydym yn gwerthu unrhyw gynnyrch ac nid oes gennym gysylltiadau â'u cynhyrchwyr. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Cyflwynir y wybodaeth a'r data gan gyfranwyr Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. e.e. os yw ffeithiau maethol cynnyrch yn wahanol iawn i gynhyrchion o'r un categori, gallai fod yn gamgymeriad. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Os ydych chi'n gynhyrchydd, cysylltwch â ni (mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (gweler y [Telerau cyfraniad](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Rydym wedi datblygu platfform am ddim i'ch galluogi i fewnforio lluniau a data am eich cynhyrchion yn hawdd, waeth beth fo'ch maint. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Cysylltwch â ni i drafod sut y gallem fwrw ymlaen: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
