Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (vidi Uvjete doprinosa)

Razvili smo besplatnu platformu koja vam omogućuje jednostavan uvoz fotografija i podataka o vašim proizvodima, bez obzira na njihovu veličinu. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Kontaktirajte nas kako bismo razgovarali o daljnjem postupanju: producers@openfoodfacts.org
