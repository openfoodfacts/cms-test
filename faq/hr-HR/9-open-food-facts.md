## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. U gotovo svim slučajevima, njihovi uvjeti pružanja usluge izričito zabranjuju bilo kakvu neosobnu upotrebu i bilo kakvo izdvajanje svih podataka ili dijelova podataka.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Stoga smo, kada smo 2012. godine pokrenuli projekt i mobilnu aplikaciju Open Food Facts, odlučili učiniti upravo suprotno: ne samo da dopuštamo korištenje i ponovnu upotrebu naše baze podataka, slobodno i bez naknade, svima i za sve namjene (uključujući i komercijalne), već to i potičemo!

Javna dostupnost podataka (tzv. _otvoreni podaci_) omogućuje pojedincima, udrugama, tvrtkama, istraživačima itd. iz cijelog svijeta da osmisle i razviju aplikacije za podatke o kojima sigurno nikada ne bismo razmišljali.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (vidi [Uvjete ponovne upotrebe](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Svi (uključujući, ali ne ograničavajući se na suradnike i kreatore Open Food Factsa) mogu stoga redistribuirati i/ili ponovno koristiti podatke za izradu web stranica, usluga, softvera, mobilnih aplikacija ili za pisanje članaka i studija. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Na primjer, naša Platforma za proizvođače je potpuno besplatna, a time smo još zadovoljniji jer mislimo da bi im to moglo pomoći u poboljšanju njihovih proizvoda.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Informacije i podatke dostavljaju suradnici Open Food Factsa. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. npr. ako se nutritivne vrijednosti proizvoda jako razlikuju od proizvoda iste kategorije, to može biti pogreška. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Ako ste producent, molimo [kontaktirajte nas](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (vidi [Uvjete doprinosa](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Razvili smo besplatnu platformu koja vam omogućuje jednostavan uvoz fotografija i podataka o vašim proizvodima, bez obzira na njihovu veličinu. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Kontaktirajte nas kako bismo razgovarali o daljnjem postupku: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
