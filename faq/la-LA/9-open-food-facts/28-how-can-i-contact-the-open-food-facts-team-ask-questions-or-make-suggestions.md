Quaestiones vel suggestiones in foro (https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/) ponere vel per inscriptionem electronicam nobiscum communicare potes: [contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org).

Si productor es, nobis inscriptionem electronicam ad producers@openfoodfacts.org mitte.

Si diurnarius es, nobis quaeso inscriptionem electronicam ad presse@openfoodfacts.org mitte.

Nota bene: Open Food Facts est index rerum cibariorum collaborativus; nullas res vendimus nec nexus cum eorum productoribus habemus. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
