The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Fere omnibus casibus, condiciones servitii eorum explicite vetant quemlibet usum non personalem et quamlibet extractionem omnium datorum vel partium datorum.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Itaque cum anno MMXII incepimus propositum "Open Food Facts" et applicationem mobilem, omnino contrarium facere decrevimus: non solum usum et reutilizationem basis datorum nostrae, libere et sine pretio, omnibus et ad omnes usus (incluso commerciales) permittimus, sed etiam eum hortamur!

Publicatio notitiarum (quod "data aperta" appellatur) permittit individuis, consociationibus, societatibus, investigatoribus, et cetera, ex toto orbe terrarum, ut applicationes pro his notitiis excogitent et evolvant, de quibus certe numquam cogitassemus.
