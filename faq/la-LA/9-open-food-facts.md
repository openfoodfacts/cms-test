## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Fere omnibus casibus, condiciones servitii eorum explicite vetant quemlibet usum non personalem et quamlibet extractionem omnium datorum vel partium datorum.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Itaque cum anno MMXII incepimus propositum "Open Food Facts" et applicationem mobilem, omnino contrarium facere decrevimus: non solum usum et reutilizationem basis datorum nostrae, libere et sine pretio, omnibus et ad omnes usus (incluso commerciales) permittimus, sed etiam eum hortamur!

Publicatio notitiarum (quod _data aperta_ appellatur) permittit individuis, consociationibus, societatibus, investigatoribus etc. ex toto orbe terrarum ut applicationes pro his notitiis excogitent et evolvant, de quibus certe numquam cogitassemus.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (vide [Condiciones usus reutilis](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Omnes (inter quos, sed non solum, contributores et creatores Open Food Facts) data redistribuere et/vel iterum adhibere possunt ad situs interretiales, officia, programmata, applicationes mobiles construendas, vel ad articulos et studia scribenda. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Exempli gratia, suggestus noster pro productoribus omnino gratuitus est, et eo magis gaudemus quia putamus eos adiuvare posse ad producta sua emendanda.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Informationes et data a contributoribus Open Food Facts submittuntur. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. Exempli gratia, si notitia nutritionis producti a productis eiusdem categoriae valde differt, error esse potest. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Si productor es, quaeso [nobiscum communica](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (vide [Condiciones contributionis](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Platformam gratuitam elaboravimus ut facile imagines et notitias de tuis mercibus importare possis, magnitudine tua non obstante. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Nobiscum communicare potes ut de ratione procedendi disseramus: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
