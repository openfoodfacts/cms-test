Proposita specifica pro cosmeticis, pro pabulo animalium, necnon pro aliis productis creavimus: [Aperi Facta de Pulchritudine](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Aperi Facta de Cibo Animalium Domesticorum](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) et [Aperi Facta de Productis](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) respective.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
