## Waa maxay Folksonomi Engine?

Folksonomi Engine waa nidaam u oggolaanaya dadka isticmaala inay ku daraan calaamadaha gaarka ah, ama sifooyinka, alaabada cuntada ee ku jira Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Furan, oo ah kaydka cuntada furan ee ugu weyn adduunka. Tani waxay u oggolaanaysaa dadka inay cuntada u kala saaraan oo ay ku qeexaan siyaabo cusub, marka laga reebo sifooyinka hore loo sii qeexay.
