## Waa maxay habka Dhibcaha Eco-Score?

Dhibcaha Eco-Score waxay ku salaysan yihiin xisaabinta falanqaynta wareegga nolosha ee ay soo saareen ADEME (hay'adda kala-guurka deegaanka) iyo INRAE (machadka qaranka ee cilmi-baarista beeraha). Qiyaastii shan iyo toban qaybood ayaa la xisaabiyaa oo la isku xiraa si loo abuuro dhibcaha PEF.

Dhibcaha PEF waxaa lagu daray gunnooyin iyo khaladaad (habka wax soo saarka, asalka

maaddooyinka, noocyada dabar-go'aya, baakadaha) si loo xisaabiyo Dhibcaha Deegaanka.

Wixii macluumaad dheeraad ah, eeg bogga kooban [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/eco-score-the-environmental-impact-of-food-products](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/eco-score-the-environmental-impact-of-food-products) ee ku saabsan Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Furan iyo sidoo kale bogga internetka ee ururka Eco-Score. (Bandhigga Dhibcaha Deegaanka [https://docs.score-environnemental.com/](https://docs.score-environnemental.com/))

---

## Noocee macluumaad ah ayaa la bixin doonaa si loo xisaabiyo Dhibcaha Eco-Score sida ugu saxsan ee suurtogalka ah?

Si loo xisaabiyo Dhibcaha Eco-Score si sax ah, waa in la cayimaa boqolleyda walax kasta iyo asalkeeda.

Haddii macluumaadkan aan hore loogu qeexin liiska maaddooyinka, waxaad ku dari kartaa tiir si aad u xusto, oo leh qaabkan soo socda:

Bur qamadi ah 16% (Faransiiska)

Shukulaato 8% (Xeebta Ivory Coast, Ecuador)

Waxaan sidoo kale u baahanahay qaybaha baakadaha qaab ahaan: 1 filim balaastig ah oo la tuurayo, 1 sanduuq kartoon FSC ah oo dib loo warshadaynayo, 6 dhalo balaastig ah oo hufan oo PET ah oo 1.5 L ah si dib loogu warshadeeyo, 6 dabool oo balaastig ah oo midabaysan, 12 qasacadood oo balaastig ah oo 33 cl aluminium ah.

## Sidee loo isku daraa qiimaha tilmaamayaasha 15?

**Si aad si fiican u fahamto nidaamka PEF, waxaad akhrin kartaa: sharraxaadda dhibcaha PEF ee bogga habka Eco-Score**

## Dhinaca deegaanku ma wuxuu khuseeyaa goobta wax soo saarka ama meesha laga soo saaray alaabta ceeriin?

\*\*Dhinaca deegaanku wuxuu khuseeyaa asalka agabka ceeriin ah. Tani waa sababta ay muhiim u tahay in boqolleydooda iyo sidoo kale asalkooda lagu daro liiska maaddooyinka wax soo saarka ama goobta loogu talagalay asalka maaddooyinka.

---

## Sidee loo geli karaa asalka maaddooyinka loogu talagalay dhibcaha deegaanka?

Haddii aad rabto inaad la wadaagto macluumaadka ku saabsan asalka agabka ceeriin ee ku yaal xaashidaada, waxaan kugu martiqaadeynaa inaad ku darto liiska maaddooyinka.
