## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Ku dhawaad dhammaan kiisaska, shuruudaha adeeggooda si cad ayay u mamnuucayaan isticmaalka aan shaqsiga ahayn iyo soo saarista dhammaan xogta ama qaybaha xogta.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Markaa markii aan bilownay mashruuca Furan ee Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada iyo abka moobaylka, sannadkii 2012, waxaan go'aansannay inaan sameyno wax liddi ku ah: kaliya ma aha inaan u oggolaanno isticmaalka iyo dib u isticmaalka xogtayada, si xor ah oo aan lacag lahayn, qof walba iyo dhammaan isticmaalka (oo ay ku jiraan ganacsiga), laakiin sidoo kale waan ku dhiirigelinaynaa!

In xogta si dadweyne loo heli karo (waxa loo yaqaan _xog furan_) waxay u oggolaanaysaa shakhsiyaadka, ururada, shirkadaha, cilmi-baarayaasha iwm. ee ka kala yimid daafaha adduunka inay ka fikiraan oo ay horumariyaan codsiyada xogta oo aynaan waligeen ka fikiri lahayn.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (fiiri [Shuruudaha dib u isticmaalka](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Qof walba (oo ay ku jiraan laakiin aan ku xaddidnayn kuwa wax ka dhiga iyo abuurayaasha Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Furan) sidaas darteed waxay dib u qaybin karaan oo/ama dib u isticmaali karaan xogta si ay u dhisaan mareegaha, adeegyada, barnaamijyada, barnaamijyada moobaylka, ama si ay u qoraan maqaallo iyo daraasado. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Tusaale ahaan, Madalkeena Soo-saareyaasha waa bilaash, waana ku faraxsanahay taas sababtoo ah waxaan u maleyneynaa inay ka caawin karto inay horumariyaan alaabadooda.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Macluumaadka iyo xogta waxaa soo gudbiya ka-qaybgalayaasha Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Furan. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. Tusaale ahaan haddii xaqiiqooyinka nafaqada ee badeecadu ay aad uga duwan yihiin alaabada isku qaybta ah, waxay noqon kartaa qalad. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Haddii aad tahay soo-saare, fadlan [nala soo xiriir](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (fiiri [Shuruudaha Ka-qaybgalka](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Waxaan samaynay madal bilaash ah si aan kuu oggolaano inaad si fudud u soo dejiso sawirro iyo xog ku saabsan alaabtaada, iyadoon loo eegin cabbirkaaga. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Nala soo xiriir si aad ugala hadasho sida aan u sii wadi karno: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
