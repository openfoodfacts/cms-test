Ujeedada tilmaamayaashani waa in macaamiisha loo siiyo macluumaad si fudud loo fahmi karo.

Kooxda NOVA waxay ka shaqeysaa isbeddelka badeecada, Eco-Score oo leh saameynteeda deegaanka iyo Nutri-Score oo leh walxaha nafaqada, iyadoo la ogyahay in inta badeecadu ay dhibco u dhow A leedahay, ay tayo badan tahay.

Saddexda dhibcood ma khuseeyaan isku mid ah walxaha. Sidaa darteed suurtagal ma aha in la is barbar dhigo.
