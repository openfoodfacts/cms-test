Waxaan abuurnay mashaariic gaar ah oo loogu talagalay qurxinta, quudinta xoolaha, iyo sidoo kale alaabada kale: [Xaqiiqooyinka Quruxda Furan](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Xayawaanka Furan](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) iyo [Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) siday u kala horreeyaan.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
