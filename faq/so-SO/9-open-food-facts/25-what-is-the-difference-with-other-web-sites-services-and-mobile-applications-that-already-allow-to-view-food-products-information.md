The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Ku dhawaad dhammaan kiisaska, shuruudaha adeeggooda si cad ayay u mamnuucayaan isticmaalka aan shaqsiga ahayn iyo soo saarista dhammaan xogta ama qaybaha xogta.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Markaa markii aan bilownay mashruuca Furan ee Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada iyo abka moobaylka, sannadkii 2012, waxaan go'aansannay inaan sameyno wax liddi ku ah: kaliya ma aha inaan u oggolaanno isticmaalka iyo dib u isticmaalka xogtayada, si xor ah oo aan lacag lahayn, qof walba iyo dhammaan isticmaalka (oo ay ku jiraan ganacsiga), laakiin sidoo kale waan ku dhiirigelinaynaa!

In xogta si dadweyne loo heli karo (waxa loo yaqaan xog furan) waxay u oggolaanaysaa shakhsiyaadka, ururada, shirkadaha, cilmi-baarayaasha iwm. ee ka kala yimid daafaha adduunka inay ka fikiraan oo ay horumariyaan codsiyada xogta oo aynaan waligeen ka fikiri lahayn.
