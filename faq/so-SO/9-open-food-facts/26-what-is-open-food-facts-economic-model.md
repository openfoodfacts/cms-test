Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (fiiri Shuruudaha Dib-u-isticmaalka)

Qof walba (oo ay ku jiraan laakiin aan ku xaddidnayn kuwa wax ka dhiga iyo abuurayaasha Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Furan) sidaas darteed waxay dib u qaybin karaan oo/ama dib u isticmaali karaan xogta si ay u dhisaan mareegaha, adeegyada, barnaamijyada, barnaamijyada moobaylka, ama si ay u qoraan maqaallo iyo daraasado. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Tusaale ahaan, Madalkeena Soo-saareyaasha waa bilaash, waana ku faraxsanahay taas sababtoo ah waxaan u maleyneynaa inay ka caawin karto inay horumariyaan alaabadooda.
