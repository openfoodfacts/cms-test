Waxaad su'aalo ku weydiin kartaa ama talooyin ka bixin kartaa [forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/) ama nagala soo xiriir emayl ahaan:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Haddii aad tahay soo-saare, fadlan noogu soo dir emayl producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Haddii aad tahay wariye, fadlan noogu soo dir emaylka presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Fadlan ogow: Furan Cuntada Xaqiiqooyinka waa xog ururin wada jir ah oo ku saabsan cuntooyinka, ma iibinno wax badeeco ah mana hayno xiriir aan la leenahay soo-saareyaasha. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
