Panjenengan saged takon utawi menehi saran wonten ing [forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)utawi hubungi kita liwat email: [contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Menawi panjenengan minangka produser, sumangga kirim email dhateng producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Menawi panjenengan wartawan, sumangga kirim email dhateng presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Wigati: Open Food Facts minangka basis data kolaboratif produk panganan, kita ora adol produk apa wae lan kita ora duwe pranala menyang produsen. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
