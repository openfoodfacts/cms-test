## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Ing meh kabeh kasus, syarat layanan kasebut kanthi tegas nglarang panggunaan non-pribadi lan ekstraksi kabeh data utawa bagean saka data kasebut.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Dadi nalika kita miwiti proyek lan aplikasi seluler Open Food Facts, ing taun 2012, kita mutusake kanggo nindakake sing ngelawan: kita ora mung ngidini panggunaan lan panggunaan maneh database kita, kanthi gratis lan tanpa biaya, kanggo kabeh wong lan kanggo kabeh panggunaan (kalebu komersial), nanging kita uga nyengkuyung!

Ndadekake data kasedhiya kanggo umum (sing dikenal minangka _data terbuka_) ngidini individu, asosiasi, perusahaan, peneliti, lan liya-liyane saka sak ndonya mikir lan ngembangake aplikasi kanggo data sing mesthi ora bakal kita pikirake.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (waca [Katentuan panggunaan maneh](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Saben uwong (kalebu nanging ora winates ing kontributor lan kreator Open Food Facts) bisa nyebarake maneh lan/utawa nggunakake maneh data kasebut kanggo mbangun situs web, layanan, piranti lunak, aplikasi seluler, utawa kanggo nulis artikel lan panliten. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Umpamane, Platform kanggo Produsen kita gratis, lan kita luwih seneng karo kuwi amarga kita mikir iki bisa mbantu dheweke ningkatake produke.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Informasi lan data iki diajukake dening kontributor Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. contone, yen fakta nutrisi produk beda banget karo produk saka kategori sing padha, bisa uga ana kesalahan. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Menawi panjenengan minangka produser, mangga [hubungi kita](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (waca [Katentuan kontribusi](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Kita wis ngembangake platform gratis supaya sampeyan bisa ngimpor foto lan data babagan produk sampeyan kanthi gampang, preduli saka ukurane. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Hubungi kita kanggo ngrembug babagan carane nerusake: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
