Kita wis nggawe proyèk khusus kanggo kosmetik, pakan kéwan, uga kanggo produk liyané: [Fakta Kecantikan Terbuka](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Fakta Panganan Kéwan Terbuka](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) lan [Fakta Produk Terbuka](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) masing-masing.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
