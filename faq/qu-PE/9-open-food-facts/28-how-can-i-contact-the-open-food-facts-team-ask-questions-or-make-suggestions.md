Tapukuyta atinki utaq yuyaychaykunata ruwayta atinki kay[forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)utaq kay correo electrónico nisqawan rimanakuy:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Sichus ruruchiq kanki chayqa, kayman correo electrónicota apachimuwayku producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Sichus periodista kanki chayqa, kayman correo electrónicota apachimuwayku presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Ama hina kaspa, kayta qhaway: Open Food Facts nisqaqa mikhuy rurukunamanta kuskachasqa willay tantana wasim, manam ima rurutapas ranqhaykuchu nitaqmi ruruchiqninkunawanpas tinkiykuchu. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
