Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (jereo ny Fepetra momba ny fandraisana anjara)

Namorona sehatra maimaim-poana izahay ahafahanao manafatra sary sy angon-drakitra momba ny vokatrao mora foana, na firy na firy ny habeny. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Mifandraisa aminay raha te hiresaka momba ny fomba ahafahanay manohy: producers@openfoodfacts.org
