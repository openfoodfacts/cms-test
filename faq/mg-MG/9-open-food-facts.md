## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Saika amin'ny tranga rehetra, ny fepetran'ny serivisy ampiasain'izy ireo dia mandrara mazava tsara ny fampiasana tsy manokana sy ny fitrandrahana ny angon-drakitra rehetra na ny ampahany amin'izany.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Koa rehefa nanomboka ny tetikasa Open Food Facts sy ny fampiharana finday izahay tamin'ny taona 2012, dia nanapa-kevitra ny hanao ny mifanohitra amin'izany mihitsy: tsy vitan'ny hoe mamela ny fampiasana sy ny fampiasana indray ny angon-drakitray izahay, maimaim-poana sy tsy misy sarany, ho an'ny rehetra sy ho an'ny fampiasana rehetra (anisan'izany ny ara-barotra), fa mamporisika izany koa izahay!

Ny fanomezana ho an'ny daholobe ny angon-drakitra (fantatra amin'ny hoe _open data_) dia ahafahan'ny tsirairay, fikambanana, orinasa, mpikaroka sns. avy amin'ny lafivalon'izao tontolo izao mieritreritra sy mampivelatra fampiharana ho an'ny angon-drakitra izay tsy ho noeritreretintsika mihitsy.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (jereo ny [Fepetra fampiasana indray](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Ny olon-drehetra (anisan'izany fa tsy voafetra amin'ireo mpandray anjara sy mpamorona ao amin'ny Open Food Facts) dia afaka mizara indray sy/na mampiasa indray ny angon-drakitra hanamboarana tranonkala, serivisy, rindrambaiko, fampiharana finday, na hanoratana lahatsoratra sy fanadihadiana. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Ohatra, maimaim-poana tanteraka ny sehatray ho an'ny mpamokatra, ary vao mainka faly izahay amin'izany satria mihevitra izahay fa mety hanampy azy ireo hanatsara ny vokatra vokariny izany.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Ireo mpandray anjara amin'ny Open Food Facts no manolotra ny fampahalalana sy ny angon-drakitra. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. ohatra, raha tena tsy mitovy amin'ny vokatra mitovy sokajy aminy ny mombamomba ny sakafo ao amin'ny vokatra iray, dia mety ho fahadisoana izany. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Raha mpamokatra ianao dia azafady [mifandraisa aminay](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (jereo ny [Fepetra momba ny fandraisana anjara](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Namorona sehatra maimaim-poana izahay ahafahanao manafatra sary sy angon-drakitra momba ny vokatrao mora foana, na firy na firy ny habeny. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Mifandraisa aminay raha te hiresaka momba ny fomba ahafahanay manohy: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
