Namorona tetikasa manokana ho an'ny kosmetika, ho an'ny sakafom-biby, ary koa ho an'ny vokatra hafa izahay: [Open Beauty Facts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Open Pet Food Facts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) ary [Open Products Facts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) tsirairay avy.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
