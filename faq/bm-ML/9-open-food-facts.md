## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. A bɛ se ka fɔ ko u ka baarakɛcogo sariyaw bɛ baara si bali k’a jɛya min tɛ mɔgɔ yɛrɛ ta ye ani ka kunnafoniw bɛɛ walima kunnafoniw yɔrɔ dɔw bɔ.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. O la, an ye Open Food Facts porozɛ ni mobili app daminɛ tuma min na, ka segin san 2012, an y’a latigɛ ka a kɔfɛ tigitigi kɛ : an tɛ sɔn dɔrɔn ka baara kɛ n’a ye ani ka baara kɛ n’a ye kokura an ka kunnafonidilanw na, hɔrɔnya la ani wari tɛ min na, bɛɛ ma ani baara bɛɛ kama (jago fana sen bɛ o la), nka an b’a jija fana !

Ka kunnafonidilanw kɛ foroba la (min bɛ wele ko _donan dafalenw_) o bɛ kɛ sababu ye mɔgɔ kelen-kelenw, tɔnw, tɔnw, ɲininikɛlaw wdfl ka bɔ diɲɛ fan bɛɛ la ka miiri ani ka baarakɛminɛnw labɛn kunnafonidilanw kama, siga t’a la ko an tun tɛna miiri minnu na abada.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (aw ye [Baarakɛcogo kura sariyaw](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse) lajɛ)

O cogo la, bɛɛ (Open Food Facts dɛmɛbagaw ni a dabɔbagaw fana sen bɛ o la, nka a dan tɛ o la) bɛ se ka kunnafoniw tila-tila kokura ani/walima ka baara kɛ n’u ye kokura walasa ka ɛntɛrinɛti yɔrɔw, baarakɛminɛnw, porogaramuw, telefɔni selilɛriw, walima ka barokunw ni kalanw sɛbɛn. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Misali la, an ka Plateforme pour les Producteurs ye fu ye pewu, wa o ka di an ye kosɛbɛ bawo an hakili la, o bɛ se k’u dɛmɛ k’u ka fɛn dilannenw ɲɛ.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Kunnafoni ni kunnafoniw bɛ di Open Food Facts dɛmɛbagaw fɛ. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. misali la ni fura dɔ balocogo tiɲɛnenw ni fɛn minnu bɛ sɔrɔ suguya kelen na, olu tɛ kelen ye kosɛbɛ, o bɛ se ka kɛ fili ye. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Ni aw ye fɛn dilannikɛla ye, aw bɛ se ka [anw sɔrɔ](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org) .

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (aw ye [Dɛmɛni sariyaw](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contributionlajɛ))

An ye yɔrɔ dɔ labɛn fu walasa i ka se ka fotow ni kunnafoniw don nɔgɔya la i ka fɛn dilannenw kan, i bonya mana kɛ min o min ye. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Aw ye ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ-anw fɛ walasa ka baro kɛ an bɛ se ka taa ɲɛ cogo min na: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
