Aw bɛ se ka ɲininkaliw kɛ walima ka hakilinaw di[forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)walima ka anw sɔrɔ e-mail fɛ:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

N’i ye fɛn dilabaga ye, i bɛ se ka bataki ci an ma producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Ni aw ye kunnafonidilaw ye, aw bɛ se ka bataki ci an ma presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Aw k’a kɔlɔsi: Open Food Facts ye jɛ-ka-baara kunnafonidilan ye min bɛ dumunifɛnw kan, an tɛ fɛn si feere ani an ni u dilannikɛlaw tɛ jɛɲɔgɔnya kɛ. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
