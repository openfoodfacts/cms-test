A bɛna
waati jan ta ka jɛkuluw bɛɛ tɔgɔ fɔ minnu bɛ u jigi da Dumuni Dabɔlenw ka tiɲɛw
kɛnɛ kan.

- Mobili baarakɛminɛn 100 ni kɔ
  bɛ lamɛnnikɛla caman mago ɲɛ : sukarodunbana (VeryDiab), faritanabana, dumuni
  balansi hakililata fɛ (FoodVisor), bololabaara, ani fɛn wɛrɛw. An ka
  kunnafonidilan fana ye sira Di Yuka dabɔli ma.
- Siti caman bɛ yen: CombienDeSucres.fr,
  CestEmballePresDeChezVous.fr...
- Donanw ka kunnafonidilaw : Les Décodeurs du Monde, BFM
  telewisɔn
- Ɲininikalaw ye dɔnniya
  barokun 100 ɲɔgɔn na
- Dumuniko bankiw ni jɛɲɔgɔnya magasinsinw : porozɛ dɔ
  Data For Good ka tɛmɛn porozɛ piloti dɔ kan min daminɛna
  Faransi Croix Rouge fɛ, jɛkafɔ kɛli dumuniko
  koperatifu fitinin dɔ ka wariko ɲɛnabɔli la.
- Kɔnpɔsitɛriw : Terraillon bɛ baara kɛ n’an ka kunnafoniw ye
  walasa ka ɲɛnamaya di a ka balansi jɛlenw ma.
- Porogaramu lakɔlisow (Simplon, Open
  Kalansow) ye Open Food Facts don ɲɔgɔn na i n’a fɔ porozɛ min bɛ dusu don u kɔnɔ u ka
  kalanbolodalen kɔnɔ.
