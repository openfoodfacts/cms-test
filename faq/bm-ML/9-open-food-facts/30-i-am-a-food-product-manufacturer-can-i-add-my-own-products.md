Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (aw ye sɛbɛn ɲɛ bolomafaraw lajɛ)

An ye yɔrɔ dɔ labɛn fu walasa i ka se ka fotow ni kunnafoniw don nɔgɔya la i ka fɛn dilannenw kan, i bonya mana kɛ min o min ye. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Aw ye ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ-anw fɛ walasa ka baro kɛ an bɛ se ka taa ɲɛ cogo min na: producers@openfoodfacts.org
