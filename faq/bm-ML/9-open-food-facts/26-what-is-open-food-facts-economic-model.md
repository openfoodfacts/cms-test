Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (aw ye Baarakɛcogo kura sariyaw lajɛ)

O cogo la, bɛɛ (Open Food Facts dɛmɛbagaw ni a dabɔbagaw fana sen bɛ o la, nka a dan tɛ o la) bɛ se ka kunnafoniw tila-tila kokura ani/walima ka baara kɛ n’u ye kokura walasa ka ɛntɛrinɛti yɔrɔw, baarakɛminɛnw, porogaramuw, telefɔni selilɛriw, walima ka barokunw ni kalanw sɛbɛn. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Misali la, an ka Plateforme pour les Producteurs ye fu ye pewu, wa o ka di an ye kosɛbɛ bawo an hakili la, o bɛ se k’u dɛmɛ k’u ka fɛn dilannenw ɲɛ.
