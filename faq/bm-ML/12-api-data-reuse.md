## Dumuni minnu tɛ barɔdi ye ?

Open Food Facts kɔnɔ, kunnafoniw dɔrɔn de bɛ sɔrɔ dumuni pakew kan. Ni aw b’a fɛ ka sɛnɛfɛnw (misali la, tomati walima bananku) ni dumunifɛn wɛrɛw nafa cɛmancɛ la, aw bɛ se ka baara kɛ ni jamana ka balodɛsɛko kunnafonidilan ofisiyali dɔ ye o nɔ na.

**Kɔlɔsili:** Nin lisi min bɛ duguma, jamana ka dumuniko kunnafonidilan nafama dɔw bɛ yen. Ni aw hakili la ko kunnafonidilan wɛrɛw ka kan ka don lisi kɔnɔ, aw bɛ se ka anw sɔrɔ nin yɔrɔ in na: [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact)

## Ne bɛ se ka kunnafoniw sɔrɔ/ka kunnafoniw lajɛ cogo di n ka porozew kama?

Open Food Facts ɲɛba kan, ekran sanfɛla kinin fɛ, menu dɔ bɛ yen min bɛ taa ɲɛ. A jukɔrɔ, i bɛna “ɲini kɔrɔlen” sugandi, i bɛ se ka min digi. O kɔfɛ, a bɛ i yɛrɛ de bolo k’a dɔn sariya minnu bɛ bɛn i ka poroze (poroze) ma kosɛbɛ. Ni i sugandira ka ban, i bɛ se ka jaabi sɔrɔlenw telesarse ni i ye i ɲɛsin duguma ɲɛ jukɔrɔ ani ka “Jaabiw telesarse” digi.

Aw bɛ se ka nin fana lajɛ:

- An ka API sɛbɛnw:[ https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/)
- Dumuni dafalenw ka baarakɛcogo sariyaw:[ ](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/baarakɛcogo sariyaw)
- An ka kunnafoniw kan:[ https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data](https://diɲɛ.openfoodfacts.org/data)

## Yala cogoyaw bɛ yen minnu bɛ se ka baara kɛ ni API ye wa?

Sɛbɛn minnu bɛ API baarakɛcogo kan, olu bɛɛ bɛ se ka sɔrɔ [API sɛbɛnni ɲɛ](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/), nka kumasen teliyalen filɛ nin ye:

- Dumuni dafalenw kunnafonisɛbɛn bɛ sɔrɔ i n’a fɔ kunnafoni dafalenw kunnafonidilan dafalen lase (ODbL) kɔnɔ, aw ye [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use) lajɛ walasa ka sariya kunnafoniw sɔrɔ. O sarati fla ye attribution (dafa) ni share-like (jatebɔ) ye. N’i ye kunnafoniw fara ɲɔgɔn kan ka bɔ Open Food Facts la ni kunnafonidilan wɛrɛw ye, o tuma na, ODbL b’a ɲini ko kunnafonidilan min bɛ sɔrɔ o la, o ka kan ka bɔ kɛnɛ kan i n’a fɔ kunnafonidilan dafalenw fana. O kɔrɔ fana ye ko i bɛ se ka kunnafoniw fara ɲɔgɔn kan dɔrɔn ni sɔrɔyɔrɔw ye minnu tun bɛna sira di o tila-tilacogo kura ma.

- I ka kan **tuma bɛɛ** ka baara kɛ ni User-Agent ladamulen ye ni i bɛ API weleli kɛ walasa k’i ka appli dɔn.

- Rate-limits bɛ waleya API labanyɔrɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ la.

## Ne bɛ se ka tariku kunnafoniw sɔrɔ cogo di?

Sisan, an tɛ tariku kunnafonidilanw (JSONL, MongoDB, CSV) di.

Nka, fɛn kelen-kelen bɛɛ kama, a bɛ se ka kɛ ka fɛn dilannenw kunnafoniw bɔcogo kɔrɔw sɔrɔ ni API ye walima fɛn dilannenw ɲɛ kan ni seginnkanniw ye.

Tuma o tuma ni fɛn dɔ bɛ ladamu, seginnkanni kura (jateden min bɛ caya ka daminɛ 1 la) bɛ dabɔ.
