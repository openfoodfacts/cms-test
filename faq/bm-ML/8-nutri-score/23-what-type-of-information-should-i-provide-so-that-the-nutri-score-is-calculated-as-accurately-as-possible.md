Nutri-Score jatebɔ bɛna kɛ ni aw ka fɛn dilannen balocogo tabali ye.

Aw kana ɲinɛ ka jiridenw, nakɔfɛnw ani nɔnɔmugu kɛmɛsarada la don aw ka tabali kɔnɔ, walasa Nutri-Score jatebɔlen kana kɛ tiɲɛ ye.
