Open Food Facts kɔnɔ, kunnafoniw dɔrɔn de bɛ sɔrɔ dumuni pakew kan. Ni aw b’a fɛ ka sɛnɛfɛnw (misali la, tomati walima bananku) ni dumunifɛn wɛrɛw nafa cɛmancɛ la, aw bɛ se ka baara kɛ ni jamana ka balodɛsɛko kunnafonidilan ofisiyali dɔ ye o nɔ na.

Kɔlɔsili: Nin lisi min bɛ duguma, jamana ka dumuniko kunnafonidilan nafama dɔw bɛ yen. Ni aw hakili la ko kunnafonidilan wɛrɛw ka kan ka don o lisi kɔnɔ, aw bɛ se ka anw sɔrɔ nin yɔrɔ in na: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact

Jamana ka dumuniko kunnafonidilanw lisɛli

- Ɔsitarali - FSANZ - NUTTAB 2006: https://www.dumunikɛcogo.gov.au/media/documents/FSANZ%20Conf%20PostersNUTTAB.pdf
- Bɛliziki - NUBEL - Bɛliziki dumuniw labɛncogo kunnafoniw: https://www.internubel.be
- Kanada - FCEN: https://banakisɛ-balocogo.kanada.ca/cnf-fce/index-fra.jsp
- Tcheki jamana - Dumuniko hakɛ kunnafonidilan min bɛ jamana ka kɛnɛyako baarada la: http://www.chpr.szu.cz/dbdata/foodcomp/nut2001.asp
- Danemarke - Danmakɛ ka dumuniw labɛncogo kunnafonidilan: https://frida.fooddata.dk/?lang=en
- Ɛsitoni - Ɛsitoni dumuniw labɛncogo kunnafonidilan: https://tka.nutridata.ee/en/
- Finlande - Finlande dumuniw labɛncogo kunnafonidilan - FINELI: https://fineli.fi/fineli/en/index
- Faransi - CIQUAL: https://www.anses.fr/en/search/site/Taabolo%20cɲɔgɔn
- Alimanjamana - Souci-Fachmann-Kraut Ɛntɛrinɛti kunnafonidilan: https://www.sfk.online/#/home walima Alimanjamana ka kunnafonidilan ofisiyali: Bundeslebensmittelschlüssel: https://blsdb.de/
- Itali - Banka Dati di Composizione degli Alimenti CREA: https://www.crea.gov.it/web/alimenti-e-nutrizione/banche-dati
- Pays-Bas - Hollande dumuniw labɛncogo kunnafonidilan: https://www.rivm.nl/en/dutch-food-composition-database
- Norvege - Norvege dumuniw danfara taabolow 2006: https://www.matvaretabellen.no/?language=en
- Pologne - Dumuni kɛcogo taabolow: http://www.izz.waw.pl/en/?lang=en
- Ɛsipaɲi - Ɛsipaɲi dumuniw danfara kunnafonisɛbɛn - BEDCA: https://www.bedca.net/bdpub/index.php
- Suwisi - Suwisi jamana ka dumunifɛnw hakɛ kunnafonidilan: https://www.naehrwertdaten.ch/de/
- Angletɛri - Dumunifɛnw danfaralenw ka kunnafonidilan (CoFID): https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/composition-of-foods-integrated-dataset-cofid
- Ameriki - USDA: https://ndb.nal.usda.gov/ Kalanjɛ ni sɛbɛnni: kalanjɛ ni sɛbɛnni gafew.
