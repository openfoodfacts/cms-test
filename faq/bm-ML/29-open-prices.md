## Ne ye feerekɛla ye wa ne b’a fɛ ka sɔngɔw di. Ne bɛ se k’o kɛ cogo di?

Aw bɛ se ka sɔngɔw di ni [an ka API](https://prices.openfoodfacts.org/api/docs) ye. N’aw b’a fɛ ka sɔngɔw di sɛgɛsɛgɛli la, aw bɛ se ka kuma an fɛ [contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org).

---

## Yala aw bɛ jateminɛ kɛ ka sɔngɔw bɔ feerekɛlaw ka sitiw kan wa?

Sariya ni fɛɛrɛko siratigɛ la, an t’a jate ko sɔngɔw bɔli feerekɛlaw ka sitiw kan, k’o kɛ fɛɛrɛ ɲuman ye walasa ka dɛmɛ don Open Prices la. An b’a fɛ k’a lajɛ ko an bɛ sɔngɔ minnu lajɛ, olu ye tiɲɛ ye, wa u bɛ waati la, wa sɔngɔ sɔgɔlenw sɔrɔli ka bɔ bolomafaraw la, o t’a to an k’o kɛ.

Price scraping is a considered option in a future version of Open Prices, but it would be done by Open Prices itself so that we can have a proof of the price based on the HTML page.

---

## Mun na Open Food Facts bɛ ka nin kɛ (Open Prices)?

Sɔngɔ kunnafoniw nafa ka bon kosɛbɛ walasa ka dumuniko siratigɛw faamuya. O ye fɛnba ye dumuni musaka faamuyali la ani ka dumuni nafamaw sabati. Sɔngɔko kunnafoniw dabɔli ye fɛɛrɛ ye walasa ɲininikɛlaw, kunnafonidilaw ani jamanadenw ka se ka faamuyali ɲuman sɔrɔ dumuni sɔngɔw bɛ ɲɔgɔn ta cogo min na dugukoloko siratigɛ la ani waati kɔnɔ.
