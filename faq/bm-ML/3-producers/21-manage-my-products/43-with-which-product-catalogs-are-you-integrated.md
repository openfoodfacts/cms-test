Sisan, fɛn dilannikɛlaw bɛ se k’u ka fɛn dilannenw kunnafoniw n’u ka fotow ci waati yɛrɛ la ka bɔ Equadis, AGENA3000 ani BAYARD ladilikanw na ka taa Open Food Facts ma.

Siga t’a la, i bɛna nafa sɔrɔ fana labɛncogo kura ladilikanw na ani plateforme in kɔnɔfɛnw bɛɛ la.

An ka bulɔgu sɛbɛn bɛ fɛɛrɛw ɲɛfɔ minnu ka kan ka tugu n’i ye EQUADIS ka kiliyan ye.

N'i ye AGENA3000 ka kiliyan ye (Product A3 PIM INDUSTRY), i ka kan ka "Open Food Facts" sɔrɔbaga sugandi dɔrɔn n'i bɛ i ka fɛn dilannenw sɛbɛnw ci. Kunnafoni wɛrɛw bɛ sɔrɔ nin bulɔgu in kan.

N’i ye fɛn dilannenw kunnafonidilan wɛrɛ ka kiliyan ye (Salsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, wdfl) aw k’a fɔ an ye producers@openfoodfacts.org kan

→ Walasa ka kunnafoni wɛrɛw sɔrɔ, an b’aw wele aw ka an ka baarakɛcogo gafe telesarse ani/walima aw ka an ka wideyow kalanw lajɛ :-)
