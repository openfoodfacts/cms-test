An ye porozɛ kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw da ɲɛgɛnw kama, baganw balo kama, ka fara fɛn wɛrɛw kan:[Open Beauty Facts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/),[Open Pet Food Facts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)ani[Open Products Facts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/)o cogo kelen na.
