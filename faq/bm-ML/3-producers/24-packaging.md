## Ne bɛ se ka n ka pakew kunnafoniw fara Open Food Facts kan cogo di?

I n’a fɔ fɛn dilannikɛla, fɛɛrɛ ɲuman min bɛ se ka kɛ ka pakew kunnafoniw fara i ka fɛn dilannenw kan, o ye ka kunnafoni wajibiyalenw dafa nin [excel jatebɔsɛbɛn](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) kɔnɔ, ka bɔ kulu "AU" kɔnɔ.

Foroko yɔrɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ kama, aw bɛna a ɲini ka nin sɛbɛn:

- Unit hakɛ (misali la: **4** nɔnɔmugu dagaw) .

- A cogoya (misali la: \*\* daga, da, kɛsu, wdfl\*\*)

- Fɛn min bɛ kɛ (ex: **PET, papier, verre, etc**)

- Recyclage instruction (misali la: **ka recyclage, ka fili, etc**) .

- Unit lankolon kelen girinya (gr kɔnɔ) .

- Fɛn min bɛ sɔrɔ fɛn kelen na, o hakɛ jɛlen

O kɔfɛ, nin jatebɔsɛbɛn in bɛ se ka don an ka plateforme kɔnɔ min bilalen bɛ baarakɛlaw ye.

---

## Porozɛ min bɛ wele ko Tackling Food Packaging, o ye mun ye?

"Tackling Food Packaging" porozɛ in ye kunnafoni caman lajɛli ye fɛnw foroko kan minnu bɛ dun kosɛbɛ (u cogoya, u kɛcogo, u suguya jiracogo, u girinya, u seko).

Ka da dɔnniya siratigɛ la, min bɛ kɛ ni mɔgɔw sendonni ye, a bɛna a jigi da tɔnw ka sɔnni kan k’u ka pakew kunnafoniw tila ɲɔgɔn na ani ka kɛnɛya tulon kɛ, ani jamanadenw ka sira di kunnafoniw lajɛli ma fɛn dilannenw kan. O kunnafoniw bɛna baara kɛ kokura hɔrɔnya la ("donan dafalenw").

**Ni aw b’a fɛ ka kunnafoni wɛrɛw sɔrɔ:** [https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/packaging-projet-producers](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/packaging-projet-producers)

---

## Hali ni Tackling Food Packaging porozɛ banna, yala n bɛ se ka n ka pakew kunnafoniw fara a kan hali bi wa?

Hali porozɛ in bannen kɔfɛ, a bɛ se ka kɛ ka pakew kunnafoniw fara a kan bawo pakew forow bɛna to an ka siti kan.
