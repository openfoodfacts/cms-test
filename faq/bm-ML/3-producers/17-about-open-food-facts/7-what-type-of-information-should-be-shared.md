Kunnafoni fɛn o fɛn bɛ aw bolo, o bɛ sɔn, k’a ta balodɛsɛ kunnafoniw na ka se pakew kunnafoniw ma.

O b’a to a bɛ se ka kɛ ka dosiye dafalen dɔ sigi senkan, ka jatew bɛɛ jate ni tigitigi ye ani ka caya, kɛnɛya min bɛ waleɲumandɔn feerekɛlaw fɛ.

Nka an b’a ɲini aw fɛ aw ka nin foro ninnu bila jɔyɔrɔ fɔlɔ la:

- Nutritional information
- Fɛn dilannenw suguya (misali la, minfɛnw, foromazi, nɔnɔmugu, a ɲɔgɔnnaw) .
- Fɛn min bɛ dilan, o tɔgɔ
- Allergens
- ani a kɔnɔfɛnw, nin foro in tɛ wajibi ye nka a bɛ fɔ walasa ka baara kɛcogo ɲuman sɔrɔ ani ka jatew latigɛ

→ Walasa ka kunnafoni wɛrɛw sɔrɔ, an b’aw wele aw ka an ka baarakɛcogo gafe telesarse ani/walima aw ka an ka wideyow kalanw lajɛ :-)
