I n'a fɔ fɛn dilannikɛla, fɛɛrɛ ɲuman min bɛ se ka kɛ ka pakew kunnafoniw fara i ka fɛn dilannenw kan, o ye ka kunnafoni wajibiyalenw dafa nin excel jatebɔsɛbɛn in kɔnɔ, ka bɔ "AU" kulu la.

Foroko yɔrɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ kama, aw bɛna a ɲini ka nin sɛbɛn:

- Unit hakɛ (misali la: nɔnɔmugu daga 4) .

- A cogoya (misali la: daga, datugulan, kɛsu, wdfl) .

- Fɛn min bɛ kɛ (misali la: PET, papier, gilasi, wdfl) .

- Ladilikan min bɛ kɛ ka fɛnw lasegin u cogo kɔrɔ la (misali la: ka fɛnw lasegin, ka fɛnw fili, a ɲɔgɔnnaw) .

- Unit lankolon kelen girinya (gr kɔnɔ) .

- Fɛn min bɛ sɔrɔ fɛn kelen na, o hakɛ jɛlen

O kɔfɛ, nin jatebɔsɛbɛn in bɛ se ka don an ka plateforme kɔnɔ min bilalen bɛ baarakɛlaw ye.
