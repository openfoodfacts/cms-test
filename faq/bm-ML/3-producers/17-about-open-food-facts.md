## Kunnafoni sugu jumɛnw ka kan ka tila ɲɔgɔn na?

Kunnafoni fɛn o fɛn bɛ aw bolo, o bɛ sɔn, k’a ta balodɛsɛ kunnafoniw na ka se pakew kunnafoniw ma.

O b’a to a bɛ se ka kɛ ka dosiye dafalen dɔ sigi sen kan, ka jatew bɛɛ jate ni tigitigi ye ani ka caya, kɛnɛya min bɛ waleɲumandɔn feerekɛlaw fɛ.

Nka an b’a ɲini aw fɛ aw ka nin foro ninnu bila jɔyɔrɔ fɔlɔ la:

- Balocogo kunnafoniw

- Fɛn dilannenw suguya (misali la, minfɛnw, foromazi, nɔnɔmugu, a ɲɔgɔnnaw) .

- Fɛn min bɛ dilan, o tɔgɔ

- Alɛrijini (alrégènes).

- ani a kɔnɔfɛnw, nin foro in tɛ wajibi ye nka a bɛ fɔ walasa ka baara kɛcogo ɲuman sɔrɔ ani ka jatew latigɛ

→ Walasa ka kunnafoni wɛrɛw sɔrɔ, an b’aw wele aw ka an ka [gafe min bɛ baara kɛ ni baarakɛminɛnw](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) telesarse ani/walima aw ka an ka [videyo kalanw](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Yala ne bɛ se ka Open Food Facts kunnafoniw telesarse wa?

Aw bɛ se ka kunnafoniw bɛɛ telesarse minnu bɛ sɔrɔ Open Food Facts kan. Aw k’aw janto, o bɛ disiki yɔrɔ caman de wajibiya, ɔridinatɛri barikama n’i b’a fɛ ka baara kɛ ni jatebɔsɛbɛn ye walima ka R walima Python dɔn cogo wɛrɛ la.

Aw bɛ se fana ka kunnafonidilanw kulu fitinin dɔ telesarse (aw ka marka misali la) ɲinini ɲɛtaa sababu fɛ.

→ Walasa ka kunnafoni wɛrɛw sɔrɔ, an b’aw wele aw ka an ka [gafe min bɛ baara kɛ ni baarakɛminɛnw](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) telesarse ani/walima aw ka an ka [videyo kalanw](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Yala Pro platform sɔrɔli ni kunnafoniw donli tɛ fu wa?

Tiɲɛ na, wa a bɛna kɛ fo abada.Open Food Facts bɛ baara minnu kɛ, olu bɛɛ ye fu ye.  
An nisɔndiyara kosɛbɛ k’a ye ko aw y’a latigɛ k’aw ka fɛn dilannenw kunnafoniw di dumuniko jɛlen ma, wa an b’aw fo o la.
