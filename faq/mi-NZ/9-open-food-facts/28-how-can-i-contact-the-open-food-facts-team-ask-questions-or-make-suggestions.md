Ka taea e koe te pātai, te tuku whakaaro rānei i runga i te [huinga kōrero](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)me te whakapā mai rānei mā te īmēra: [contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Mena he kaihanga koe, tēnā īmēra mai ki a mātou ki producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Mena he kairīpoata koe, tēnā īmēra mai ki a mātou ki presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Kia mōhio mai: He pātengi raraunga mahi tahi a Open Food Facts mō ngā hua kai, kāore mātou e hoko i tētahi hua, ā, kāore hoki he hononga ki ngā kaihanga o aua hua. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
