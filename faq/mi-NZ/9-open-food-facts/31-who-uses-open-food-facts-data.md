It would
take too long to list all the organizations that rely on the Open Food Facts
platform.

- Neke atu i te 100 ngā taupānga pūkoro
  e aro ana ki ngā hiahia o te tini o ngā hunga whakarongo: te mate huka (VeryDiab), ngā mate pāwera, te taurite kai
  mā te matauranga horihori (FoodVisor), ngā hauātanga, me ētahi atu. I taea hoki te waihanga i a Yuka nā tā mātou pātengi raraunga
  .
- He maha ngā paetukutuku: CombienDeSucres.fr,
  CestEmballePresDeChezVous.fr...
- Raraunga-kairipoata : Les Décodeurs du Monde, BFM
  TV
- Ngā kairangahau i roto i ngā tuhinga pūtaiao tata ki te 100
- Ngā peeke kai me ngā toa tautoko kotahitanga: he kaupapa
  Raraunga Mō te Pai hei kaupapa tonu mō tētahi kaupapa whakamātautau i tīmatahia e te
  Ripeka Whero Wīwī, he whakaurunga ki te whakahaere moni o tētahi rōpū kai iti
  .
- Ngā Kamupene: Ka whakamahia e Terraillon ā mātou raraunga
  hei whakaora i ōna tauine hono.
- Kua whakauruhia e ngā kura kaupapa (Simplon, Open
  Classrooms) ngā Open Food Facts hei kaupapa whakahihiri ki roto i tā rātou marautanga
  .
