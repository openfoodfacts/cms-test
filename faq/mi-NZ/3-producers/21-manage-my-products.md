## He aha te mea e whakamana ana i te kounga o ngā raraunga i roto i te Open Food Facts?

**Hei whakapai tonu i te kounga o ngā raraunga, e whā ngā pou e whakawhirinaki ana mātou:**

1. Kei te tāpiri tonu te hapori i ngā raraunga me ngā whakaahua, ā, he tino kaiārahi rātou o ngā kaiarotake.

2. Kua whakatakotohia e mātou ngā ture arorau hei tautuhi i ngā hapa kei runga i ngā pepa hua.Hei tauira: mēnā he nui ake te taumaha o ngā kai i te taumaha katoa o te hua, he rerekētanga tērā. E 50 pea ngā tirotiro kounga kei a mātou.

3. Nā te Ako Mīhini, ka whakawhāitihia e mātou te mōrearea o ngā hapa tāuru. Mā tā mātou hangarau ka taea e mātou te tango raraunga tuhinga mai i ngā whakaahua i tangohia e ngā kaituku kōrero.

4. Nā te tautoko a Santé Publique France, i whakawhanakehia e mātou he tomokanga e āhei ai ngā kaihanga ki te tuku ake i ā rātou raraunga i hangaia mō te papatipu, ā, mā reira ka whakatika/whakaoti i ngā takoha a te hapori.

## Me pēhea te tāpiri i ngā hua?

Hei tāpiri i ngā hua ki tā mātou pūhara, me whakatau tuatahi koe me pēhea te haere tonu. E whā ngā kōwhiringa:

- **Ā-ringa**: ki te mea he iti āu hua, ka taea tonu te waihanga ā-ringa i ia pepa hua i runga i te Pro Platform.

- **Mā te kawemai i tētahi ripanga tātai**: ka taea e koe te tango i te ripanga tātai [tauira](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl) i whakaratohia e Open Food Facts, ka taea rānei te kawemai tika i tētahi tangohanga mai i tō pūnaha IT ki te tūāpapa Pro.

- **Mā ngā hononga o ngā rarangi hua**. Mena he kiritaki koe nō EQUADIS, AGENA3000, BAYARD rānei, ka taea e koe te tukuake raraunga ki Open Food Facts mā te pāwhiri kotahi. Mena kei te mahi koe me tētahi atu PIM (Whakahaere Mōhiohio Hua), tēnā whakapā mai ki a mātou: producers@openfoodfacts.org

- **Mā te hononga kua whakaritea** i waenganui i tō kamupene me Open Food Facts. Mena he nui atu i te 1000 ngā hua kei roto i tō putea hokohoko, ka taea e tāua te matapaki i te whai wāhitanga ki te hanga i tētahi hononga motuhake hei whakahaere i te whakawhiti raraunga.

Mō ētahi atu kōrero, ka tono atu mātou kia tikiake koe i tā mātou [aratohu kaiwhakamahi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) me/ranei tirohia ā mātou [akoranga ataata](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Is it possible to delete products that are no longer sold?

Ki te hiahia koe ki te tango i tētahi hua kāore e wātea ana i te mākete, me tohu noa e koe te pouaka "kāore tēnei hua e hokona ana" ina whakatika koe i tō pepa hua.

Ki te hiahia koe ki te tango i ētahi hua, ka tūtohu mātou kia whai koe i te tukanga kua whakahuatia i roto i te [aratohu kaiwhakamahi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Platform-User-Guide.pdf), i te whārangi 18.

Ka taea hoki e koe te whakapā mai ki a mātou mā te pae tukutuku producers@openfoodfacts.org mēnā he pātai āu.

Ina purangatia te hua, kāore e puta i roto i te Open Food Facts.Heoi, ka noho tonu ki roto i tā mātou pātengi raraunga kia taea ai e mātou te pupuri i te hītori o ngā hua kua hokona i roto i te wā.

## He mea nui ake ngā mōhiohio a te kamupene i ngā mōhiohio kua wātea kē i runga i te tūāpapa?

Ina tuku te kaihanga i tētahi o ana hua ki te ipurangi me tana pūkete kaihanga, ā, kua wātea kē he pepa hua, ka oti i tēnei.

Ko ngā mōhiohio ka tukuna e te kaihanga ka noho hei kaupapa matua, ā, ko ia anake ka taea te whakarerekē i ana hua ina oti ngā mōhiohio te whakakī.

Mena he wehewehe ngā raraunga i tukuna mā te tūāpapa, mā ngā mōhiohio i tāpirihia e te hapori mai i te takai pea e whakaoti.

## I roto i tēhea hōputu me tuku atu aku raraunga?

Nā Open Food Facts i whakarato te [tauira](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/generate_sample_import_file.pl).

Heoi, ka taea te kawemai i ngā hōputu ripanga tātai katoa (xlsx, csv) ki te tūāpapa.  
Kua hangaia tā mātou pūnaha hei taurite aunoa i ngā huanga o tō kōnae, ki ngā mea kei roto i te Open Food Facts. Kia tūpato ki te tirotiro anō i te wāhanga taurite i te mea me whakarerekē ā-ringa pea ētahi o aua mea.

Mena kei te wehewehea ō mōhiohio i waenga i ētahi ripanga tātai, ka taea e koe te kawemai i aua mea i runga i te rārangi - me whakarite kia taurite mēnā ka huri te hōputu.

Ka taea hoki e koe te kawemai i tētahi kōnae Excel hei tāpiri atu ki tētahi kawemai EQUADIS, AGENA3000 rānei, mēnā kāore anō kia tae mai ngā raraunga ki roto i tō pūnaha mōhiohio. I ēnei āhuatanga, kaua e wareware ki te tono kia kaweake ki te pātengi raraunga tūmatanui mai i te atanga.

→ Mō ētahi atu kōrero, ka tono atu mātou kia tikiake koe i tā mātou [aratohu kaiwhakamahi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) me/ranei tirohia ā mātou [akoranga ataata](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Ko ēhea ngā rārangi hua e hono ana koe?

Ka taea e ngā kaihanga te tuku raraunga me ngā whakaahua o ā rātou hua i te wā tonu mai i **Equadis,** **AGENA3000 me BAYARD consulting** ki Open Food Facts.

Ka whai hua anō hoki koe i ngā whakaaro whakahou me ngā āhuatanga katoa o te tūāpapa.

[Tā mātou pou rangitaki](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/real-time-product-data-from-producers-on-open-food-facts-thanks-to-the-new-equadis-integration) e whakamārama ana i ngā mahi hei whai mēnā he kiritaki EQUADIS koe.

Mena he kiritaki AGENA3000 koe (Hua A3 PIM INDUSTRY), me tīpako noa e koe te kaiwhiwhi "Open Food Facts" ina tukuna ana ō pepa hua. [Ētahi atu kōrero mō tēnei pou rangitaki](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/news/share-your-product-data-in-1-click-with-the-new-agena3000-connector).

Mena he kaihoko koe nō tētahi atu putumōhio raraunga hua (Salsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, me ētahi atu) tēnā whakamōhio mai ki a mātou ki [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

→ Mō ētahi atu kōrero, ka tono atu mātou kia tikiake koe i tā mātou [aratohu kaiwhakamahi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) me/ranei tirohia ā mātou [akoranga ataata](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)

## Can animal products, non-food products, cosmetics and other products be integrated into Open Food Facts?

Kua waihangahia e mātou ētahi kaupapa motuhake mō ngā mea whakapaipai, mō te whāngai kararehe, me ētahi atu hua anō hoki: Ngā Mea Tuwhera mō te Ataahua, Ngā Mea Tuwhera mō te Kai Mōkai me Ngā Mea Tuwhera mō ngā Hua.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.

## Kei te paetukutuku Open Food Facts ngā raraunga hua o taku kamupene. Ka taea te tango i te mana whakahaere o te pūkete kaihanga e pā ana?

Āe, koia rā! Ka taea e koe te whakahaere i te pūkete kaihanga e pā ana mā te waihanga i tētahi pūkete Open Food Facts me te wāhitau o tō pakihi. Ina rēhita koe, whakahuatia te ingoa o te whakahaere kei runga i te puka kia taea ai e koe te uru atu ki te wāhi kaihanga e tika ana.

Me whai wā whakamana poto hei whakarite ko koe tonu te kaihanga.

Ka taea e koe te whakaoti i ngā raraunga, te tāpiri whakaahua, te tiki aunoa i ngā taunakitanga hei whakapai ake i te Nutri-Score, me te maha atu anō. He tino kore utu tēnei katoa. Ka taea hoki e koe te hono atu i tētahi pūnaha whakahaere raraunga hua tuatoru pērā i a EQUADIS, AGENA3000 me Bayard consulting.

## Kia pēhea te roa ka whakahoutia ngā raraunga a ngā kaihanga i runga i tētahi atu taupānga e whakamahi ana i te pātengi raraunga Open Food Facts?

## Mā te kawemai i ngā raraunga mai i tētahi pūkete hua ka oti, ka tuhirua rānei te pūkete hua o nāianei?

Ka whakamahia e mātou te waehere pae hei tautuhi i ngā hua, ki te kawemai koe i tētahi pūkete me te waehere pae ōrite ki tētahi pūkete o mua, ka hanumihia ngā mōhiohio.

Mō ngā raraunga pēnei i te rārangi kai, ngā uara kai (kotahi anake te uara tika e taea ana), ka tuhiruatia ngā raraunga o nāianei e ngā raraunga i tukuna mā te tūāpapa kaihanga.

Mō ngā raraunga pēnei i ngā tapanga/kāwai/waitohu (he maha ngā uara tika ka taea), ka hanumihia ngā mōhiohio. Mena he hē ngā raraunga kei runga i te tūāpapa tūmatanui, ka taea e koe te whakarerekē i te pepa hua kei runga i te tūāpapa tūmatanui hei tango i ngā uara hē.

→ Mō ētahi atu kōrero, ka tono atu mātou kia tikiake koe i tā mātou [aratohu kaiwhakamahi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) me/ranei tirohia ā mātou [akoranga ataata](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55) :-)
