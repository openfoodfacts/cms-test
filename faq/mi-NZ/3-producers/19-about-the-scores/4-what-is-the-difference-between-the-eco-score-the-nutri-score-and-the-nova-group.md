Ko te whāinga o ēnei tohu he whakarato i ngā mōhiohio e māmā ai te mārama ki te kaihoko.

Ko te rōpū NOVA e aro ana ki te whakarerekētanga o te hua, te Eco-Score me ōna pānga ki te taiao, me te Nutri-Score me ngā huānga kai, me te mōhio ko te nui ake o ngā kaute o tētahi hua e tata ana ki te A, ka nui ake te kounga.

Kāore ngā kaute e toru e pā ana ki ngā huānga ōrite. Nō reira, kāore e taea te whakataurite i a rātou tetahi ki tetahi.
