## Ko wai te kawenga mō te tatau i te tapuwae me/ranei te kaute?

Ka tatauhia te Nutri-Score, te rōpū NOVA me te Eco-Score i runga i ngā raraunga kei roto i te Open Food Facts. E tino whai ana ā mātou rauropi i ngā tikanga whaimana mō ēnei kaute.

---

## He aha te rerekētanga i waenga i te Eco-Score, te Nutri-Score me te rōpū NOVA?

Ko te whāinga o ēnei tohu he whakarato i ngā mōhiohio e māmā ai te mārama ki te kaihoko.

Ko te rōpū [NOVA](https://fr.openfoodfacts.org/nova) e pā ana ki te panonitanga o te hua, ko te [Eco-Score](https://fr.openfoodfacts.org/ecoscore) me ōna pānga ki te taiao, me te [Nutri-Score](https://fr.openfoodfacts.org/nutriscore) me ngā huānga kai, me te mōhio ko te nui ake o ngā kaute o tētahi hua e tata ana ki te A, ka nui ake tōna kounga.

Kāore ngā kaute e toru e pā ana ki ngā huānga ōrite. Nō reira, kāore e taea te whakataurite i a rātou tetahi ki tetahi.

---

## Ka taea te whakatauira i te Eco-Score, i te Nutri-Score rānei o aku hua?

Mā te rēhita ki te tūāpapa o ngā kaihanga me te tukuake i ngā raraunga o ō hua, ka taea e koe te tatau i te Nutri-Score me te Eco-Score e pā ana ki ō hua.



Heoi anō, me muku e koe ina oti te whakatauira kia kore ai e kaweake ki te tūāpapa tūmatanui me ngā hua kua hokohokohia kētia i roto i tō awhe.
