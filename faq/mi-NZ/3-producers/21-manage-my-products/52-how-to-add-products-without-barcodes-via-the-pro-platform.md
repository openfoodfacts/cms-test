Mō ngā hua kāore he waeherepae, kei runga i te pae tukutuku, kei runga rānei i te tūāpapa kaihanga, kei reira he pātene "Hua kāore he waeherepae" kei te pou maui e taea ai e koe te tāpiri atu. Kātahi ka hangaia aunoatia he tohu tautuhi.
