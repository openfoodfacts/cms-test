He mea nui te raupapatanga o ngā kai\*\*\*\*: e tohu ana i te raupapatanga mā te rahinga.
Ko te huarahi pai ki te whakakī i ngā kai ko te whai i ngā mea kua tuhia ki runga i te takai. Koia te mea nui kia tāia ngā whakaahua o ngā kai.
