Ka taea e koe te tuku whakaahua engari i roto i te mahi ka tīpakohia aunoa te whakaahua matua, ka tukuna ngā whakaahua kē atu, engari kāore e tapahia/tīpakohia mō ngā kai, ngā matūkai me ētahi atu.
