Ka taea e ngā kaihanga te tuku raraunga me ngā whakaahua o ā rātou hua i te wā tonu mai i a Equadis, AGENA3000 me BAYARD consulting ki a Open Food Facts.

Ka whai hua anō hoki koe i ngā whakaaro whakahou me ngā āhuatanga katoa o te tūāpapa.

E whakamārama ana tā mātou pou rangitaki i ngā mahi hei whai māu mēnā he kiritaki EQUADIS koe.

Mena he kiritaki AGENA3000 koe (Hua A3 PIM INDUSTRY), me tīpako noa e koe te kaiwhiwhi "Open Food Facts" ina tukuna ana ō pepa hua. He kōrero anō mō tēnei pou rangitaki.

Mena he kaihoko koe nō tētahi atu putumōhio raraunga hua (Salsify - ex-Alkemics, ConsoTrust, 1worldSync, Akeneo, PIMWorks, me ētahi atu) tēnā whakamōhio mai ki a mātou ki producers@openfoodfacts.org

→ Mō ētahi atu kōrero, ka tono atu mātou kia tangohia mai e koe tā mātou aratohu kaiwhakamahi, kia tirohia rānei ā mātou akoranga ataata :-)
