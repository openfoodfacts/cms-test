Kua waihangahia e mātou ngā kaupapa motuhake mō ngā mea whakapaipai, mō te whāngai kararehe, me ētahi atu hua anō hoki: [Ngā Mōhiotanga Ataahua Tuwhera](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/), [Ngā Mōhiotanga Kai Mōkai Tuwhera](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/) me [Ngā Mōhiotanga Hua Tuwhera](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) ia.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
