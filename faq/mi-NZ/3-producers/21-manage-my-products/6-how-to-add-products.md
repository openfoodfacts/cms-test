Hei tāpiri i ngā hua ki tā mātou pūhara, me whakatau tuatahi koe me pēhea te haere tonu. E whā ngā kōwhiringa:

- Ā-ringa: ki te mea he iti āu hua, ka taea tonu te waihanga ā-ringa i ia pepa hua i runga i te Pro Platform.

- Mā te kawemai i te ripanga tātai: ka taea e koe te tango i te tauira ripanga tātai i whakaratohia e Open Food Facts, ka taea rānei te kawemai tika i tētahi tangohanga mai i tō pūnaha IT ki te tūāpapa Pro.

- Mā ngā hononga o ngā rarangi hua. Mena he kiritaki koe nō EQUADIS, AGENA3000, BAYARD rānei, ka taea e koe te tukuake raraunga ki Open Food Facts mā te pāwhiri kotahi. Mena kei te mahi koe me tētahi atu PIM (Whakahaere Mōhiohio Hua), tēnā whakapā mai ki a mātou: producers@openfoodfacts.org

- Mā te hononga kua whakaritea i waenga i tō kamupene me Open Food Facts. Mena kei roto i tō putea he neke atu i te 1000 ngā hua, ka taea e tātou te matapaki i te whai wāhitanga ki te hanga i tētahi hononga motuhake hei whakahaere i te whakawhiti raraunga. Mō ētahi atu kōrero, ka tono atu mātou kia tangohia e koe tā mātou aratohu kaiwhakamahi me/ranei tirohia ā mātou akoranga ataata :-)
