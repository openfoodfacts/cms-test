Ina tuku te kaihanga i tētahi o ana hua ki te ipurangi me tana pūkete kaihanga, ā, kua wātea kē he pepa hua, ka oti i tēnei.

Ko ngā mōhiohio ka tukuna e te kaihanga ka noho hei kaupapa matua, ā, ko ia anake ka taea te whakarerekē i ana hua ina oti ngā mōhiohio te whakakī.

Mena he wehewehe ngā raraunga i tukuna mā te tūāpapa, mā ngā mōhiohio i tāpirihia e te hapori mai i te takai pea e whakaoti.
