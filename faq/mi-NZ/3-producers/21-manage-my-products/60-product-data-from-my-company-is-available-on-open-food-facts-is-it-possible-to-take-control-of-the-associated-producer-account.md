Āe, koia rā! Ka taea e koe te whakahaere i te pūkete kaihanga e pā ana mā te waihanga i tētahi pūkete Open Food Facts me te wāhitau o tō pakihi. Ina rēhita koe, whakahuatia te ingoa o te whakahaere kei runga i te puka kia taea ai e koe te uru atu ki te wāhi kaihanga e tika ana.

Me whai wā whakamana poto hei whakarite ko koe tonu te kaihanga.

Ka taea e koe te whakaoti i ngā raraunga, te tāpiri whakaahua, te tiki aunoa i ngā taunakitanga hei whakapai ake i te Nutri-Score, me te maha atu anō. He tino kore utu tēnei katoa. Ka taea hoki e koe te hono atu i tētahi pūnaha whakahaere raraunga hua tuatoru pērā i a EQUADIS, AGENA3000 me Bayard consulting.
