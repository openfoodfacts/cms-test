Ko te Nutri-Score kei te whakahaeretia e Santé Publique France. E whakarāpopoto ana i te ripanga o ngā uara kai kia mārama ai te katoa. Ko te whāinga kia wātea ki te katoa ngā mōhiotanga kai mārama, e mārama ana hoki.
