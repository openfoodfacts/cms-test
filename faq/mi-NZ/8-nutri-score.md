## Me aha ahau mēnā kāore i te tika te Nutri-Score o aku hua?

Mēnā he raru kei tō Nutri-Score, tirohia mēnā he tika ō mōhiohio i te ripa "whakarerekē" o tō pepa hua. Mena kei te ngaro, kei te hē rānei tetahi o ngā mōhiohio, kaua e mangere ki te whakapā mai ki a mātou i te \*\* producers@openfoodfacts.org\*\*

---

## He aha ngā momo mōhiohio me whakarato e au kia tika ai te tatau i te Nutri-Score?

Ka tātaihia te Nutri-Score mā te whakamahi i te ripanga kai o tō hua.

Kaua e wareware ki te tāpiri i te ōrau o ngā hua rākau, huawhenua me ngā nati ki tō ripanga, kia kore ai e hē te Nutri-Score i tatauhia.

---

## Nō hea te Nutri-Score?

Ko te Nutri-Score kei te whakahaeretia e Santé Publique France. E whakarāpopoto ana i te ripanga o ngā uara kai kia mārama ai te katoa. Ko te whāinga kia wātea ki te katoa ngā mōhiotanga kai mārama, e mārama ana hoki.
