Avèm creat de projèctes especifics per las cosmeticas, per l'alimentacion animala, e tanben per d'autres produches:[Fats de beutat dobèrts](https://world.openbeautyfacts.org/),[Fats de noiridura de mascotas dobèrts](https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org/)e[Fats de produchs dobèrts](https://world.openproductsfacts.org/) respectivament.
We are therefore happy to be able to import your products into the project that suits them.
