## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Dins gaireben totes los cases, lors condicions de servici enebisson explicitament tota utilizacion non personala e tota extraccion de totas las donadas o de partidas de las donadas.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Alara quand comencèrem lo projècte Open Food Facts e l'aplicacion mobila, en 2012, decidiguèrem de far exactament lo contrari: non solament permetèm l'utilizacion e la reutilizacion de nòstra basa de donadas, liurament e sens còst, a totes e per totes los usatges (inclusent los comercials), mas o encoratjam tanben!

Rendre las donadas disponiblas publicament (çò que se nomena _donadas dobèrtas_) permet a d'individús, d'associacions, d'entrepresas, de cercaires etc. de tot lo mond de pensar e de desvolopar d'aplicacions per las donadas que de segur auriam pas jamai pensat.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (veire las [Condicions de reutilizacion](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Tot lo monde (inclusent mas pas limitat als contributors e creators d'Open Food Facts) pòt aital redistribuir e/o tornar utilizar las donadas per bastir de sites web, de servicis, de logicials, d'aplicacions mobilas, o per escriure d'articles e d'estudis. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Per exemple, nòstra Plataforma pels Productors es totalament gratuita, e sèm d'autant mai contents d'aquò perque pensam que los poiriá ajudar a melhorar lors produches.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

L'informacion e las donadas son somesas pels contributors Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. per exemple se los faches nutricionals d'un produch son fòrça diferents dels produchs de la meteissa categoria, pòt èsser una error. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Se sètz un productor, mercés de [contactatz-nos](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (veire las [Condicions de contribucion](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Avèm desvolopat una plataforma gratuita per vos permetre d'importar aisidament de fòtos e de donadas sus vòstres produches, sens importar vòstra talha. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Contactatz-nos per discutir de cossí podriam procedir: [producers@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
