Inhloso yaletinkhomba kuniketa lwati loluvisiseka kalula kumtsengi.

Lelicembu le NOVA lisebenta ngekuguculwa kwemkhicito, iEco-Score ngemtselela wayo wemvelo kanye ne Nutri-Score ngetintfo tekudla, kwati kutsi uma umkhicito unemaphuzu lasondzelene na A, kulapho unelizinga lelikhulu.

Emaphuzu langu-3 awabhekani netintfo letifanako. Ngako-ke akukhonakali kuticatsanisa naletinye.
