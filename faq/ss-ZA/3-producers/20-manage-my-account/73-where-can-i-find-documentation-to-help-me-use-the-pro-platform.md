Kukhona tinsita letimbalwa letingakusita kutsi ukhone kusebentisa kahle ipulatifomu ye-Pro.

Uvumelekile kutsi:

- hlola umhlahlandlela wetfu wemsebentisi - sikumema kutsi ulandzele i-PDF kute kutsi tikhonjiso tichafateke, lokutawenta kutsi ukhone kuhamba ngendlela lelula kulombhalo.

- buka tifundvo tetfu temavidiyo

- yabelana ngemibono noma tiphakamiso ngalelifomu

- Hlela lucingo naManon, umphatsi wetfu wekubambisana, uma unemibuto leminyenti: lapha

- Xhumana natsi nge-imeyili: bakhiciti@openfoodfacts.org
