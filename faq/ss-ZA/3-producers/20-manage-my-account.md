## Ingabe ema-akhawunti lamanyenti e-Open Food Facts angahlanganiswa yini nenkampani yinye?

Yebo, sicela utfumele i-imeyili ku [bakhiciti@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:support@openfoodfacts.org), kute sikhone kuhlanganisa onkhe ema-akhawunti nenkampani yinye.

---

## Indlela yekugucula i-akhawunti yami ye-Open Food Facts ibe yi-akhawunti ye-Pro?

Ungafinyelela [ipulatifomu yePro](https://world.pro.openfoodfacts.org/) ngeticu te-akhawunti yakho lekhona.

Nangabe unguchwepheshe, kutawudzingeka kutsi usebentise likheli lakho le-imeyili lebungcweti kute sikhone kucinisekisa i-akhawunti yakho.

## Ngingayitfolaphi imibhalo lengangisita kutsi ngisebentise iplatifomu ye-Pro?

Kukhona tinsita letimbalwa letingakusita kutsi ukhone kusebentisa kahle ipulatifomu ye-Pro.

Uvumelekile kutsi:

- hlola [umhlahlandlela wetfu wemsebentisi](https://blog.openfoodfacts.org/en/EN-Pro-Plateform-User-Guide.pdf) - sikumema kutsi ulandzele i-PDF kute kutsi tikhonjiso tichafateke, lokutawenta kutsi kube lula kuhamba ngalombhalo.

- buka [tifundvo tetfu temavidiyo](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjAH-USadsF3xgSVfUUBS4we3XBr-1r55)

- yabelana ngemibono noma tiphakamiso ngekusebentisa [lelifomu](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1QSZ_v50Ueoe8JPCqivvcyEdv8AnsuUbrbKQQofSAMFc/edit)

- Hlela lucingo naManon, umphatsi wetfu wekubambisana, uma unemibuto leminyenti: [lapha](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/appointments/schedules/AcZssZ3b8P2k9anVmeJzYiZ8lN0-lIrZiqfJecw2zB9zl82pzX9oGtt2XAMPnOfGwMj6bVa7dCunhktp)
