Njengemkhiciti, indlela lephumelelako kakhulu yekwengeta idatha yekupakisha kumikhicito yakho kugcwalisa kuleli spreadsheet le-excel lwati loludzingekako, kusuka kukholomu "AU".

Kuyo yonkhe incenye yekupakisha, kutawudzingeka kutsi ufake:

- Inani lemayunithi (ex: 4 emabhodwe e-yogurt)

- Lesimo (ex: imbita, sivalo, libhokisi, njll)

- Tintfo (ex: PET, liphepha, ingilazi, njll)

- Umyalo wekuphindza usebentise (sibonelo: kuphindze usebentise, kulahla, njll)

- Sisindvo seyunithi yinye lengenalutfo (nge gr)

- Inani leliphelele lemkhicito locuketfwe yiyunithi ngayinye

Leli spreadsheet lingabese lingeniswa enkhundleni yetfu letinikele kubongcweti.
