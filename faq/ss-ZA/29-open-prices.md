## Ngingumtsengisi futsi ngifuna kunikela ngemanani. Ngingakwenta njani loko?

Ungafaka sandla ngemanani ngekusebentisa [i-API yetfu](https://prices.openfoodfacts.org/api/docs). Uma ufuna kunikela ngemanani ngesikali, sicela utsintse tsine ku [tsintsana@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org).

---

## Uyacabanga yini kutsi uklwebhe emanani kumawebhusayithi ebatsengisi?

Ngetizatfu temtsetfo netebuchwepheshe, asicabangi kutsi kukhipha emanani kumawebhusayithi ebatsengisi njengendlela levumelekile yekunikela ku-Open Prices. Sifuna kwenta siciniseko kutsi emanani lesiwabutsela ndzawonye anembile futsi asesikhatsini, futsi kutfola emanani lakhuhlwe kulabo labafaka sandla akusivumeli kutsi sente loko.

Price scraping is a considered option in a future version of Open Prices, but it would be done by Open Prices itself so that we can have a proof of the price based on the HTML page.

---

## Kungani i-Open Food Facts yenta loku (Emanani Lavulekile)?

Lwati lwemanani lubaluleke kakhulu kuvisisa tinhlelo tekudla. Kuyintfo lebalulekile ekuvisisekeni kwetindleko tekudla kanye nekukhutsata kudla lokunemphilo. Kuvula idatha yentsengo kuyindlela yekwenta kube lula kubacwaningi, tintsatseli, kanye netakhamuti kutsi babe nekuvisisa lokuncono kwekutsi intsengo yekudla yehluka njani ngekwendzawo nangesikhatsi.
