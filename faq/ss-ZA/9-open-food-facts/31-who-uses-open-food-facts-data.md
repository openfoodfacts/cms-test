Bekutawutsatsa
sikhatsi lesidze kakhulu kubala tonkhe tinhlangano letitsembele enkhundleni yeMaciniso Ekudla Lavulekile
.

- Ticelo letingetulu kwa-100 temaselula
  tilungisa tidzingo tebalaleli labanyenti: sifo sashukela (VeryDiab), kugula, kudla
  ibhalansi ngekusebentisa buhlakani bekufakelwa (FoodVisor), kukhubateka, kanye nalokunye. I-database yetfu
  nayo yavumela kudalwa kweYuka.
- Emawebhusayithi lamanyenti: Hlanganiswa Kwekuphumelela.fr,
  I-CestEmballe.fr...
- Tintsatseli temininingwane : Tincwadzi tekuchaza emakhodi emhlaba, BFM
  TV
- Bacwaningi etihlokweni leticishe tibe ngu-100 tesayensi
- Emabhange ekudla kanye netitolo tekuhlangana: iphrojekthi ye
  Data For Good ekuchubekeni kwephrojekthi yekuhlola lecalwe yi
  French Red Cross, kuhlanganiswa ekuphatfweni kwemali yekudla lokuncane kwe
  kubambisana.
- Tinkampani: Terraillon usebentisa idatha yetfu
  kuniketa kuphila etikalini tayo letihlangene.
- Tikolo tekuhlela (Simplon, Emakilasi Lavulekile
  ) tihlanganise Emaciniso Ekudla Lavulekile njengephrojekthi lekhutsatako ekharikhulamu yabo
  .
