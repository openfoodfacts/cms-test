Ungabuta imibuto noma wente tiphakamiso ku[inkundla](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)noma usitsintse nge-imeyili:[tsintsana@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Nangabe ungumkhiciti, sicela usibhalele i-imeyili ku producers@openfoodfacts.org.

Nangabe uyintatheli, sicela usibhalele i-imeyili ku-press@openfoodfacts.org.

Sicela wati: Emaciniso Ekudla Lavulekile yi-database yekubambisana yemikhicito yekudla, asitsengisi noma ngumuphi umkhicito futsi asinato tikhonjiso nebakhiciti babo. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
