Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (buka Imibandzela yekuphindze uyisebentise)

Wonkhe umuntfu (kufaka ekhatsi kepha hhayi kuphela labafaka sandla e-Open Food Facts kanye nebadali) ngaleyo ndlela angaphindze asabalalise kanye/noma asebentise kabusha idatha kwakha emawebhusayithi, tinsita, isoftware, tinhlelo tekusebenta temaselula, noma kubhala tihloko netifundvo. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Sibonelo saloko kutsi, Ipulatifomu yetfu Yebakhiciti imahhala ngalokuphelele, futsi siyajabula kakhulu ngaloko ngobe sicabanga kutsi kungabasita kutsi batfutfukise imikhicito yabo.
