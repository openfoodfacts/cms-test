Baphatsi kuphela labangasusa titfombe, kugwema kucekela phansi lokungenteka.

Vele ubute ku Slack noma ku contact@openfoodfacts.org kutsi ususe titfombe takho letiphindvwe kabili noma ngutiphi titfombe letingakafaneli (kufanele uzame kuniketa inombolo yebhakhodi noma i-URL kuloko).

Siphindze futsi sibe ne-API lensha yembiko wesitfombe uma ungumhleli.
