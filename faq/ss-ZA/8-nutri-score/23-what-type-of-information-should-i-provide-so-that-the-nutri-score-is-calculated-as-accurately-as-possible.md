Kubalwa kwe Nutri-Score kutawentiwa ngelitafula lekudla kwemkhicito wakho.

Ungakhohlwa kufaka liphesenti letitselo, tibhidvo kanye nemantongomane etafuleni lakho, kwentela kutsi iNutri-Score lebaliwe ingabi yintfo lengakalungi.
