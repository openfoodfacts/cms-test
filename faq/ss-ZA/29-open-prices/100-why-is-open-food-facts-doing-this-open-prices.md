Lwati lwemanani lubaluleke kakhulu kuvisisa tinhlelo tekudla. Kuyintfo lebalulekile ekuvisisekeni kwetindleko tekudla kanye nekukhutsata kudla lokunemphilo. Kuvula idatha yentsengo kuyindlela yekwenta kube lula kubacwaningi, tintsatseli, kanye netakhamuti kutsi babe nekuvisisa lokuncono kwekutsi intsengo yekudla yehluka njani ngekwendzawo nangesikhatsi.
