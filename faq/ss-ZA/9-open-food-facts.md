## What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view food products information?

The main difference is for us a critical one: our data is freely available to all and for all uses. It's what is called open data.

Almost all other sites, services and applications forbid others from reproducting and reusing their data. Quite to the contrary, they jealously keep it for themselves. Cishe kuto tonkhe timo, imibandzela yabo yekusebenta ivimbela ngalokucacile nobe ngukuphi kusetjentiswa lokungesiko kwemuntfu kanye nanoma ngukuphi kukhishwa kwayo yonkhe idatha nobe tincenye tedatha.

We consider that food products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. Ngako-ke ngesikhatsi sicala iphrojekthi ye-Open Food Facts kanye ne-app yeselula, emuva nga-2012, sancuma kwenta lokuphambene ncamashi: asivumeli kuphela kusetjentiswa nekuphindze kusetjentiswe i-database yetfu, ngekukhululeka futsi ngaphandle kwekukhokha, kuwo wonkhe umuntfu kanye nako konkhe kusetjentiswa (kufaka ekhatsi kwekutsengisa), kepha siyaphindze sikukhutsate!

Kwenta kutsi idatha itfolakale emmangweni (loko lokwatiwa ngekutsi _datha levulekile_) kuvumela bantfu, tinhlangano, tinkampani, bacwaningi njll labavela emhlabeni wonkhe kutsi bacabange futsi batfutfukise tinhlelo tedatha lebesingeke sicabange ngayo ngalokucinisekile.

## What is Open Food Facts' economic model

Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (buka [Imibandzela yekuphindze usebentise](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#reuse))

Wonkhe umuntfu (kufaka ekhatsi kepha hhayi kuphela labafaka sandla e-Open Food Facts kanye nebadali) ngaleyo ndlela angaphindze asabalalise kanye/noma asebentise kabusha idatha kwakha emawebhusayithi, tinsita, isoftware, tinhlelo tekusebenta temaselula, noma kubhala tihloko netifundvo. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Sibonelo saloko kutsi, Ipulatifomu yetfu Yebakhiciti imahhala ngalokuphelele, futsi siyajabula kakhulu ngaloko ngobe sicabanga kutsi kungabasita kutsi batfutfukise imikhicito yabo.

## Can I add product pictures or data from the manufacturer's site, shopping sites or other sites?

Probably not. Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that is actually the reason why we have created Open Food Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

To avoid any legal problem, we therefore ask contributors to only add pictures that they took themselves, and only data that is coming from the product packaging and label.

## Is the information and data on products verified?

Lwati kanye nemininingwane kuhanjiswa ngulabafaka sandla ku-Open Food Facts. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and nutrition facts table. When in doubt, visitors can thus check the accuracy by themselves, and if there is an error, they can correct it on the spot.

To detect potential errors more easily, we will progressively add automated checks. sibonelo uma emaciniso ekudla kwemkhicito ehluke kakhulu kumikhicito yesigaba lesifanako, kungaba liphutsa. If the nutriments add up to more than 100%, it's definitely an error.

Finally, many producers send us photos and data about their products using our Platform for Producers. Uma ungumkhiciti, sicela [sitsintse](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)

## I am a food product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Yes! The only condition is to accept that the data and the pictures be made available under an open licence. (buka [Imigomo yekunikela](https://en.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use#contribution))

Sitfutfukise iplatifomu yamahhala yekukuvumela kutsi ungene kalula titfombe kanye nemininingwane mayelana nemikhicito yakho, kungakhatsaliseki kutsi ukhulu kangakanani. It will let you import complete and up-to-date data and visuals into Open Food Facts that will be visible in more than 100 other nutritional apps. Additionaly, the platform provides suggestions to improve the Nutri-Score of your products. Xhumana natsi kute sicoce ngekutsi singachubeka njani: [bakhiciti@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:producers@openfoodfacts.org)
