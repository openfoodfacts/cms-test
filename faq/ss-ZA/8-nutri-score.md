## Yini lokufanele ngiyente uma iNutri-Score yemikhicito yami ingakalungi?

Uma kwenteka inkinga nge-Nutri-Score yakho, hlola kutsi lwati lwakho lulungile yini kuthebhu letsi “gucula” yeliphepha lakho lemkhicito. Nangabe kukhona lwati lolulahlekile nobe lolungakalungi, ungangabati kusitsintsa ku\*\* producers@openfoodfacts.org\*\*

---

## Nguluphi luhlobe lwelwati lokufanele ngilunikete kute kutsi i-Nutri-Score ibalwe ngendlela lenembile?

Kubalwa kwe Nutri-Score kutawentiwa ngelitafula lekudla kwemkhicito wakho.

Ungakhohlwa kufaka liphesenti letitselo, tibhidvo kanye nemantongomane etafuleni lakho, kwentela kutsi iNutri-Score lebaliwe ingabi yintfo lengakalungi.

---

## I-Nutri-Score ivelaphi ?

Le-Nutri-Score ilawulwa yi-Santé Publique yaseFrance. Ifingqa litafula lekudla kute wonkhe umuntfu alicondze. Umgomo kuniketa kufinyelela kulwati lolucacile naloluvisisekako lwemsoco kuwo wonkhe umuntfu.
