You can ask questions or make suggestions on the[forum](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)or contact us by e-mail:[contact@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

If you are a producer, please email us at producers@openfoodfacts.org.

If you are a journalist, please email us at presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Please note: Open Food Facts is a collaborative database of food products, we do not sell any product and we do not have links to their producers. 如果對某產品有疑問或投訴，請直接透過產品標籤上的地址聯絡生產商。
