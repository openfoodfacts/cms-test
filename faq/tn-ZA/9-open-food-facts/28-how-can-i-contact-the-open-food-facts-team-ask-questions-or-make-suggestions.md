O ka botsa dipotso kgotsa wa dira ditshitshinyo mo[foramong](https://forum.openfoodfacts.org/)kgotsa wa ikgolaganya le rona ka imeile:[ikgolaganye le@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org)

Fa o le motlhagisi, tsweetswee re romelele imeile kwa go batlhagisi@openfoodfacts.org.

Fa o le mmegadikgang, tsweetswee re romelele imeile kwa presse@openfoodfacts.org.

Tsweetswee ela tlhoko: Open Food Facts ke polokelotshedimosetso ya tirisanommogo ya dikumo tsa dijo, ga re rekise setlhagiswa sepe e bile ga re na dikgolagano le batlhagisi ba tsona. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).
