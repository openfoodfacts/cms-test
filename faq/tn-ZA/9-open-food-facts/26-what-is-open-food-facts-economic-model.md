Contributors are volunteers. Their contributions are gathered in an open database that can be used by everyone and for all uses. (bona Melawana ya tiriso gape)

Mongwe le mongwe (go akaretsa mme e seng fela ba ba nang le seabe le batlhami ba Open Food Facts) ka jalo a ka anamisa gape le/kgotsa a dirisa tshedimosetso gape go aga diwebosaete, ditirelo, dirweboleta, ditiriso tsa selulafouno, kgotsa go kwala diathikele le dithutopatlisiso. They are free to make the resulting work freely available, or to sell or monetize it (e.g. with ads), as long as they respect the terms of reuse.

We are also strictly independent from the food industry, and all the services and software we build are free. Ka sekai, Polatefomo ya rona ya Batlhagisi ga e duelelwe gotlhelele, mme re itumelela seo le go feta ka gonne re akanya gore se ka nna sa ba thusa go tokafatsa dikumo tsa bone.
