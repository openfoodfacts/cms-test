## Ke morekisi mme ke batla go tsenya letsogo ka ditlhwatlhwa. Nka dira seo jang?

O ka ntsha ditlhwatlhwa ka go dirisa [API ya rona](https://prices.openfoodfacts.org/api/docs). Fa o batla go tsenya letsogo ka ditlhwatlhwa ka selekanyo, tsweetswee ikgolaganye le rona mo go [ikgolaganye le@openfoodfacts.org](mailto:contact@openfoodfacts.org).

---

## A o akanyetsa go ntsha ditlhwatlhwa mo diwebosaeteng tsa barekisi?

Ka mabaka a semolao le a setegeniki, ga re tseye go tlhotlhora ditlhwatlhwa mo diwebosaeteng tsa barekisi jaaka tsela e e siameng ya go nna le seabe mo Ditlhwatlhweng tse di Buletsweng. Re batla go netefatsa gore ditlhwatlhwa tse re di kokoanyang di nepagetse e bile di mo nakong, mme go amogela ditlhwatlhwa tse di gogilweng go tswa mo batseneleding ga go re letle go dira jalo.

Price scraping is a considered option in a future version of Open Prices, but it would be done by Open Prices itself so that we can have a proof of the price based on the HTML page.

---

## Ke eng Open Food Facts e dira se (Ditlhwatlhwa tse di Buletsweng)?

Tshedimosetso ya ditlhwatlhwa e botlhokwa thata go tlhaloganya dithulaganyo tsa dijo. Ke ntlha ya botlhokwa mo go tlhaloganyeng ditshenyegelo tsa dijo le go rotloetsa dijo tse di itekanetseng. Go bula tshedimosetso ya ditlhwatlhwa ke tsela ya go dira gore go nne bonolo gore babatlisisi, babegadikgang le baagi ba tlhaloganye botoka kafa ditlhwatlhwa tsa dijo di farologanang ka gone go ya ka mafelo le ka nako.
