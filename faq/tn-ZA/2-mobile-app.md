## Ke itse jang gore ke dirisa mofuta ofe wa app?

Eya go **Porofaele**,
morago ga moo **Dipotso tse di bodiwang thata**, morago ga moo **Ka tiriswa eno.**

O tshwanetse go
bona nomoro ya mofuta e e tshwanang le 3.4.5+662. O seka wa lebala go re bolelela make le model
ya founo fa e le gore e a tlhotlhomisa diphoso.

- bothata jwa neteweke

- bothata jwa ditetlelelo

- bothata jwa khoutu ya dibara
