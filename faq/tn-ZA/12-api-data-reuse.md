## Go tweng ka dijo tse di senang dikhoutu tsa dibara ?

Open Food Facts e na le tshedimosetso fela ka ga dijo tse di phuthetsweng. Bakeng sa boleng bo mahareng ba dihlahiswa (mohlala, ditamati kapa dipanana) le dihlahiswa tse ding tsa dijo, o ka sebedisa e nngwe ya dipolokelotshedimosetso tsa semmuso tsa naha tsa phepo boemong ba yona.

**Ela tlhoko:** Lenaane le le fa tlase le na le dingwe tsa dipolokelotshedimosetso tsa dijo tsa bosetšhaba tse di botlhokwa thata. Fa o akanya gore go tshwanetse ga akarediwa polokelotshedimosetso e nngwe mo lenaaneng, tsweetswee ikgolaganye le rona mo go: [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/contact)

## A nka batla leina le le tlhomameng la sedirisiwa ka API?

Ka maswabi ga go ise go kgonege go batla bonolo ka leina la setlhagiswa fela le ka nepo ka API.

Go dirisa setlhotlhi mo setlhopheng go ka go thusa go dira gore patlo ya gago e nne e e nepagetseng le fa go ntse jalo.

## Nka fitlhelela/kokoanya jang tshedimosetso ya diporojeke tsa me?

Mo tsebeng e kgolo ya Open Food Facts, kwa sekhutlong se se kwa godimo sa molema sa sekerini, go na le menyu o o tsamayang. Kwa tlase ga yone, o tla fitlhela kgetho ya “go batla ka tsela e e tseneletseng,” e o ka tobetsang mo go yone. Go mo go wena go tlhomamisa gore ke dikelo dife tse di maleba thata mo (di)porojekeng tsa gago. Fa o setse o tlhophilwe, o tla kgona go laisolola dipholo tse di bonweng ka go tsamaisetsa kwa tlase kwa tlase mo tsebeng le go tobetsa “Laisolola dipholo”.

O ka leba gape:

- Ditokomane tsa rona tsa API:[ https://openfoodfacts.github.io/api-documentation/](https:dintlha tsa dijo tse di bulegileng.
- Melawana ya Tiriso ya Dintlha tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng:[ ](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use)[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://lefatshe.dijo tse di bulegileng.org/melawana-ya-tiriso)
- Mo tshedimosetsong ya rona:[ https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data](https://dintlha tsa dijo tse di bulegileng.org/tshedimosetso)

## A go na le maemo a go dirisa API?

Ditokomane tsotlhe ka ga tiriso ya API di ka bonwa mo [tsebeng ya ditokomane tsa API](https://openfoodfacts.github.io/openfoodfacts-server/api/), mme tshoboko e e bonako ke eno:

- Polokelotshedimosetso ya Dintlha tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng e teng jaaka tshedimosetso e e bulegileng ka fa tlase ga Laesense ya Polokelotshedimosetso e e Buletsweng (ODbL), leba [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/melawana-ya-tiriso) go bona dintlha tsa semolao. Maemo a mabedi ke go tlhagisa le go abelana ka go tshwana. Fa o kopanya tshedimosetso go tswa mo Dintlhang tsa Dijo tse di Bulegileng le dipolokelotshedimosetso tse dingwe, gona ODbL e tlhoka gore polokelotshedimosetso e e tlholang e tshwanetse go gololwa jaaka tshedimosetso e e bulegileng le yona. Gape go raya gore o ka kopanya tshedimosetso fela le metswedi e e neng e tla letla kabo e e ntseng jalo.

- O tshwanetse go dirisa **ka metlha** Moemedi wa Modirisi yo o tlwaelegileng fa o dira dipitso tsa API go supa tiriswa eno ya gago.

- Ditekanyetso tsa seelo di gapelediwa mo ntlheng nngwe le nngwe ya bofelo ya API.

## Nka fitlhelela jang tshedimosetso ya hisitori?

Ga jaana, ga re neele thotobolo ya tshedimosetso ya hisitori (JSONL, MongoDB, CSV).

Le fa go ntse jalo, mo ditlhagisweng ka bongwe, go a kgonega go fitlhelela mefuta e e fetileng ya tshedimosetso ya sedirwa o dirisa API kgotsa mo tsebeng ya sedirwa o dirisa dipoeletso.

Nako le nako fa setlhagiswa se tlhabololwa, go tlhamiwa poeletso e ntšhwa (palo e e oketsegang e e simololang ka 1).

Ka sekai, go bona poeletso ya ntlha (=mofuta wa ntlha wa sedirwa) sa sedirisiwa seno, dirisa

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/product/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1](https/lefatshe.dintlha tsa dijo tse di bulegileng.org/setlhagiswa/7623186089763/joghurt-baumnuss-migros?rev=1).

Fela jalo, parameter ya rev e ka dirisiwa le API:

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/api/v2/product/7623186089763?rev=1](ditlhopho tsa dijo tse di bulegileng.org/api/v2/setlhagiswa/7623186089763?rev=1)
