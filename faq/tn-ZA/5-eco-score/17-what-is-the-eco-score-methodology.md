Eco-Score e ikaegile ka palelo ya tshekatsheko ya modikologo wa botshelo e e tlhagisitsweng ke ADEME (setheo sa phetogo ya ikholoji) le INRAE (setheo sa boset≈°haba sa dipatlisiso tsa agronomic). Dikarolo tse ka bang leshome le metso e mehlano di balwa le ho hokahanngwa ho theha dintlha tsa PEF.

Mo maduong ano a PEF go tsenngwa dibonase le diphoso (mokgwa wa tlhagiso, tshimologo ya

metswako, mefuta e e mo kotsing ya go nyelela, go phuthelwa) gore go kgonege go bala Eco-Score.

Go bona tshedimosetso e nngwe, leba tsebe ya tshoboko https://world.openfoodfacts.org/eco-score-ya-ditlhagiswa-tsa-dijo-mo-tikologong mo Dintlhang tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng mmogo le mo webosaeteng ya setlhopha sa Eco-Score. (Tlhagiso ya Maduo a Tikologo https://ditokomane.maduo-a-tikologo.com/)
